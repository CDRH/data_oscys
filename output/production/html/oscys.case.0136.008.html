Hannah Stewart
vs
Allison Naylor

No 77 Trials March Term 1842

Upon motion of the counsel for the Petitioner in the above case Hannah Stewart it is this sixth day of April 1842 ordered that the said Defendant Allison Naylor bring the said Petitioner into Court on Monday next the eleventh day of this present month April and that the said Petitioner have leave to attend to him laid quit during the term

By order of the Court
Test W Brent Clk

 

77.

Rule on Deft

Filed 11th Apl 1842.
