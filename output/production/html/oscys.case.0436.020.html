Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwealth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Mrs John Kagely, Reid Cross, and Bazel Williams to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of Law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of November court which commences on the second Monday in November next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Christiana Dunbar in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between said Christiana Dunbar, Plff: & Richd B. Alexander, Dft and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee, Clerk of the said Court at the court-house in Alexandria the 14th day of October 1847.

Cassius F. Lee. C.C.
