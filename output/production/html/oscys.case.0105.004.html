Anne Williams & her children
vs
Geo Miller

Robert B. Boyd makes oath in due form that he is credibly informed & verily believes that George Miller has early this morning removed away out of the City of Washington a boy about 12 years old the son of Anne Williams and one of the Petitioners in the above case

Sworn to in open court this 27 June -32.

W Brent Ck
