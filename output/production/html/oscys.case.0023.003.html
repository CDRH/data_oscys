District of Columbia....to wit,

The United States of America, To Elizabeth Wilson administratrix of Alexander Wilson

You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, on the fourth Monday of December next, to answer the bill of complaint petition for freedom of Esther Bradley in the said court exhibited against you.

Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.

Witness, William Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the 29 day of June 18011

Issued the tenth day of October 18011

Wm Brent, Cl
