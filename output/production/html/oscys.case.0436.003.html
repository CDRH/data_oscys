I am willing that Judgement be entered in the case of Christy Dunbar vs. Alexander according to terms, Viz: that she be free at the age of thirty years of age, as far as my right or interest is concerned, but not thereby admitting her right to freedom.

Each party paying their own costs.

R. B. Alexander
June 12th 1851.

To be filed in the records or papers of the suit.
R. B. Alexander
June 12th 1851.
