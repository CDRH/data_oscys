District of Columbia to wit

The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greetings

You are hereby commanded to summon Anne Tilley that all excuses and delays set aside, she be and appear before the Court here on the fourth Monday of December next to answer the petition of Susan Bordley, and her children, Rachel Bordley, Charlotte Bordley, Philis Bordley & James Bordley for their freedom in this Court exhibited against her. Hereof fail not at your peril - and have you then and there this Writ. Witness the honble Wm Cranch Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court the 30th day of June in the year 1809.

Wm Brent Clk

Issued the 10th day of July 1809.

 

20. 211. 240. 191.

Susan Bordley & her children
vs.
Anne Tilley

Subpa

Petn for freedom.

Sumd the 15 July 1809
W. Boyd
Marshal
