No. 33

Wm H. Williams Plff. in Er.
v
James Ash

In error to the Circuit Court of the United States for the district of Columbia holden in & for the County of Washington.

This cause came on to be heard on the Transcript of the record from the Circuit Court of the United States for the District of Columbia holden in & for the County of Washington & was argued by counsel. On consideration whereof, It is now here ordered & adjudged by this Court that the Judgement of the said Circuit Court in this cause be & the same is hereby affirmed with costs

Pr. Mr. Ch. Jus. Taney
