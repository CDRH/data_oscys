Novr. 17th 1794
This day Joseph Slater came before me William Haynes a Justice for Greenbrier County and made Oath that the Within is a True list of the Negroes Brot By him from the State of Maryland into Virginia Greenbrier County
Will Haynes

February Greenbrier Court 1795.
This list of slaves of Joseph Slater Gent. proved before Wm Haynes Gent & Lately Brought in his Family from the State of Maryland was presented in Court & Ordered to be Recorded.

Test
John Stuart Cy C
