District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, That you summon Thomas Tucker S, Edward Adams & Austin Adams to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the Monday Wenesday next day of Court, which commences on the Monday to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Jacobs & Williams in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined between Negro Delilah & als. v. said Jacobs & Williams and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this writ.

Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 3d day of November in the year of our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and thirty two

E J Lee CC
