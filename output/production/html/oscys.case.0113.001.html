To the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia.

The Petition of Charles Cross shews to your honors that he is a free man held unjustly in bondage by Gabriel Moore Esq from whom he prays to be released by your honors and as in duty bound will ever pray &c

James Hoban for Petitioner

April 5, 1836

 

189. 197. 173. 128. 93.

Charles Cross
vs
Gabriel Moore

Petition of freedom

Filed 5 April 1836
