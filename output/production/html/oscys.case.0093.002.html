District of Columbia to wit

The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greeting

You are hereby commanded to summon William Crawford, that all excuses set aside, he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer the Petition of William Ethington for his freedom, exhibited in the said Court against him, the said William Crawford. hereof fail not at your peril, & have you then and there this writ.

Witness the hon William Cranch, chief Justice of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, the 9th Day of Jany 1816.

Wm Brent Clk

Issued the 20th Day of May 1816.
