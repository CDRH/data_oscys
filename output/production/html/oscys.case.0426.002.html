To the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington

The Petition of Charles Taylor otherwise called Charles a negro man humbly complaining sheweth unto your honors that he is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Bernard H Buckner and Aris Buckner who claim your Petitioner as their slave

Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to the said Bernard H Buckner and Aris Buckner directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law

John J Dermott
atty for Petitioner

 

340

Filed 24 Sept 1833
