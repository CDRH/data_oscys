Prince Georges County, to wit,

Personally appeared before me the subscriber one of the Justices of the Peace of the County aforesaid, Samuel Hepburn Esqr also one of the Justices of the Peace for the same, and made oath on the Holy Evangels of Allmighty God, that Mrs Ann Cook, of the same County, who on the Seventeenth day of October 1792 deposed before this deponent, in a suit depending in the General Court between Charles Mahony plaintiff and John Ashton defendant was to the best of this deponents belief and Judgment of sound mind and perfect memory, at the time [strikethrough] when the deposition as aforesaid was taken.

Sworn this 27th June 1797.

Before Robt Bowie
