The United States of America
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Dennis Johnston, & Peter Davis and John D Harrison to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Criminal Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the Eleventh of June next court, which commences on the Monday in next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Julia Roberts in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between her & Adams & Harding and this shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Thomson F. Mason, Judge of the said Court, this 29" day of May 18341.

C. F. Lee CC
