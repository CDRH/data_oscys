Trials March T. 1849

No. 139. Mary Jane Wade vs. Wm C. Greenleaf

Petition for Freedom.

The Defendant moves the Court to grant a new trial in this cause; because
1. The verdict was against law.
2. The verdict was against evidence.
3. Evidence material to the justice of the case has been discovered since the trial.

Marbury & Fendall for Dft.
