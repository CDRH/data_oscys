Circuit Court, Alexandria County, District of Columbia,
May Term, 1834

You are hereby commanded to summon John L. Hampson & Alexander Moore that they be and appear before the Court here, immediately, as Witness for Harriet Lemon agt. Nehemiah Hicks

Issued 15th day of May 1834

By Order, Edm: J Lee C. C.

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.
