Petition for Freedom

The clerk will please issue subp & give the Petitioner a certificate of the pendency.

J. M. Carlisle
atty for Petr.

Petition

To the Honble. The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District for Washn Co.

The Petition of Samuel Stacy humbly represents

That he is a free man of color, having become legally and equitably entitled to his freedom, as he is prepared to shew.

That nevertheless one James Knox claims to hold him as a slave: Wherefore he prays subp[?] against the said James Knox, and that Judgment may be rendered establishing the freedom of your petitioner: & he will ever pray &

Carlisle for Petr.
