To the Sheriff of Anne Arundel County

You are hereby commanded to Summon Three good & lawful men de circumstantibus to make a Jury of Twelve men to try an issue now depending in the General Court between Charles Mahony against John Ashton returnable Immediately.

Jno Gwinn Cl

 

Lancelot Warfield Senr
Lewis Duvall
Abner Crain

Intersect.
