District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America, to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, That you summon Martha McClish, Johnathan C May and Edward A May to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria on the present day of this court which commencesd on the fourth Monday in this month to testify, and the truth to say, on behalf of Susan Luckett in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Susan Luckett & Elizabeth & Johnathan Walkom and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cts. And have then there this writ. Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court this 26th day of November in the year of our Lord 1824.

Edm: J. Lee CC

Henry Pittman, pr...Alexandria.
