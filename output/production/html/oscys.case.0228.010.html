Winney Cryer and Turner Cryer
v
Elizabeth Cocke and Peter E Hoffman

Petition for freedom

The Defendant Peter E. Hoffman in this case impleaded with Elizabeth Cocke in open court appears, and asks leave to file in the said cause his disclaimer; and he doth hereby deny that he holds or claims, or at the time of the filing of this Petition did hold or claim any title or right or interest in the said Petitioners or either of them.

PE Hoffman

 

262

disclaimer by P. E. Hoffman

Filed 21st May 49
