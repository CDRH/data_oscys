Greenfield, and the depositions taken in support of it, and the defendant may read the depositions in the [illegible] record taken on the part of the caveater[?] to invalidate the will in the same manner, and to [illegible] the same effect and none other as if the original paper purporting to be a will was ever produced and the witnesses produced against it [illegible] examined viva voce.
