Circuit Court, Term 184 .

You are hereby commanded to summon Hudson M. Garland that he be appear before the Court here, immediately, as Witness for on the fourth Monday of Novr next to answer the Petition for Freedom of Frederick Mortimer and so forth.

Issued 2 day of May 1842.

By Order, W. Brent. C

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.
