Mr Marbury,
Dr Sir,

I have seen and conversed with Mr Ryland, who have been good enough to give me the enclosed certificate which I send you. I am sorry I did not give you and Mr Cox your fee before I left the City Hall. I will be up in a few days.

respectfully yours
George Kirby

April 17th 1837.

 

John Marbury Esqr
Attorney at Law
City Hall
City Washington D.C.
