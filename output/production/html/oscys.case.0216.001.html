To the Honourable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington

The Petition of Charles Blue a person of colour humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honourable court that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun who claim your Petitioner as their slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the said Honourable Court to grant a subpoena to the said John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law

John J Dermott atty for Petitioner

 

44

Charles Blue
vs
John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun

The Clerk will file this Petition and issue as above
John J Dermott
atty for Petitioner
