District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Joseph M White Late of Washington County if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the First Monday day of December next, to testify on the part of answer the petition for freedom of Maria White in said court exhibited against him

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept

Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 26th day of August 1829.

W. Brent, Clk

Beale
