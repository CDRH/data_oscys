District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Peggy Barnes if she shall be found in the County of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the first fourth Monday of March next, to answer the bill of complaint of the Petition for freedom of George Crowner & Daniel Over filed in Court here against her and others. for their freedom

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.

Witness the Hon. Wm Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 27th day of Jany 1843.

Wm Brent Clk

Key

Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.
