Petition for Freedom in the General Court.

Petitioners Interrogatories

1 Do you know the parties in this Suit, and how long have you known them?

2 Do you know the mother of the Petitioner and how long have you known her?

3 Do you know the Grand Mother of the Petitioner and how long have you known her?

4 Did you know Betty Mingo and do you know or have you heard and from whom and when, that Betty Mingo was Descended from a White Woman or a Free Woman. Relate your Knowledge fully & Freely.
