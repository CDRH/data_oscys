The State of Maryland Sst To the Sheriff of Saint Marys County.

Greeting we command you that you Summon William Thomas late of Saint Mary's County that all Excuses and delays Set aside he be and appear before the Judges of our General Court To be held at the City of Annapolis on the second Tuesday of May next to answer the Petition of Augustus Butler prefered against him for Freedom Hereof he is not to fail and fail not at your Peril and have you then and there this Writ Witness the Honourable Thomas Johnson Esquire Chief Judge of our said Court the 24th day of November Anno Domini 1890

Issued 7th day of December 1790.

Jno Gwinn Clk

(G. Duvall)
