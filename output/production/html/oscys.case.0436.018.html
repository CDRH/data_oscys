Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwaelth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Reid Cross and Bazil Williams to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of Law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of next court which commences on the second Monday in June inst next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Christiana Dunbar in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between said Christiana, & Richd B Alexander and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee Clerk of the said Court at the court-house in Alexandria the 5th day of June 1847.

Cassius F. Lee CC
