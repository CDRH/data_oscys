To the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.

The petition of Margaret Tyler a free woman of colour of said City and District respectfully represents that she is claimed as the slave by Wm Duvall of said City and District surviving executor of John Smith deceased late of said City and District and is apprehensive that she may be removed beyond the limmits[?] of said District and the jurisdiction of your Hon. court. She would pray that the said Wm. Duvall may be summoned to answer this petition and that your petitioner may have her right of freedom decided by the judgement of your Hon. court and such other and further releif as her case may require.

Carrington & Lloyd
Attornies for petitioner

 

377. 229 212 199 178 169 133

Margaret Tyler
vs
Robert Duvall

Petition for freedom.

The clerk will file this petition & issue.
Carrington & Lloyd
Attornies for Petitioners

Filed 1st July 1858
