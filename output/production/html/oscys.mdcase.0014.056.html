The State of Maryland Sst To the Sheriff of Montgomery County

Greeting we Command you that you summon Joseph Sims late of Montgomery County that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Judges of our General Court to be held at the City of Annapolis on the second Tuesday of May next to Answer the Petition of Ignatius Butler prefered against him for Freedom hereof he is not to fail at and fail not at your peril and have you then and there this writ Witness the Honourable Thomas Johnson Esquire Chief Judge of our said Court the 24th day of November 1790.

Issued the 7th day of December 1790.

Jno Gwinn Clk

G Duvall.

 

No 58

Ignatius Butler
a
Joseph Sims

Sums

Montgomery
