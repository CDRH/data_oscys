District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you take Summon Alexander Tolson late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his bod before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3rd Monday of Octo. next, to answer unto The petition for freedom of Harriet Young, Wm. F. Young, Marg't Young & Sarah J. Young

Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 11th day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 59

Issued the 13th day of June 1859

Jno A Smith Clk

Hall
