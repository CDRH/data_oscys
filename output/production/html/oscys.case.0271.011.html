District of Columbia, To wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon John P Lambert if He shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the first Monday of July next, to testify on the part of to appear and defend himself against the petition of freedom of Nace Humphreys filed in Court here agst. him

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.

Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 20th day of July 1826.

W. Brent Clk

(K. & D.)
