My dear sir

Enclosed is the letters of administration granted to Mr. Meade on Wallingsford's estate. Please to file it in the case I asked you to suggest Defend appellants death and obtain leave for his representatives to appear &c.

yrs truly
Wm Brent

16 Jany 1836

 

Wm T. Carroll Esqr
Clerk Sup. Court

Wm L. Brent
1836, 16th Jany
