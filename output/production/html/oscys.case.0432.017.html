To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Dennis Johnson, James S. Scott, Tench Ringgold, Wm Ball, Hannah Elliot, Peter Davis, Wm Lightfoot & Wm Adams. to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Criminal Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the first 23 day of next court, which commences on the first Monday in May next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Julia Roberts in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between said Roberts & Adams & Harding and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Thomson F. Mason Wm Cranch chief Judge of the said Court, this 25 day of April 18342

Cassius F. Lee cc
