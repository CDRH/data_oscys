To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the Dist. of Cola. for the County of Washington

The Petition of Milley humbly sheweth That she is entitled to her freedom & is unjustly held and claimed as a Slave be a certain Samuel Thomas Massey of this County & District. Wherefore she prays the United States writ of subpoena to the said Samuel Thomas Massey directed, that he may be compelled to be and appear before your Honors & answer hereunto. So that your Petitioner may be discharged & obtain such other & further relief as to your Honors shall seem right.

Key & Dunlop for Petrs:

 

582

Negro Milley
vs
Samuel Thomas Massey

Petn.

Mr Brent
File this & issue thereon a subpa as prayed & give Petr a certificate for her protection.
Yrs
K. & D.

Filed 18 March 1823
