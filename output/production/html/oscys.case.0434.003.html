District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, that you summon Dr. Tho: R. Miller, Philip R. Fendall, William Duncan, George Stewart, William Hebb to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the second day of next Court, which commences on the first Monday in May next to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Cyrus Jackson & als: in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between them & Bernard & A. H. Buckner and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this writ

Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 27 day of April in the year of our Lord, 1835.

Edm: J Lee CC
