Francis S. Key Esq

Sir,

I was informed that the Davis Trial was to come in this Court & as you have been kind enough to do the Business for the Petition that was filed against me I hope you will enter a Disclaimer for me the boys name was Isaac & I sold him in the yr 1803 or 4 but think it was in the yr 1803 you may be sure I will pay you

I am sir with due respect
yrs J Travers

PS[?] Mr Morsell is kind enough to give any assistance that lays in his power with you

July 4. 1810

 

221.

Filed fourth July 1810.

Francis S. Key Esq
