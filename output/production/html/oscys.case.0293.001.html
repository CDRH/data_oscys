To the Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County

Rachel Ann Williams of the District & County aforesaid represents that she is held in slavery by Samuel Stettinius of said District & County, and that she, is entitled the said Rachel, is entitled to her freedom, and humbly prays your honors to inquire into, & do, or cause justice to be done in the premises; and your petitioner will ever pray &c.

M. Thompson
Atty for Petitioner

Washington Apl. 1st 1853

 

2.

Rachel Ann Williams
vs
Samuel Stettinius

Petn.

Filed 1st April 1853.
