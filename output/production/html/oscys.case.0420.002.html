To the Keeper of the Jail
For the County of Alexandria

Receive[?] unto your Jail & Custody a Dark mulatto Boy calling himself Arnold Jackson brought before me by Henry Talsapaugh Supposed to be a Runaway & him the said Arnold Jackson safely keep until Discharged by due Course of Law.

Given under my hand this 18th day of January 1837
Newton Keene
J Peace

 

Belongs to Arthur West

Andrew Jackson
Arnold
