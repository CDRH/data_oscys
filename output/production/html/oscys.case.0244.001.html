District of Columbia, to wit.
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Bennet Jarboe if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the second Monday day of April next, to testify on the part of answer the petition of Negro Isaac this day exhibited in our said court against him for freedom &c

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.

Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 23rd. day of October 1821

Wm. Brent. Clk

Wallach
