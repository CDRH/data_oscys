No. 15. Richard B. Alexander Plff. In Er. v. Moses Graham. In error to the Circuit Court of the United States for the District of Columbia holden in & for the County of Washington.

The Plff. in Error having filed an order in writing directing the clerk to dismiss this suit, It is thereupon now here considered [illegible] and adjudged by this Court that this Writ of Error be & the same is hereby dismissed with Costs.

Pr. Mr. Jus. Thompson[?]
11th Jany. 1843.
