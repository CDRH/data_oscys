On the motion of Negro Criss and her three children Henry Tom & George who are detained in slavery by Hugh Carolin of this County, she is allowed allowed to sue her said master in this Court for her freedom in forma pauperis, and Nathaniel S. Wise is assigned her Counsel to prosecute the said suit
