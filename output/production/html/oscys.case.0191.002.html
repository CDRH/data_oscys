Petition for Freedom Apps March Term 1845

I hereby authorize Rd. Wallach atty to enter his appearance for me in the above suit & confers judgment for the freedom of the Petitioner. she pay all costs.

Cushing Daniel

I acquiese in the above authority to confer Judgment for the Petitioner so far as I have claim. to her services.

Jno. H. Daniel
