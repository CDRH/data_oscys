Circuit Court, Alexandria County, District of Columbia,
May Term Term, 1834

You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Flinn & Benjamin Wood that they be and appear before the Court here, immediately, as Witness for Kitty Harriet Lemon agt. Nehemiah Hicks

Issued 7th day of May 1834

By Order, Edm: J Lee C. C.

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

To appear Saturday the 9th of the May.
Ed: J Lee
