Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwealth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Mrs John Kagely, Reid Cross, & Bazil Williams. to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of Law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of November court which commences on the 2nd Monday in November next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Christiana Dunbar in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between Christiana Dunbar, Plff. and Richd B. Alexander, Dft: and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee Clerk of the said Court at the Court House in Alexandria 18 day of October 1849.

C. F. Lee .C.C.

 

Dunbar.
v.
Alexander.

Spa.

Executed Upon Reid Cross & Bazil Williams.
Mrs Jno Kagely Not Found
