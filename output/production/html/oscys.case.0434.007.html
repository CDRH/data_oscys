District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, that you summon Zephaniah Buckley, Daniel Kembler, Robert Baily, John Baily, Reuben Hutchinson, John Hutchinson, Lloyd Lowe, Greenberry Lowe, Charles Lewis, John Palmer, William Rose, James Wigginton to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the second Tuesday day of next Court, which commences on the first Monday in May next to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Cyrus Jackson & others in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between them & Bernard & A. H. Buckner and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this write.

Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 17 6th day of April May in the year of our Lord, 1835.

Edm: J. Lee CC
