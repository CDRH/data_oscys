District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, That you summon Jane Eliza Glasscock s to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the to-morrow 10 o'clock a.m. day of Court, which commences on the Monday to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Thomas Morris in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined between and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this writ.

Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 12 day of November in the year of our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and thirty two.

E.J. Lee C.C.

 

Ex parte Thos. Morris

Spa. for E. Jane Glasscock s

Sumd
C. Scott, DM
