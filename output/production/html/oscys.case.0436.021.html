Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwealth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Nancy Casene, to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of Law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of November. court which commences on the second Monday in November next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Richd. B. Alexander, in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between wherein said Alexander is Deft & Christiana Dunbar is Plff and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee, Clerk of the said Court at the court-house in Alexandria 14th day of October 1847.

Cassius F. Lee C.C.
