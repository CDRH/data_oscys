District of Columbia to wit
The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon John Hepburn late of the County of Washington if he shall be found in the County of Washington that all excuses and delays set aside, he be and appear before the Court here immediately, to answer the Petition for freedom of Mina Queen and Louisa her child in this Court exhibited against him. Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this Writ. Witness the hon. Wm Cranch Chief Judge of our said Court, this 8th day of Jany 1810.

Wm Brent Clk

Issued the 8th day of Jany 1810.
