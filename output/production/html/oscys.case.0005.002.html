District of Columbia towit

The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting

You are hereby commanded to summon William Jenkins that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held in and for the County of Washington at the City of Washington on the fourth monday of December next to answer unto the Petition of Becky Joice on behalf of herself and children in the said court exhibited against him for their freedom. Hereof fail not to your peril and have you then and there this writ.

Witness William Cranch Esq chief Judge of the said court the 28th June 1808.

Issued this 21st July 1808.

Wm Brent Clk
