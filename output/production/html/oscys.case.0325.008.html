District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you take summon Alexander Tolson late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the first Monday of May next, to answer unto Harriet Young, Wm F. Young Margt. Young & Sarah J. Young's petition for freedom

Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 31st day of Jany. Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 59.

Issued the 1st day of Feby 1859.

Jno: A. Smith, clk

Hall.
