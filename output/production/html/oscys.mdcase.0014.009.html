The State of Maryland Sst To the Sheriff of Charles County Greeting

We Command you that you Summons Edward Hamilton late of Charles County that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Judges of our General Court to be held at the City of Annapolis on the Second Tuesday of May next to answer the Petition of Henrietta Butler prefered against them for freedom Hereof he is not to fail and fail not at your peril and have you then and there this Writ, Witness the hnourable Thomas Johnson Esquire Chief Judge of our said Court the 24th day of November Anno Domini 1790.

Issued the 7th day of December 1790.

Jno Gwinn Clk

G Duvall
