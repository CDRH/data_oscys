To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States for the District of Columbia and County of Washington

The Petition of Priscilla King a colo woman, humbly sheweth unto your Honors that she is entitled to Freedom & is unjustly held in bondage by William Wilkinson, who claims your petit petitioner as his Slave, wherefore your petition prays your Honors to grant a subpoena directed to the said William Wilkinson and such other and further relief in the premises as maybe in accordance with Law.

Ratcliffe & Kennedy
Attys for petitioner.

 

64. 100.

Pricilla King
vs.
Wm Wiklinson

The Clerk will file this petition & issue accordingly.
Ratcliffe & Kennedy
Attys for Petitioner

Filed 9th Jany 1854.
