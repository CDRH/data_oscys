Susan Vigel
vs
Henry Naylor Administrator

Petition for freedom

To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia

Your petitioner respectfully represents unto your Honors that she is free & is unjustly detained in slavery by said defendant and prays that defendant be summoned to appear before your Honorable Court to make answer hereto and upon the hearing give judgment against defendant & grant your petitioner such other & further relief as your petitioner may appear to be entitled to & your petitioner will ever pray &c

M. Blair for petitioner

 

446.

In Circuit Court
Dist Columbia
October Term 1858

Susan Vigel
vs
Henry Naylor Admr George Naylor

Petition for freedom

Filed 9t Augt. 1858.

M. Blair for petr.
