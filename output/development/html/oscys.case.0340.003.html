District of Columbia, To wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon William M Offutt & Zadock Offutt if they shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) they be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the first Monday of May next, to testify on the part of defend &c. at Su the petition of freedom of Milly Bowie

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there is precept.

Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 22d day of February 1826.

W. Brent Clk

(C. Cox)
