Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwealth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Mrs John Kagely (Washn) Reid Cross, and Bazil Williams, to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of June court which commences on the 2d Monday in June next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Christiana Dunbar, in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between said Dunbar Plff: agt. Rd B. Alexander Dft: and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee Clerk of the said Court at the Court House in Alexandria the 13th day of May 1848.

Cassius F. Lee, C.C.

 

Dunbar.
v.
Alexander.

Spa: for Plffs: Wtnesses.

Tn[?] Monday 12th June 1848.
