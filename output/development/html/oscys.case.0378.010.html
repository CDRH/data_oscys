Sally Clares mother Molly Clare is living about 3 or 4 miles from Wm Pattersons when B. D. Patterson married her she resided with her aunt mother & had not lived with Wm Patterson for 13 yrs. before. (Molly Clare Calvert)

Negroe China has for man about 4 years before B.D.P marriage with Sally Clare lived with Wm Patterson the son of W. P. the Witness. Jacob Patterson could probably prove this. he resides in Ann Arundel

B. D. Patterson was married in June 1806 brought his wife over in June and brot the plf over the 1st Jan following.
