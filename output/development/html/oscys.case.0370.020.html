District of Columbia—to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you summon William Stoops, James Gallatt, Joshua Doing, Robert Brockett, And Mrs Margaret Jewell, to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House, in the town of Alexandria, on the 23th day of theis Court, which commencesd on the Fourth Monday in November to testify, and the truth to say on behalf of Negro London in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro London pltff and Charles Scott and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of Dolls. 333 33 cts. And have then there this writ. Witness William Kilty, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 10th day of December 1805.

G. Deneale Cc
