District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, that you summon George Minor, William Minor & Smith Minor to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the seventh day of June Court, which commences on the Monday next to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Adams & Harding in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between them & Julia Roberts and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents.—And have then there this writ.

Witness William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 29' day of May in the year of our Lord 18344.

C. F. Lee CC
