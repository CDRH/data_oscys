To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington

The Petition of Cassius Jackson otherwise called Cassius a Coloured Man humbly complaining Sheweth unto your Honors that he is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Bernard H Buckner and Aris Buckner who claim your Petitioner as their slave

Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to the said Bernard H Buckner and Aris Buckner directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law

John J Dermott
Atty for Petitioner

 

345.

Cassius Jackson
vs
Bernard H Bucker & Aris Buckner

Petition for freedom

Filed 25th Septr 1833
