District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Milo Mason if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the First Monday of Decr next, to testify on the part of answer the Petition for Freedom of Louisa Johnson in said Court exhibited against him

Hereof fail not at your peril: and have you then and there this precept.

Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 20th day of Nov 1827

Wm Brent, clk

Beale
