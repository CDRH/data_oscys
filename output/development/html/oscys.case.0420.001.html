District of Columbia to wit
The United States' of America
to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting.

We command you, that the bodies of Rebecca Jackson & Arnold Jackson, or by whatever name they may be called, detained by you, or under your custody as it is said, together with the day, & cause of their being taken & detained, you have immediately before the Hon Judges of our Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at the Court house in the town of Alexandria, to do, receive, & submit to all & singular those things which shall then & there be considered of them in this behalf.

Witness the Hon: Wm Cranch, Chief Judge of our said Court this 3d day of October 1831.

Ed: J Lee CC

 

as Within directed I have the bodies of Rebecca Jackson and Arnold Jackson before the Court the men Committed to Jail by Newton Keene Esqr. the Commitments are herewith Attached which will be received as a Part of this return
D Minor DM
