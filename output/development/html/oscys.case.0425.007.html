Circuit Court, Alexandria County, District of Columbia,
Oct Term, 1835

You are hereby commanded to summon W. L. Hodgson that he be and appear before the Court here, immediately, as Witness for John Armfield ads Negro Rachael

Issued 27 day of Oct 1835

By Order, Edm: J Lee cc

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

 

John Armfield

Negro Rachel

Subpa

W. L. Hodgson

Sumd
D Minor DM
