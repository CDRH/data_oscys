To the Judges of the Circuit Court of the Dist. of Cola for the County of Washington

The Petition of Rebecca Rawlings showeth that she is now unjustly held in slavery by Washington Wallace and Jesse Dallerhite, and that she is entitled to her freedom: she prays a F Subpona may issue to said Parties &c.

W. Redin for Petr.

 

161

Rebecca Rawlings
vs
Wallace & Dallerhite

petn for freedom

Filed 13 Augt 1828.
