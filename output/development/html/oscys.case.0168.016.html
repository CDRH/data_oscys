District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you that you take sum. James Bowling & Clarissa Gardner admors of Richard B. Gardner before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer unto the petition for freedom of Margaret Atkins & Alfred Atkins by David Atkins their next friend

Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said County, at the City of Washington, the 17th day of Dec Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 50

Issued the 24th day of December 1850.

Jno A. Smith Clk

Bradley
