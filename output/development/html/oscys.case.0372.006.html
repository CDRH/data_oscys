District of Columbia towit

Negroe Fanny Smith plf
against
Joseph Thornton Deft

July Term 1808. 9th day

On Motion it is ordered that the deft. bring into Court Negroe Fanny Smith the plf. to attend the trial of this suit.

A Copy
G. Deneale CC.

 

Served the within order of Court on Joseph Thornton, the 13th day of July 1808
