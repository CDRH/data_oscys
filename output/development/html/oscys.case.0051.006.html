District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:

We command you that you take to summon John B. Lambert late of Washington County if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod to be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer unto the Petition for freedom of Esther Humphreys filed in said Court against him

Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have then and there this writ.

Witness William Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court at the City of Washington, the 23d day of Jany Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 33

Issued the 25th 7th day of Jany Febry 1833.

Wm Brent Clk

K & D

 

109.

Esther Humphreys.
vs.
John B. Lambert.
