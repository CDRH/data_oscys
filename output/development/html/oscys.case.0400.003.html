Jonathan Walcom & Elizabeth his Ann Alias Mary Ann Susanna

District of Columbia, to wit.

The United States of America, to the Marshal of the District of Columbia. Greeting:

We command you, That you take Jonothan Walcom and Elizabeth his Wife if they be found within the county of Alexandria, in your said district, and them safely keep, so that you have their bodies before the honorable the Judges of the United States' Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the county of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, at the next court to answer Ann (alias Maryann) Luckett of a plea of trespass Assault & Battery and False imprisonment. Damage five hundred Dollars

And have then there this writ. Witness, the honorable William Cranch, Chief Judge of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, this 11th day of June 1823.

Edm. J. Lee C. C.

Henry Pittman, print.
