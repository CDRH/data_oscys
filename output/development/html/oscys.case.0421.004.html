Civ Criminal Court, Alexandria County,
Octo Term 18341

You are hereby commanded to summon Richard Stanton, Thomas Slatford & Chas McKnight that they be and appear before the Court here, immediately as witness for Negro Nathaniel Holmes

Issued 12 day November 18341

By order

Cassius F Lee C.C

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

Nathaniel Holmes
Negro Committed as a Runaway

Richard Stanton sd
Thomas Statford sd
Charles McKnight sd

Sumd.
D Minor DM
