District of Columbia towit

The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting

You are hereby commanded to summon Sabritt Scott that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Circuit Court (of the District of Columbia, holding for the County of Washington at the City of Washington) here immediately to answer the petition of Negro "Ben" for his freedom this day filed against him. Hereof fail not at your peril and an have you then and there this writ Witness the honl. William Cranch Esq. Chief Judge of the said court the 8th October 1806 Issued 8th October 1806

Wm Brent Clk
