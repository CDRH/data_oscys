County of Alexandria, to wit.

Christiana Dunbar complains of Richard B. Alexander in custody &c, of a plea of trespass, for this, to wit: that the said defendant on the     day of       in the county aforesaid, with force and arms made an assault upon the said plaintiff, and her the said plff. then and there did beat & ill treat, and other injuries to be the said plaintiff then and there did, against the peace of the government of the U. States & to the damage of the plff. $100. & therefore she brings suit.

Chas. H. Lee p. q.

 

Dunbar

R. B. Alexander.
