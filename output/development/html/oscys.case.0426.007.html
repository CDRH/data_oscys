Circuit Court, Alexandria County, District of Columbia,
May Term, 1835

You are hereby commanded to summon William Duncan that he be and appear before the Court here, immediately on Tuesday next, as Witness for Charles Taylor v. B. H. & A. Buckner

Issued 5th day of May 1835

By Order,
Edm. J. Lee CC

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

 

Non Est
A Hunter
Marshal
