Alexandria

Mr Jacob Hoffman Mayor. Dear Sir

My woman has been to See wheather she Could obtain Certificate to prove her freedom the man was not at home. She Shows [illegible] instrument of writing From [illegible] Mr Lounds [illegible]   which satisfies me that the woman is Certainly free. Therefore if you will put Your Signature to her freedom I shall be perfectly Content for I believe from every Circumstance She is free Your signature will   be necessary to indemnify me

Yours &c.
Wm. F. Luckie

 

Mr Jacob Huffman
