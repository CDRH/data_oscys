Washington City DC Feb 20th 1849

John A. Smith Esq

Dear Sir

Please send me by the bearer Jno.[?] Woodyens's[?] will the execution of whic which was the question in the case of Harriet v Learned & which was in court yesterday on you desk.

Very respectfully
Yr obt servt
Christopher Grammer
