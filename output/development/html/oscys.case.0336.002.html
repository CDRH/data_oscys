Circuit Court, June Term, 1812

You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Quandell that all excuses and delays set aside, he be and appear before the court here immediately, to testify for answer the petition of Negro Fanny & her three children exhibited against him in our said Court for freedom

Issued the 17 day of June 1812

By order, Wm Brent Clk

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

 

District of Columbia
Washington County Sct.

June 17th 1812

I Washington Boyd Marshal of the District aforesaid do hereby authorise & depute Henry Ruth to execute the within process given under my hand the day and year above written.

Washington Boyd
Marshall D.C.

Sumd.
W. Boyd
Marshall

262 Tris.

Negro Fanny & Children
vs
Thomas Quandell
