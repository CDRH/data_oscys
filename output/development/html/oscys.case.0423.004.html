Prince William County Court: May 7" 1827.

"Administration on the Estate of Francis H. Dunnington decd. is granted to Elizabeth Dunnington, who came into Court and took the oath of an administratrix and entered into and acknowledged a bond with security according to law."

A Copy - Teste. J. Williams C.C.

Prince William County Court: August 6. 1832.

"Elizabeth Dunnington is appointed guardian of Chas. Dunnington. & Francis Dunnington (orphans of Frans. H. Dunnington decd.) who came into Court and entered into and acknowledged a bond with security according to law, which bond is admitted to record.

A Copy. Test. J. Williams C.C.
