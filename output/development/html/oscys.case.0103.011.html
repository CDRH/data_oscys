Amy Gray
v.
Henry Howison

On Petition for Freedom

And the said defendant for answer to the said petition says, that without prejudice to his right to the said Negro Amy as a Slave for life before the day of filing this plea he the defendant now Voluntarily disclaims all ownership right property and estate in and to the said negro henceforth.

H M Morfit[?] for deft
22 April 1835

 

No 640 Impls
