To the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia for the County of Washington.

The petition of Jane (negress) represents unto your honors that your petitioner is entitled to her freedom; and that she is held in bondage slavery by one Edmund E Richards of the said County, and prays that a subpoena may issue to said Richards, and his rights be enquired into by your honors.

S. S.[?] Williams for Petitioner

 

200. 190

(Negress) Jane
vs
Edmund E Richards

Petition for Freedom

filed 15th July 1847
