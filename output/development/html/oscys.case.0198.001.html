To the Honorable The Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington

The Petition of Martha Ann Cross a coloured person humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honorable Court that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Sarah E Linton who claim your Petitioner as her slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the said Honorable Court to grant a subpoena to the said Sarah E Linton directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law

John J Dermott atty for Petitioner

 

70 48. 29. 37. 98. 61.

Martha Ann Cross
vs
Sarah E Linton

The clerk will file this Petition and issue accordingly
John J Dermott
Atty for Petitioner

Filed 23d August "43
