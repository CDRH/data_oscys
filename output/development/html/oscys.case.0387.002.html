Negro Mima
vs
James Kincaid

On motion of the said Mima for leave to bring suit for her freedom, the Court doth appoint Edmnd J Lee as Counsel to enquire and report the facts on which she claims her freedom. And it is ordered that the Petitioner be permitted to examine before any Justice of the Peace in the District Columbia   any witnesses which she may require to be examined.

 

Negroe Mima
vs
James Kincaid

papers

1816. November Term 11th day
On motion of said Mima for leave to bring a suit for her freedom, the Court do appoint E. J. Lee as Counsel to enquire into and report the facts on which she claims her freedom and ordered that the petitioner be permitted to examine Witnesses before any Justice of the Peace in the District of Columbia
Caps. Issd.

E Lee
