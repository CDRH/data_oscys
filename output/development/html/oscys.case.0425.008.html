Circuit Court, Alexandria County, District of Columbia, October Term, 1835

You are hereby commanded to summon William Brent, Geo. W. Stewart and William Archer that they be and appear before Court here, immediately on Monday next 10'oclk, as Witnesses for Negro Rachael Brent vs John Armfield

Issued 24th day of October 1835

By order, Edm: J Lee cc

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.
