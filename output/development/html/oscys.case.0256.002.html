To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.

The Petition of Wm Killegrew, Humbly Sheweth,

That he is unlawfully detained in slavery by Daniel Carroll Esqr of Duddington, and prays that Process may issue to enable him to assert his right to freedom, And Your Petitioner as in duty bound with ever pray,

Hiort for the Petitioner
6th January 1809.

 

54

Wm Killegrew
vs
Daniel Carroll Esq. of Duddington

Petn for Freedom

Filed
6 January 1809

Hiort
