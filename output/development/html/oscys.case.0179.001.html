The Petition of Moses Graham humbly complaining, sheweth unto your Honors that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom, and is unjustly held in bondage by Richard B Alexander, who claims your Petitioner as his slave. Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena, to the said Richard B Alexander directed and such relief in the [torn page] according to law [torn page]

John J Dermott Atty for Petitioner

 

March 1840

Moses Graham
vs
Richd B. Alexander

Filed 1 June 1838.
