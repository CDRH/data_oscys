District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you take summon Henry Naylor late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod before the circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto the petition for freedom of Susan Vigel

Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 14t day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 58.

Issued the 9t day of Augt. 1858.

Jno: A. Smith Clk

Blair.
