This is to Certify that Josias Slater Color'd man serv'd Mr. John Hill in Alexa. five years and that during that whole period of time he Conducted himself in an honest, Sober, Steady, & Obedient manner throughout. and evinc'd a disposition of a good & faithful Servant throughout his five years Service in my family.

Mary Parker

Resident at time in the family of Mr Hill
Robert F Evans
