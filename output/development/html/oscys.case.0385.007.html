District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia,
Greeting:

We command you, That you summon Edward Burrage to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the town of Alexandria, on the day of the Court, which commence on the Monday immediately to testify, and the truth to say, on behalf of Negro Sam in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between the said Negro Sam vs James Green and this he shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33. And have then there this Writ. Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 23d day of April 1819

Edm: J. Lee C.C.

 

Negro Samuel
vs
James Green

Spa

Summon
Edward Burrage sd
