District of Columbia,
Washington County, sct.
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting:

You are hereby commanded to attach the body of Nobody and him immediately have before the Circuit Court for the District of Columbia, now sitting at the City of Washington, for the County of Washington, to answer unto the United States of America, touching a certain contempt by him committed in not attending this Court as a witness in the matter between A and B after being thereto legally summoned. Hereof fail not at your peril.

Witness the Honourable William Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 2nd day of June 1842.
