To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court.

The petition of Louisa Bell humbly sheweth that she is free and has been seized & is now held & is about to be carried out of this District as a slave by a certain     Lee, and she prays the process of this Court & that she may be deemed adjudged to be free.

Bradley & Bradley
for Petitioner
