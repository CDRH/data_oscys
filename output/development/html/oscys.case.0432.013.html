District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

We command you, that you summon Dennis Johnson, James S. Scott Tench Ringgold, Wm Ball, Hannah Elliot, Peter Davis, Wm Lightfoot & Wm Adams to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the Second day of next Court, which commences on the first Monday in October to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Julia Roberts in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between said Julia & Adams & Harding and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents.—And have then there this writ.

Witness William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 25th day of Sept in the year of our Lord 18344.

Cassius F Lee CC
