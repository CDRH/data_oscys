Circuit Court, Washington County Term, 184 .

You are hereby commanded to summon Francis Bowling & Clarissa B. Gardner adrs. of Richard B. Gardner that they be and appear before the Court here, on the 4th Monday of March next immediately, as Witness for to answer the Petition for freedom of Margaret Norah Atkins, & Alfred Atkins by David Atkins the father & next friend filed in said Court is then

Issued 16th day of Decr 1841.

By order, W. Brent Clk

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

 

37 March 1842

Marg't Norah Atkins & Alfred Atkins by David Atkins their father & next friend
vs
Francis Bowling & Clarissa B. Gardner adrs of Richard B. Gardner
