Saint Mary's County ss the 15th day of May [torn page] Thompson Executors of John Thompson late of Saint Mary's County, deceased, and made Oath on the Holy Evangels of Almighty God that the aforegoing is a true and perfect Inventory of all and singular the goods, Chattels and personal Estate of the Said deceased, that hath come to their hands or possession at the time of the making thereof, and that, what hath since or shall hereafter come to their hands or possession they will return in an additional Inventory that they know of no Concealement of any part or parcel of the deceaseds estate by any person whatsoever and that if they shall hereafter discover any Concealement or Suspect any [illegible] be, they will acquaint the Justices of process for the time being of the Orphan's Court in St Mary's County or the Regr Wills for Saint Mary's County, with such Concealemen or cause of Suspicion that it may be enquired into according to law.

Certified [illegible] Jeremiah Jordan Reg Wills for St Mary's County

In Testimony that the aforegoing is truly taken from record I have set my name this 15 June 1816

Jas Forrest Reg Wills
