District of Columbia—to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia Maryland Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you summon Thomas Butler, to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House, in the town of Alexandria, on the 2423th day of theis Court, which commencesd on the Fourth Monday in November to testify, and the truth to say on behalf of Negro London in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro London Pltff and Charles Scott, Deft and this he shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of Dolls. 333 33 cts. And have then there this writ. Witness William Kilty, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 10th day of December 1805.

G. Deneale Cc

 

Negro London,
vs
Charles Scott,

Subpoe

Thomas Butler
Sumd.
Danl. C Brent
Marshal
