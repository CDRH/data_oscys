Trunnell et al Petitioners
vs
Robinson & Keen

Circuit Court Dist. Columbia

The above named Henry B. Robertson one of the Defendants to the above petition for freedom comes here into Court in proper person this 24th day of April 1845 & disclaims & renounces all claim to hold the said petitioners in slavery, & as he has no interest in this cause he prays the Court to have his name stricken out as Defendant.

H B Robertson

 

126.
