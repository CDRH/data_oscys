To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County

The Petition of Nace Humphreys humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom & is unjustly held in bondage by a certain John B Lambert who claims your Petitioner as a slave.

Wherefore your Petitioner prays subpoena to said Defendant directed that he may appear & answer hereto & that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.

Key & Dunlop
for Petr:

 

27 104

Nace Humphreys
vs
John B Lambert

Mr. Brent
Issue sbpa as prayed, & give the Petr. a certificate for his protection.
yrs
FSK.

Filed 8 Novr. 1822
