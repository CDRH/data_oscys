District of Columbia,
Alexandria County, Sct.

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to attach the body of William Hebb and him immediately have before the Circuit Court for the District of Columbia, now sitting at Alexandria, for the county of Alexandria, to answer to the United States of America, touching a certain contempt by him committed in not attending this Court as a witness after being thereto legally summoned. Hereof fail not at your peril.

Witness the honorable William Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court, the 13 day of May 1835.

Edm: J Lee CC
