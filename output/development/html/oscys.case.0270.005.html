To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.

The Petition of Nace Humphreys junr: humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom & is unjustly held in bondage by a certain William Berry, who claims your Petitioner as a slave for life. Wherefore he prays your Honors that sbpa may issue, to the said William Berry directed, commanding him to appear & answer hereunto, that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.

Key & Dunlop
for Petr:

 

11 102

Nace Humphreys junr.
vs.
William Berry

Mr. Brent
File this & issue sbpa as prayed & give the Petr. a certificate for his protection.
yrs
K. & D.

Filed 6 Novr. 1822
