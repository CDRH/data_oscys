Circuit Court, Term, 18

You are hereby commanded to summon Fredk Golding that he be and appear before the Court here, immediately, as Witness for Judge tomorrow at 1 o'clock at his chambers as a witness for Wilson & Tooker vs Wm Fenwick

Issued 6 day of Novr 1835

By order, W Brent Ck

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.

 

Wm Fenwick
vs
Wilson & Tooker

Subpa

Sum for Petr
Fredk Golding

Ret. 1. O.clock tomorrow

Summoned
Alexr Hunter
Marshal
