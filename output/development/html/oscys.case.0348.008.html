I have this day bargained sold and delivered, and by these presents do bargain sell and deliver, to Henry Clay, for and in consideration of four hundred and fifty dollars, a negro female slave named Charlotte, aged about nineteen, which said slave I warrant and defend to said Clay against the claim of all and every person whatsoever; and I likewise warrant her to be sound.

Witness my hand and seal this 12th May, 1806

(signed) Jas. Condon (L.S.[?])

Teste.
Isaac Wells.

 

H. Clay
adv
Charlotte &c

Ans to Petition &c & Exhibit of a copy of Condon's bill of sale to Respondt.

Filed 25th Decr 1829
