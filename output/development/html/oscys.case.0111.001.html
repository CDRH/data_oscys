To the Hon Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.

The petition of Catharine Carter respectfully sheweth that she is free as appears & that a certain William Creecy claims her illegally & threatens to remove her to the south

Wherefore she prays that she may be adjudged free &c.

Catharine Carter by Brent & Brent

 

334.

Cath. Carter
vs
W. Creecy

The Clerk will file this petition
Brent & Brent

Filed 16th August 1841
