District of Columbia,
Alexandria County. to wit.

The United States of America
to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Mrs Margaret Riffell or Riffle, to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Criminal Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the 3d day of next court, which commences on the 1st Monday in May next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Rachel Graham in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between said Rachel Graham v Francis Swann and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Thomson F. Mason Wm Cranch Chief Judge of the said Court, this 30 day of April 18342

Cassius F Lee CC
