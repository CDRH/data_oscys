Virginia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The Commonwaelth of Virginia,
To the Sheriff of the County of Alexandria, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Nancy Casene to appear before the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Superior Court of Law and Chancery for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the first day of next court which commences on the first 2d Monday in June inst next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Richd B. Alexander in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between Christiana Dunbar & Rd B Alexander and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Cassius F. Lee Clerk of the said Court at the court-house in Alexandria 12' day of June 1847.

Cassius F. Lee CC

 

5

R. B. Alexander
at the suit of
Christiana Dunbar

Spa for deft

Nancy Casene sd
