District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

You are hereby commanded to summon Robert H. Perkins if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the first 4th Monday of March next, to answer the bill of complaint of Petition for freedom of William Lewis filed in Court here against him and others.

Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.

Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.

Issued the 20th day of Decr 1845.

W. Brent Ck

Rd Wallach

Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit on or before the day on which the above subpoena is retunable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.
