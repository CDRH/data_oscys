District of Columbia,
Alexandria County, To wit:

The United States of America
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.

You are hereby commanded to summon Mrs John Kagely (Washn) to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria on the 1st day of next court, which commences on the 1st Monday in October next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of Christiana Dunbar in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined between said Dunbar & Richd B. Alexander and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.

Witness the Hon. Wm Cranch Ch Judge of the said Court this 24' day of Sept 1846.

Cassius F. Lee, CC
