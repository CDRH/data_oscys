District of Columbia to wit

The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greeting

You are hereby commanded to summon John B Armistead if he shall be found in the County of Washington, so that he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington on the first Monday of June next to answer the Petition of Milford Foote in the said Court exhibited against him, for his freedom. Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ. Witness the Hon   the Hon Wm Cranch chief Judge of the said Court, the 20th Jany 1817.

Wm Brent Clk

Issued the 7th day of Mar 1817.
