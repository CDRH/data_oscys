District of Columbia, to wit:
The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:

We command you that you take summon Wm. Kinner Eliza Kinner & al late of Washington County, if shall be found within the county of Washington, in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have the bod before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of January next, to answer unto the petition for freedom of Charles Beale

Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.

Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 15th day of Novr. Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 60

Issued the 16th day of Novr. 1860.

Jno. A. Smith Clk
