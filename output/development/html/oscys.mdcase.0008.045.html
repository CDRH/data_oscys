It is admitted that William Diggs' Sue a mulatto woman David a Carpenter, Frank Crane Herbert, Jack Wood and Tom Crane all four mulatto men were the children of Joice who died at the Woodyard and who together with her children above named were in the property possession of Henry Darnall of Anne Arundel County.

That the above named Sue was the mother of Warrens Polly, Hills Nelly & Carrols Sue.

That Carrolls Sue was the mother of Nelly who was the mother of Charles and Patric Mahony the petitioners.

Jno Johnson for Petr
John T. Mason for Deft

 

Charles Mahoney

Ashton
