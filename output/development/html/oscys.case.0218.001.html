To the Honourable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington

The Petition of Fanny Blue a person of colour humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honourable Court that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly bend in bondage by John W Williams, Mary S Williams his wife and William S. Colquhoun who claim your Petitioner as their slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the said Honourable Court to grant a subpoena to the said John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law

John J Dermott atty for Petitioner

 

42

Fanny Blue
vs
John W Williams, Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun

The clerk will file this Petition & issue as above
John J Dermott
atty for Petr

Filed 3d June 1843
