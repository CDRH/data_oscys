To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court

The Petition of Louisa Harriet Louisa Bell shows that she is free, and has been seized & is now held and is about to be carried out of this District by a certain — Lee Green & others to her unknown & she prays the process of this Court that she may be adjudged free

Bradley & Bradley
for Petitner

 

94

Louisa Bell
v
— Green Lee

filed 23 May 1854
