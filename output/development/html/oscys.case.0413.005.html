District of Columbia, to wit:

The United States of America,
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting:

We command you, That you take Thomas Jacobs if he be found within the County of Alexandria, in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the honorable the Judge of the United States' Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court House in the town of Alexandria, on Monday the 29th day of this month at 10 oclk at the next court to answer Cleary Moore of a Plea of Trespass, assault & Battery & false imprisonment. Damage Five hundred dollars.

And have then and there this writ. Witness, the honorable William Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court, this Second day of October 1832.

Edm: J Lee C. C.

 

Cleary Moore
vs
Thomas Jacobs

Capias

Trespass assault & By & false imprisonment

Neale

Executed
C. Scott, DM
for H. Ashton marshal
