         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,</p>

         <p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Gerard T. Greenfield late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> he <del>have bod</del> be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of November next to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Ann Bell and her children Daniel and David filed in the said Court against him for their freedom</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 28th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38</p>

         <p>Issued the 21st day of July 1838</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>G. L. Giberson</p>
