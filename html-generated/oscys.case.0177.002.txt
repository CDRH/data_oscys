<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
    
<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon James Rhodes late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and safely">and safely</del> keep, so that <del class="you">you</del> he be &amp; appear <del class="have bod">have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer unto Moses Bell's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 27th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty one.</p>

<p>Issued the 18th day of March 1841</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p><del class="Bradley">Bradley</del> Wallach</p>
