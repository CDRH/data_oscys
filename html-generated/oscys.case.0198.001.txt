      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Martha 
            <del class="Ann">Ann</del> Cross a coloured person humbly complaining sheweth nto the said Honorable Court that
            your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Sarah
            E Linton who claim your Petitioner as her slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the
            said Honorable Court to grant a subpoena to the said Sarah E Linton directed and such
            relife in the premises as may be according to law
         </p>
         
         
         <p>John J Dermott atty for Petitioner</p>
