      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Sarah Ann Allen for herself and three 
            <del class="ch">ch</del> infant children named Eliza Jane Julia Maria &amp; John Davy humbly sheweth 
         </p>
         
         
         <p>That she &amp; her said children are entitled to their freedom &amp; are unjustly held &amp; claimed
            by a certain Joseph Wallingsford as his slaves.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore they pray to be discharged &amp; 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> that subpona may issue to the said Joseph to appear &amp; answer hereto.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FS Key for Petrs.</p>
