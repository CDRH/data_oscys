<p>To the honorable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia in and for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Sarah Ann Allen for herself and three <del>ch</del> infant children named Eliza Jane Julia Maria &amp; John Davy humbly sheweth </p>

<p>That she &amp; her said children are entitled to their freedom &amp; are unjustly held &amp; claimed by a certain Joseph Wallingsford as his slaves.</p>

<p>Wherefore they pray to be discharged &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> that subpona may issue to the said Joseph to appear &amp; answer hereto.</p>

<p>FS Key for Petrs.</p>
