      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">                

         <p>The petition of Hester Queen of Washington County humbly sheweth that your petitioner is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Nally of said County. Wherefore she prays your Honors to grant her relief in the premises &amp; as she apprehends that she may be removed out of the jurisdiction of this Honble Court before the next term, she prays a subpoena to issue to said Nevitt &amp; Nally retble immediately.</p>
                
         <p>FS Key for Petn</p>
