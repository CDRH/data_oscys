         <p>Sally Clares mother Molly Clare is living about 3 or 4 miles from Wm Pattersons when B. D. Patterson married her she resided with her <del>aunt</del> mother &amp; had not lived with Wm Patterson for 13 yrs. before. (Molly Clare Calvert)</p>

         <p>Negroe China has for <del>man</del> about 4 years before B.D.P marriage with Sally Clare lived with Wm Patterson the son of W. P. the Witness. Jacob Patterson could probably prove this. he resides in Ann Arundel</p>

         <p>B. D. Patterson was married in June 1806 brought his wife over in June and brot the plf over the 1st Jan following.</p>
