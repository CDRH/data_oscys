         <p>District of Columbia towit</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America. To Brown and George Miller Greeting.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Whereas Cordelia Wilson a black woman hath this day filed in this Circuit Court of
            the District of Columbia for the County of Washington her petition for freedom against
            the said Brown And for sundry good Causes particularly stated &amp; set forth in the said
            petition you the said Brown and you the said George Miller your agents and servants
            are hereby Commanded Injoined and prohibited from taking and carrying away the said
            Cordelia out of the County of Washington or removing her out of the jurisdiction of
            the said Court until the further Order of the Court in the premises.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof you are not to fail or you will answer the Contrary 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span>—
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Witness William Cranch Esq Chief Judge of the said Court.—</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 22 Dec. 1815.</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent</p>
         
         
         <p>FS Key</p>
         
         
      </div>
