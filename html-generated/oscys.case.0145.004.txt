<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Nelson Conrad late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and <del class="him safely keep,">him safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you">you</del> he be &amp; appear <del class="have his body">have his body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer unto Hiram Hunt's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness William Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 4th day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty two.</p>

<p>Issued the 6th day of August  1842.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
