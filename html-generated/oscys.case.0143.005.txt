         <p>To the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Hester Johnson, Martha Bronson and Randolph Bronson her infant son, respectfully represents unto your honor that your petitioners are entitled to their freedom; that they are unjustly held in bondage by one Pricilla Gant and Bazil E. + Gant and pray that a subpoena may issue to the said Pricilla Gant and Bazil E Gant and their rights enquired into by your Honor.</p>

         <p>D Ratcliffe<br/>
for petitioners</p>

         <p>+ E</p>
