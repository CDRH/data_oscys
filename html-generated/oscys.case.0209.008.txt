<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon William Addison late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and safely keep,">and safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you have bod">you have bod</del> he be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition of Susan Wilson by John Moore her next friend, filed in the said Court against him</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 22d day of January Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

<p>Issued the 3d day of February 1838</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Bradley.</p>
