<p>Mary Vigel<br></br>
vs<br></br>
Robert K. Nevitt</p>

<p>Petition for Freedom</p>
     
<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Your Petitioner respectfully represents unto your Honors that she is free &amp; is unjustly detained in Slavery by said defendant &amp; prays that said defendant may be summoned to appear before your Honorable Court &amp; make answer to the foregoing allegations &amp; upoln the hearing <del class="that">that</del> the Court may give judgement against defendant in favor of the petitioner &amp; she will ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>M Blair<br></br>
for petitioner</p>
