<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Sarah A Linton late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in you said District, and <del>h safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> she <del>have</del> appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to <del>answer unto</del> the Petition of Martha Cross for her freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 4th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty five.</p>

<p>Issued the 25th day of Jany 1845.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
