         <p>In the trial of this cause the Counsel for the Deft. having without objection
                    offered &amp; read to the Jury the whole deposition of Benjm Duvall (here insert
                    it) particularly that part of said deposition which contains the declaration of
                    the <del>
               <span class="unclear illegible">person[?]</span>
            </del> woman who is represented to be the mother
