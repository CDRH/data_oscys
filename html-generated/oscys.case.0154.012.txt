      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Elizabeth Whitney late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and him safely keep,</del> so that <del>you have his body</del> he be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition of John Patterson for his freedom filed in the said Court</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 5th day of August Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 39</p>
