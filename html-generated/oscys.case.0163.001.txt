<p>To the Honorable the Circuit of the District of Columbia in and for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Lidia Harris respectfully represents to your Honorable Court that <del class="he">he</del> he is unlawfully held in slavery by one William M Lewis of Virginia contrary to the will of Francis Montgomery of the same state, which will <del class="provide to">provide to</del> gives her her freedom on her arrival at the age of twenty eight. <del class="to">to</del> which age she has passed. <del class="The">The</del> She therefore respectfully prays <del class="your">your</del> the honorable Court aforesaid to deliver her from the above mentioned servitude.</p>

<p>Dandridge for petitioner</p>

<p>Washington June 29th 1836</p>
