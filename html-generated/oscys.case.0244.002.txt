<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States for the District of Columbia &amp; County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Negro Isaac respectfully sheweth that your petitioner is held in bondage &amp; claimed by Bennet Jarboe of said County as a slave, Tho' your petitioner is justly entitled to his freedom. Your petitioner therefore prays that your Honors will take his case into consideration &amp; that a subpoena may issue commanding the said Bennet Jarboe to appear &amp;c &amp; such further &amp; other process &amp; relief may be granted to your petitioner as to your Honors may seem meet &amp; his case requires. And as in duty bound he will ever pray &amp;c</p> 

<p>R Wallach<br/>
for petitioner</p>    
     
</div>
