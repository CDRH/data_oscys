         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of George Crowner &amp; David Over humbly sheweth that your Petitioners are
            free and were born of a free white woman and are unjustly held in bondage by a certain
            Peggy Barnes of Washington County.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore they pray that spba may be issued to said Barnes and that she may be compelled
            to appear and answer hereto, and that they may be discharged from their service and
            adjudged free according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F. S. Key for Petrs</p>
         
         
      </div>
