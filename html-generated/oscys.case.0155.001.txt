         <p>JanuTo the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of John R. Smith a coloured person, humbly sheweth unto your Honors that
            your petitioner is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Thomas
            Haynes who claim your petitioner as a slave; wherefore your petitioner prays your
            Honors to grant a subpoena directed to the said Thomas Haynes, and such other relief
            in the premises as may be according to Law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Morgan &amp; Ratcliffe
            <br></br>
            Attys. for petitioner
         </p>
