         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Sally Ann Robertson and of Nathaniel Robertson and Ellen Robertson,
            infants under 21 years of age by the said Sally Ann their mother &amp; next friend humbly
            represent, that your petitioners are now held in bondage and claimed as his slaves
            by one Alexander Waugh That your petitioners are entitled to their freedom and therefore
            pray your Honors to grant unto them the United States Writ of subpoena to be directed
            to the said War &amp; such other relief in the premises as your Honors may deem just &amp;
            proper &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Richard Wallach for petitioners</p>
