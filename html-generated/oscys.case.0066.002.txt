<p>District of Columbia, To wit:</p>

<p>The United States of America,</p>

<p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon
James R Bosley
if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the Third Monday of
December next, to <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the <del class="bill">bill</del> Petition of Freedom of Daniel Jones filed in Court here agst him—</p> 

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril ; and have you them and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness, W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 18th day of June 1825.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>(Beale)</p>
