         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia <del>as a Court of Equity</del> for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of negro Delia and Stacey, Edmund &amp; Madison her children infants, humbly sheweth that your Petitioners are entitled to their freedom &amp; are unjustly held in Bondage by a certain Thomas Offutt of the aforesaid County &amp; District who claims your Petitioners as <del>a</del> slaves, and your said Petitioners are ready to prove their said claim to freedom whenever your Honors may direct. Wherefore they pray your Honor to grant a subpoena to said Thomas Offutt that he may appear &amp; answer to the premisses &amp; that they may have fact other &amp; further relief as may be according to law.</p>

         <p>F S Key for Petrs.</p>

      </div>
