      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Andrew Jones, respectfully represents that his mother was free, and he was born free, and is entitled to  his freedom; and that he is held in bondage by a certain Joshua Pearce, contrary to law, and asks such relief from your Honors, as is legal and right.</p>

         <p>Daniel Ratcliffe for Petitioner</p>
