<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br> 
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Olivia Kean and Silas Burke Executors of James Kean Junr. late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del class="and safely keep,">and safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you">you</del> they be <del class="have">have</del> and appear <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition of George Kean in the said Court filed against them for his freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 28th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

<p>Issued the 21st day of June 1838</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Dermott</p>
