         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Olivia Kean and Silas Burke Executors of James Kean Junr. late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> they be <del>have</del> and appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of George Kean in the said Court filed against them for his freedom.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 28th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

         <p>Issued the 21st day of June 1838</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Dermott</p>
