         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington
            County—
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Cordelia Wilson a black woman humbly sheweth to your Honors that the
            said Cordelia is entitled to her freedom and is unlawfully held in bondage and slavery
            by a certain Brown now in Washington County aforesaid and kept in the house of a certain
            George Miller— Your Petitioner further states that she verily believes that the said
            Brown is about to take and carry her away by force from this District out of the jurisdiction
            of this Honorable Court. Wherefore she prays your Honors to grant her as well the
            United States writ of Subpoena &amp; also the United States writ of Injunction enjoining
            and prohibiting the said Brown &amp; George Miller their &amp; each other agents and servants
            from taking and conveying away your Petitioner out of this Country or removing her
            out of the jurisdiction of this Court untill the further order of your Honors in the
            premises.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FS Key for Petitioner</p>
         
         
         <p>On this 22nd day of December 1815 appeared before me Jesse Torrey Jr and made solemn
            affirmation that he believes the facts stated in the above petition to be true
         </p>
         
         
         <p>affirmed 
            <del class="Jnon or">Jnon or</del> before me
            <br></br>
            Danl Rapine
         </p>
