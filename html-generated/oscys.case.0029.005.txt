<p>The Petitioner's Counsel in this case <del>prays the Court to</del> gave in evidence the following depositions (here insert them) &amp; further proves that in the Spring 1812 a certain person unknown to the witness brought with him to the house of the witness in this City the Petitioner that after being there some time the Deft came there Χ the said person &amp; the Deft conversed together by themselves for some time (the witness does not know on what subject) that while they were conversing the Petr ran out of the house &amp; jumped over the fence— that the stranger, whose horse was in the witness's stable, took his horse &amp; rode away with on the same day or the day after &amp; the witness did not see him afterwards &amp; has subsequent then to frequently seen the said Petitioner <del>who has resided here and going</del> going about in the City.</p>
