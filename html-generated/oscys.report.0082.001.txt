         <p>A petitioner for freedom has not a right to go in search of his witnesses.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>Petition for freedom.</p>

         <p>Mr. Caldwell, for the petitioner, moved the Court for leave to the petitioner to go in search of evidence in support of his petition.</p>

         <p>Refused. The Court said they knew no law which authorized them to give such leave. The usual recognizance is to permit the petitioner to attend court from time to time.</p>
