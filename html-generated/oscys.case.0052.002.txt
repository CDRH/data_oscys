      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon
            Rachel Hoskins late of Washington County
            if she shall be found in the county of
            Washington,
            that (all excuses set aside) she be and appear before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the
            county of Washington, in
            the city of Washington, on the First Monday of
            October next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>
            answer the petition for freedom of Joseph Burke, filed in court here
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court. 28th Apl 1823</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 29 day of April 1823</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>(Coxe)</p>
