<p>District of Columbia Set.</p>

<p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greeting.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summons Joel T Gustine, if he shall be found in the County of Washington in the District of Columbia, that all excuses and delays set aside, he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of December next, to answer the Petition for freedom of Prince Gray in the said Court exhibited against him. Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then &amp; there this Writ. Witness the Hon Wm Cranch chief Judge of the said Court, the 7th day of July 1820.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Issued the 6th Day of Aug 1820.</p>

<p>Jones &amp; F.</p>
