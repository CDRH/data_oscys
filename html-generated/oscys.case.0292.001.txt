<p>To the Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>James Williams of the District &amp; County aforesaid represents that he is held in slavery by Peter Havener of said District &amp; County, and that he, the said Williams, is entitled to his freedom &amp; humbly prays your Honors to inquire into &amp; do, or cause justice to be done in the premises;</p>

<p>And your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.</p> 

<p>M. Thompson<br/>
Atty for Petitioner</p>

<p>Apl 1st 1853</p>
