<p>Washington County district of Columbia set</p>

<p>on the 14th day of October 1822</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Came Elizabeth Alexander Aged about forty five Before me the
subscriber a Justice of the peace in and for said County and made Oath on the
Holy Evangelist of Almighty God that she is a Daughter of
Thomas &amp; Sarah Triplett of
Virginia. <del class="That">That</del> That she has been married about
twenty six years, that she left her Mothers family at or soon after her marriage
that her father was dead some years Before that she Knew Negro Boy
Dennis and his mother Dina that they

<span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.005.002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.005.002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.005.002.jpg" class="display"></img></a></span>

were the Slaves of Thomas Triplett and that
Dina was a Slave born in Virginia
That after the death of Thomas Triplett the said Slaves
remained in the family as part of his Estate, that she does not know at what
time Negro Dennis came to the County of
Washington in the district of Col nor
does she know at what time <span class="illegible">[Mistress?]</span> Sarah Triplett and her
Daughter Margaret removed to the district
Col and that Negro Dennis lived some time
with her Brother and Mother in Alexandria / more then a
year she thinks where they had then taken a House and resided Previously to
Negro Dennis Being Brought to the County of
Washington in the District of Col that
she visited at her mothers frequently at Alexandria,
several times in a year.</p>

<span class="hr"> </span><span class="pageimage"><a href="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.005.003.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.005.003.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;"><img src="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.005.003.jpg" class="display"></img></a></span>

<p>To a question put by Petitioner she answers that she Remembers
Dennis's Mother and Sister in Alexandria
but she believes the Girl died Before she was long there</p>

<p>2d Question by the Petitioner do you or do you not Recollect
Dennis Liveing in Alexandria
Previously to his removal to the County of Washington</p>

<p>Answer Dennis lived with my Brother in
Alexandria</p>

<p>3d do you Recollect at what time</p>

<p>answer Before my Brother went to Algiers</p>

<p>The deponant further saith that when the family Broke  up House <del class="at&#xA;Alexandria">at
Alexandria</del> Keeping at
Alexandria her mother and Sister removed to
Geo Town and her Brother went to Sea that they Lived with
Mr Love a year or two in George Town
and that Dennis's mother went Back to
Virginia where she died; that she does not recollect what
went with Dennis.</p>

<p>The deponant further saith not</p>

<p>Danl Buford</p>

<p>Admitted <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> as testimony<br></br>
Augt Taney<br></br>
Thos Randall</p>

<p>Oct 14th 1822</p>
