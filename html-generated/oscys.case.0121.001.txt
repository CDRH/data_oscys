         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Elizabeth Herbert, and Caroline Herbert Humbly shews that they are entitled to their freedom, and they are informed that a certain Henry Miller claims to own them, and is now about to take possession of them and they pray that their right may be enquired into and that a subpoena may issued against said Henry Miller.</p>

         <p>Jos H Bradley for Petitioners</p>


         <p>Col Brent, Please file this petition &amp; give the bearer, a certificate for each of the petitioners<br/>
Jos H. B</p>
