         <p>We of the Jury find that previous to the year 1837 the petitioner was the slave of a certain Lawrence Hoff a resident of Alexandria County in the District of Columbia. That in the year 1837, the said Hoff then owning &amp; possessing the petitioner as his slave in the county of Alexandria aforesaid whereof he continued to be a resident, did sell &amp; deliver the petitioner to one Little then being <del>an inhabitant</del> a resident of <del>said</del> Washington County in the District aforesaid &amp; that the delivery <del>was in</del> of the petitioner was made to the said Little in Alexandria County aforesaid, &amp; the petitioner was immediately removed by said Little to Washington County aforesaid to reside &amp; also for sale whereof said Little was resident. That the said Little shortly towit about one year or a little more afterwards the petitioner to one Keeting in Washington County who sold &amp; delivered him to the defendant. That since said sale to said Little, the petitioner has always been
