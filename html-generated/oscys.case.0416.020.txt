      <div class="body">

         <p>Prince Georges County Md. 1st June 1824</p>

         <p>Sir.</p>

         <p>Inclosed you will find the summon for Soper and others not served. I started the next morning for Mr Sopers but my horse was <del>was</del> taken so lame that I was obliged to return, and supposing the trial would be decided to day I did not think it worth while to make any further attempt to go after the witnesses named in the precept</p>

         <p>My accout for taking the depositions is $36 which I hope they will be prepared to pay when I come <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> Washington again</p>

         <p>Yours &amp;c R. J. Morsell<br/>
Dept Marshal<br/>
P.G. County</p>

         <p>William R. Stewart Esq</p>
