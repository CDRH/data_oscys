         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Addison if he shall be found within the
            county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the
            city of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March Inst
