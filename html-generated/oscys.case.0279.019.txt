      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Judges of the Circuit Court in the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of Henry Compton respectfully represents to your Honors that he is a free man and is unlawfully and unjustly held in custody of William Dorsey, who claims your petitioner as his slave</p>

         <p>Wherefore he the said petitioner, prays your Honors, to issue or cause to be issued the Unites States writ of subpoena, directed to the said Dorsey commanding him to appear &amp; show cause why he detains your petitioner as his slave as aforesaid and that such other &amp; further relief may be granted as to your Honors may seem proper &amp;c </p>

         <p>Danl Ratcliffe<br/>
atty for Petitioner</p>
     
      </div>
