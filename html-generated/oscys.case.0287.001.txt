         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear
            before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of
            Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer 
            <del class="the bill of complaint of">the bill of complaint of</del> Adam Bailey's petition for freedom in the said court exhibited against you. Hereof
            you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.
         </p>
