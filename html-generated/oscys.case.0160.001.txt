<p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County,</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Humbly complaining sheweth unto your Honours, your Petitioner negro Kitty or Kate, that she is entitled to her freedom, that nevertheless she has been seized &amp; is now falsely imprisoned &amp; held in slavery, by a certain Samuel McPherson, or some one claiming through him, as your petitioner is informed, she has been sold to a foriegn negro trader, &amp; is about to be removed from the jurisdiction of your Honourable Court. That your petitioner claims her freedom under the last will &amp; testament of a certain Miss Mary Brooke, late of the County &amp; District aforesaid, a copy of which is herewith filed, as to your Honours will appear from reference thereto.</p>

<p>Your petitioner being without relief except from your Honourable court prays that her rights may be enquired into, &amp; that your Honours would forth with grant your most gracious writ of injunction directed to said Samuel McPherson &amp; all others claiming through or by him or under him &amp; all others whatsoever forbidding &amp; restraining them from holding your petitioner in bondage &amp; especially from removing your petitioner from the Jurisdiction of your honorable court, till her rights can be inquired into &amp; your petitioner prays ever process will afford her such other further relief as to your Honours may seem meet &amp; proper, &amp; thus she will ever pray respcly &amp;c</p>

<p>Thos Turner<br/>
Sol: and atty for Petitr</p>

</div>
