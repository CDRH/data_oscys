         <p>To the Honble The Judges of The Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the
            County of Washington.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>The humble Petition of Ann Shorter on behalf of her Daughter Sally.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Humbly Sheweth,</p>
         
         
         <p>That Your Petitioners Daughter is detained in Slavery, by a certain Thomas Corcoran;
            She therefore prays that the regular process may issue, so that Your Petitioner for
            her Child may be enabled to Assert her right to freedom.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>And Your Petitioner will ever pray.</p>
         
         
         <p>H: Hiort for the Petr</p>
         
         
         <p>28 June 1808.</p>
