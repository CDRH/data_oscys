         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Sarah E Linton if she shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            
            <del class="at rules">at rules</del> to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> fourth Monday of Novr next, to answer to bill 
            <del class="of complaint of">of complaint of</del> the petition of freedom of Sally Cross filed in Court here against her 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 23d day of August 1843.</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent Clk</p>
