            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> sum: John R Ratcliff late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County
            of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid,
            at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto Letty
            Lucas her Petition for freedom &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness William Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the fifth day of August Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and fifty,
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 28th day of August 1850.</p>
         
         
         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>
