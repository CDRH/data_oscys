         <p>District of Columbia towit</p>

         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
                
         <p>You are hereby commanded <del>th</del> to Summon Mary Ann Fenwick that all excuses and delays set aside she be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington to be held at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of December next to answer the petition of Catherine Killigrew in behalf of herself and Christopher Killigrew, Anny Killigrew, Louisa Killigrew and an infant unnamed, in the said court exhibited against her for their freedom</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril &amp; have you then and there this writ</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esq. Chief Judge of the said Court the 28th day of June 1808</p>
                
         <p>Issue the 18th day of July 1808</p>
                      
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Hiort</p>   
      
      </div>
