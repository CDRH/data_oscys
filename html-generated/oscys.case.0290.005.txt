         <p>On the 18th May 1816 before the subscriber an assistant Judge of the Circuit Court
            of The District of Columbia appears George G. Minor &amp; under oath in due term of law
            that one 
            <del class="E.">E.</del> Hale came to Queens Tavern on Saturday last stating if that he came from New York
            &amp; had bought 
            <del class="this">this</del> Negro Morris whom he brought with him to Queens on his way to Washington in New Jersey
            for $400. That the boy did not know of the purchase but thought he was hired, and
            that he (Hale) wished him still to remain in ignorance of the sale.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>before J.S. Morsell</p>
