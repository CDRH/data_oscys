<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>The Petition of negro Violet and her infant children Chloe &amp; Winny humbly sheweth; that your fair Petitioners are entitled to their freedom and are unjustly held in bondage &amp; claimed as slaves by a certain Henry W. Ball of Washington County aforesaid.</p>
                    
<p>Wherefore your Petitioners being preposed to invoke good their said claim to freedom pray that subpoena may be issued to the said Henry W. Ball directed commanding him to be &amp; appear before your Honors to answer hereunto &amp; such other proceedings &amp; relief in the premises as may be according to Law &amp; seem right to your Honors.</p>
                    
<p>FSKey for Petn</p>
