         <p>To the Hon: The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington
            County
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Winny Cryer for herself and as next friend to her infant son 
            <del class="Turner Cryer">Turner Cryer</del> humbly shews
         </p>
         
         
         <p>That they are unjustly claimed to be the slaves of one Elizabeth Cocke by her agent
            Peter E. Hoffman (of Alexa. Co. Va.)
         </p>
         
         
         <p>That they are entitled to their freedom.</p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore they pray yr Honors to grant unto them subpoenas against the said Elizabeth
            and the said Peter E. that the
            <del class="ir">ir</del> right of your petitioners to freedom may be enquired into &amp; secured &amp; they will pray
            &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>H. May for Petrs.</p>
         
         
         <p>19 Jany 1847.</p>
         
         
      </div>
