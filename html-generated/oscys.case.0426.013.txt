         <p>District of Columbia,<br/>
Alexandria County, to wit.</p>

         <p>The United States of America<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Henry Daingerfield &amp; John Armfield to appear before the Honorable the United States' Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on the second <del>day of court, which commences on the</del> Monday in June next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of B. H. &amp; A. Buckner in a certain matter of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between Charles Taylor &amp; als: &amp; them and they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon. William Cranch, Chief Judge of our said Court, this 22d day of May 1835.</p>

         <p>Edm: J. Lee CC</p>
