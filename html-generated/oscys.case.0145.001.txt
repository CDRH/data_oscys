         <p>To the Hon. Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for Washington County.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Yr Petitioner Hiram Hunt respectfully represnts that he is a man of color and entitled
            to his freedom and that he is illegally detained as a slave by N. Conrad of Virginia
            and William Gadsby and       Newton of the County of Washington in the District of
            COlumbia, to whom said Conrad hired yr Petitioner as a slave. Yr Petitioner therefore
            prays that the said N. Conrad, William Gadsby and       Newton, may be cited to appear
            as the Law directs and that yr Petitioner's case may be adjudged ad that his liberty
            be granted to him and that said Defendants may pay all costs &amp;c. And as in duty bound
            he will ever pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioner</p>
