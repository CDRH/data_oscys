<p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Emanuel Prince humbly sheweth that he is free man, &amp; that he is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Thos. N. Davis of Washington County. Wherefore he prays the U.S. Writ of Sbpa to said Davis directed, commanding him to appear before your Honors &amp; answer hereunto. That your Petitioner ay be adjudged free and discharged.</p>

<p>FSKey for Petr</p>
