      <div class="body">
     
         <p>Julia Roberts complains of Austin L. Adams and Ann E. Harding in custody &amp;c. of a plea of trespass &amp; false imprisonment. For this that whereas the said plff being at the county aforesaid on the     day of       1840, was with force &amp; arms and great violence assaulted and beaten by the said defendant and was then and there illegally imprisoned by the said defendant to the great injuring of the plaintiff and to the deprivation of her personal liberty, and to the damage of the plff. one thousand dollars, and therefore she brings suit.</p>

         <p>Brent for pltff</p>

         <p>J. Doe<br/>
&amp; R. Doe Pledges &amp;c</p>
