<div class="body ">

<p>I hereby authorize Rd. Wallach atty to enter his appearance for me in the above suit &amp; confers judgment for the freedom of the Petitioner. she pay all costs.</p>

<p>Cushing Daniel</p>

<p>I acquiese in the above authority to confer Judgment for the Petitioner so far as I have claim. to her services.</p>

<p>Jno. H. Daniel</p>
