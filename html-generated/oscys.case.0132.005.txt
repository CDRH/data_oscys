         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Peggy Barnes late of Washington County, if she shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and her safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> she be &amp; appear <del>have her body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of George Crowner &amp; Daniel Over filed in this Court aft her.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 11th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty two.</p>

         <p>Issued the 14th day of Jany 1842.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Key.</p>
