<p>To the Honble Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
     
<p>The Petition of Charity Crowner humbly sheweth that your petitioner is a free woman &amp; born of a free white woman and is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Rezin Arnold of Washington County.</p>

<p>Wherefore she prays that supba maybe issued to said Arnold &amp; that he may be compelled to appear and answer hereto and that she may be discharged from her service and adjudged free according to Law.</p>

<p>FSKey for Petr</p>
