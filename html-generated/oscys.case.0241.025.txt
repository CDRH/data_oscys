      <div class="caption ">

         <p>Petition</p>

         <p>To the Honble. The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District for Washn Co.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The Petition of Samuel Stacy humbly represents</p>

         <p>That he is a free man of color, having become legally and equitably entitled to his freedom, as he is prepared to shew.</p>

         <p>That nevertheless one James Knox claims to hold him as a slave: Wherefore he prays <span class="unclear illegible">[subp?]</span> against the said James Knox, and that Judgment may be rendered establishing the freedom of your petitioner: &amp; he will ever pray &amp;</p>

         <p>Carlisle for Petr.</p>
