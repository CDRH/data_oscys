         <p>Capt. Spencer Ball agent for Henry W. Ball Esq.</p>
                
         <p>Sir</p>
                
         <p>Please to take notice that on Friday the 20th day of May 1814 at the tavern of Joseph Rogers Northumberland Courthouse Virginia— We shall proceed to take the deposition of William Routt to be used as evidence in the case now depending in the Circuit Court of the United States. for the District of Columbia &amp; County of Washington in which Emanuel alias Emanuel Gasbury is petitioner for his freedom and the said Henry W. Ball is defendant.</p>
                
         <p>F.S. Key atty for Petitioner</p>
                
         <p>Washington<br/>
May 1st 1814</p>
                
         <p>I acknowlege due service of the above notice<br/>
Spencer Ball Jr<br/>
Agt for<br/>
Henry W. Ball</p>
