<div class="body ">
                
<p>Francis S. Key Esq</p>
                
<p>Sir,</p>
                
<p>I was informed that the Davis Trial was to come in this Court &amp; as you have been kind enough to do the Business for the Petition that was filed against me I hope you will enter a Disclaimer for me the boys name was Isaac &amp; I sold him in the yr 1803 or 4 but think it was in the yr 1803 you may be sure I will pay you</p>
                
<p>I am sir with due respect<br/>
yrs J Travers</p>
                
<p><span class="unclear illegible">[PS?]</span> Mr Morsell is kind enough to give any assistance that lays in his power with you</p> 
                
<p>July 4. 1810</p>
