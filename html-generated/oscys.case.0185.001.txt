         <p>To the Hon the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Mary Duckett, respectfully sets forth, that she is claimed and unlawfully
            held as a slave in the City of Washington, by 
            <del class="one Benjamin O. Sheckles">one Benjamin O. Sheckles</del> John McCutchen and John H.G. McCutchen, and she prays that your Honours would take
            her cause into consideration, and upon a full hearing thereof, grant her that freedom
            from which she is withheld.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>And she further prays security against violence, or a forcible removal pending her
            application for a trial.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>D.A. Hall for Petitioner.</p>
         
         
      </div>
