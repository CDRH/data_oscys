         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br> 
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Zachariah Cox if he shall be found within the county
            of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city
            of Washington, on the first Monday of May next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the petition for freedom of Negro Gerrard in said Court exhibited against
            him
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril; and have you then and there this Precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness William Cranch, Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 3rd day of February 1831.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent clk</p>
         
         
         <p>K &amp; D</p>
