         <p>We command you, That you summon Mrs. Patterson to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the town of Alexandria, <del>on the</del> immediately <del>day of the Court, which commence on the Monday in</del> to testify, and the truth to say, on behalf of Negro Samuel in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro Samuel Plaintiff &amp; Jesse Green Deft: and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33. And have then there this Writ. Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 1st day of May 1819</p>
