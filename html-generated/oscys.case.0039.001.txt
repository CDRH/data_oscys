      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of James Thompson a person of colour Humbly
            Sheweth that your Petitioner of right is entitled to his freedom yet
            nevertheless is held and unjustly detained in Slavery by a certain
            Electius Spalding of Washington
            County aforesaid wherefore he prays this Honourable Court to
            grant that the United States writ of subpoena may issue to
            the said Electius Spalding directed Commanding him to be and
            appear before this Honourable Court to answer your petitioner in the premisses
            and that your Honours would grant him such other and further relief as the
            nature of his Case may require and your Petitioner will ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FS Key for Petitioner</p>
