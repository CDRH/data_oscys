         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting</p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Sabritt Scott that all excuses and delays set aside
            he be and appear before the Circuit Court (of the District of Columbia, holding for
            the County of Washington at the City of Washington) here immediately to answer the
            petition of Negro "Ben" for his freedom this day filed against him. Hereof fail not
            at your peril and 
            <del class="an">an</del> have you then and there this writ Witness the honl. William Cranch Esq. Chief Judge
            of the said court the 8th October 1806 Issued 8th October 1806
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Key</p>
