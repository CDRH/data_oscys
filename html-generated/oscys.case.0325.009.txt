         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon Alexander Tolson <del>late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3rd Monday of Octo. next, to answer <del>unto</del> The petition for freedom of Harriet Young, Wm. F. Young, Marg't Young &amp; Sarah J. Young</p>
