         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Isaac Buckingham if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            
            <del class="at rules">at rules</del> to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of March next, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint">bill of complaint</del> Petition for freedom of William H. McKenney filed in Court here against him 
            <del class="and others">and others</del>.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 2nd day of January 1846.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>S &amp; S.</p>
         
         
         <p>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on
            which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.
         </p>
