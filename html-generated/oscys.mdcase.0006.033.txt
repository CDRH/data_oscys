         <p>Robert Thomas<br/>

Henry Pile</p>

         <p>Petition for freedom in the General Court</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The deposition of Mrs. Sarah Boarman of Charles county aged forty or thereabouts, deposeth &amp; sayeth that she knows the parties in the above suit. That she knew Betty commonly called Betty Mingo that she has heard her say her Mother was a White Woman &amp; a Welch Woman. This deponant says she knows Judy the mother &amp; Sophia the Grand Mother of the petitioner &amp; that she has known them for thirty years. This deponant further sayeth that she lived with Mrs Elizabeth Pile the mother of the defendant about fifteen years &amp; that she asked her if Betty Mingo's Mother was a white woman &amp; that she answered the family of the Mingos says so. This deponant further saith that the Petitioner &amp; his descendants has always been reputed slaves &amp; held as such</p>

         <p>Sarah Boarman</p>
