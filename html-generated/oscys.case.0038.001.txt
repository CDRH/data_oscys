<div class="caption ">

<p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the
District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

<p>The Petition of Jenny Thompson a person of
colour Humbly Sheweth</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>That your Petitioner is of right entitled to her freedom yet nevertheless is held
and unjustly detained in Slavery by a Certain Joseph Clarke
of Washington County aforesaid wherefore she prays your
Honours to grant that the United States writ of subpoena
may issue to the said Joseph Clarke directed commanding him
to be and appear before your Honours to answer your Petitioner in the premises
and that your Honours would grant her such other and further relief as the
nature of her Case may seem to require &amp; your Petitioner will ever pray
&amp;c.</p>

<p>FS Key for Petitioner</p>

</div>
