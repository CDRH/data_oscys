      <div class="body">
                
         <p>The Petition of Becky Joyce on behalf of herself and Child.</p>

         <p>Humbly Sheweth</p>
                
         <p>That they <del>were</del> are detained in Slavery by William Jenkins &amp; pray process that they may assert their freedom.</p>
                
         <p>And Your Petitioner will ever pray.</p>
                
         <p>Hiort</p>
