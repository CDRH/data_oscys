         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon John B Lambert if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the second Monday of April next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> Answer the Petition of Freedom of Nace Humphreys filed in court here against him</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril: and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 4th day of November 1823</p>

         <p>W Brent clk</p>

         <p>(K. &amp; D.)</p>

      </div>
