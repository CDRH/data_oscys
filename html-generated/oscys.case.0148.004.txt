<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Nelson Conrad late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and him safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> he be &amp; appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer unto the Petition of Jane Lewis &amp; others for their freedoms filed in the said Court.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 4th day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty two.</p>

<p>Issued the 9th day of August 1842.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>B &amp; Brent</p>
