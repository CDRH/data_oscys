<div class="caption ">

<p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of
the District of Columbia for Washington County the Petition of Lynch Wormly a man of colour 
respectfully Sheweth</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>That your Petitioner is of right entitled to his freedom yet is held and unlawfully
detained in Slavery by a certain Smith Cock now of 
Washington County. He therefore prays your Honours to 
grant that the United States Writ of Subpoena may 
issue to the said Smith directed commanding him 
to be and appear before your Honours to answer your Petitioner in the 
premises and that you, would grant your Petitioner such other and further 
relief as the nature of his case may seem to require and your Petitioner will 
Ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>FSKey for the Petitioner</p>

<p>Mr Brent<br/>
will please to file the above Petition &amp; issue a subpoena immediately 
and give the Petitioner his certificate of Protection<br/>
FS Key<br/>
George Town<br/>
July 10th 1815</p>
  
</div>
