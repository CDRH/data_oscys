<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Francis Bowling and Clarissa B. Gardner administrators of Richard B. Gardner late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> they <del>have</del> be &amp; appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto the Petition of Margaret Norah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins their friend for their freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 16th day of July Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty five.</p>

<p>Issued the 19th day of July 1845.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Bradley</p>
