         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> summon Francis Bowling and Clarissa B. Gardner administrators of Richard B. Gardner
            late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington
            in your said District, 
            <del class="and safely keep,">and safely keep,</del> so that 
            <del class="you">you</del> they 
            <del class="have">have</del> be &amp; appear 
            <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid,
            at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto the
            Petition of Margaret Norah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins their friend for their freedom.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the 16th day of July Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty five.
         </p>
