      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Olivia Kean &amp; Silas Burke Executors of James Kean Jr. late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> they <del>have bod</del> be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of George Kean filed in the said Court aft them.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 13th day of July Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 37.</p>

         <p>Issued the 19th day of July 1837.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
