   <div class="case">

      <div class="caption">

         <p>Negro Fanny &amp; her children<br/>
vs.<br/>
Thomas Quantrill</p>

         <p>Interrogatories on the part of the Petitioner.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>1. Do you know the parties in the above cause or either of them?</p>

         <p>2. Did you know George Roberts late of Talbott County deceased?</p>

         <p>3. Do you know of what estate real &amp; personal the said George Roberts dec'd seized or possessed?</p>

         <p>4. Did the said George Roberts hold any &amp; what land at the time of his death &amp; what do you consider was the value of it.</p>

         <p>5. Where is the said land situated? Who owns &amp; holds it at this time?</p>

         <p>6th. Did you know Negro Fanny &amp; her children or any of them who formerly belonged to the said Roberts? Was the said Negro Fanny at any time &amp; when after the death of said Roberts out of the possession of the said Roberts's administrators? Was she passing about &amp; acting as a free woman?</p>

         <p>7. Do you know of any other matter or thing in favor of the Plaintiff or Deft.?</p>

         <p>8. State the age &amp; names of the said Fanny's children</p>

         <p>F S Key for Petr.</p>

      </div>
