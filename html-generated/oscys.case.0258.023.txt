<div class="body ">    
     
<p>I hereby agree on the part of Alexander Scott that any original papers filed in the case of Walter &amp; Denis Thomas vs. the said Alexander Scott in the circuit court for the district of Columbia &amp; county of Washington may be taken out, upon Copies being previously taken by the clerk &amp; filed, &amp; upon security being given that they shall be returned.</p>

<p>Henry Ashton att. for Alexander Scott</p>

<p>We the undersigned hereby bind ourselves in the penalty of two thousand dollars that the original papers above mentioned four in number this day delivered to J Law shall be returned. July 24th 1810</p>

<p>John Law<br></br>
Thomas Law</p>
