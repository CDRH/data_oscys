<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia 
for Washington County.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Samuel Bias, humbly sheweth, that 
your petitioner is detained illegally in slavery by <del>John Rose</del> Thomas Brooke Bell 
though he is <del>reaso</del> entitled to his freedom, <del>though he is</del> as he is prepared to verify, &amp; he therefore humbly prays that this Honorable Court will take his cause into consideration, and direct such process and steps to be taken 
therein as to this Honorable Court may seem proper and this Law has provided. &amp; your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p><del>D.W. Forrest petn</del></p>
    
<p>F S Key for Petr</p>
