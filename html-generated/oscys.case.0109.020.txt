<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you take Ann Turner, late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> <del class="Bet">Bet</del> the Petition of Freedom of Betsey Burke</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 12th day of Jany. Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 57</p>

<p>Issued the 2nd day of February 1857.</p>

<p>Jno: A Smith Clk</p>

<p>Ennis</p>
