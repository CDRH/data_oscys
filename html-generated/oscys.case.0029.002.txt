<div class="caption ">
                
<p>District of Columbia.... to wit.</p>

<p>The United States of America,</p>
                
<p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>You are hereby commanded to summon, Henry W Ball late
                        of Washington County
                    if he shall be found in the county of
                        Washington,
                    that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear
                    before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to
                    be held for the county of Washington, at
                    the city of Washington, on the first Monday of June
                    next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the
                        petition of Emanuel Gasbury this day
                        exhibited in our said Court against him for freedom and so forth</p>
                
<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
                
<p>Witness Wm Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court.</p>

<p>Issued the 13th day of May 1813</p>
                
<p>(FSKey)</p>
                
<p>Wm Brent, Clk</p>
