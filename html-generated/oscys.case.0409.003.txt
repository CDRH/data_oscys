         <p>We command you, That you summon Thomas Tucker S, Edward Adams &amp; Austin Adams to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on <del>the</del> 
            <del>Monday</del> Wenesday next day of <del>Court, which commences on the Monday</del> to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Jacobs &amp; Williams in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined between Negro Delilah &amp; als. v. said Jacobs &amp; Williams and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 3d day of November in the year of our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and thirty two</p>
