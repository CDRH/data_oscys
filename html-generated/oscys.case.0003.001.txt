<div class="caption ">
                
<p>To the Honble The Judges of The Circuit Court of the District Court for the County of Washington.</p>
                
<p>The Petition of Ann Shorter for and on behalf of her Daughter Kitty and Kitty's Child William.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>Humbly Sheweth,</p>
                
<p>That Your Petr's Child Kitty and Kitty's Child William, are detained in Slavery by Daniel Rapine; She therefore pray, that process may issue so, that the freedom of the said Kitty &amp; William may be aserted &amp; proven in this Court, And Your Petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.</p>
                
<p>H: Hiort for the Petitioner.</p>
                
<p>28 June 1808</p>
