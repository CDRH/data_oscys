<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Isaac Buckingham late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and safely keep">and safely keep</del>, so that <del class="you">you</del> he be and <del class="have">have</del> appear <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto William H. McKenny's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 7th day of <del class="May">May</del> June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.</p>

<p>Issued the <del class="17th">17th</del> 22d day of <del class="May">May</del> June 1847.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>S. &amp; S.</p>
