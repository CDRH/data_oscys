      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Isaac Buckingham late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep</del>, so that <del>you</del> he be and <del>have</del> appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto William H. McKenny's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him</p>
