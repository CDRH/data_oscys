         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside you be and
            appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the
            county of Washington, on first Monday of June
            next, to answer 
            <del class="the bill of complaint">the bill of complaint</del> Petition for Freedom of Jenny Thompson in the said court exhibited against you. Hereof
            you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.
         </p>

            court, the 19 day of April 1815.
