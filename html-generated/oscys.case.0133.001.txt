         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of George Kean otherwise called George a coloured man humbly complaining
            sheweth unto your Honors that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly
            held in bondage by Oliver Kean and Silas Burke Exer of Jas Kean Jr who clam your Petitioner
            as their slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to
            the said Oliver Kean and Silas Burke directed and such relief in the premises as may
            be according to law
         </p>
         
         
         <p>John J Dermott
            <br></br>
            Atty for Petitioner
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
