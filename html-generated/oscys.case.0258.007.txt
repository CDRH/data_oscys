         <p>The petition of Walter Thomas &amp; Dennis Thomas, humbly sheweth, That your petitioners
            are detained illegally in slavery by Alexander Scott, tho' they are entitled to freedom
            as they are prepared to verifty, &amp; they therefore humbly pray, that this Honble Court
            will take their cause into consideration, &amp; direct 
            <span class="torn page">[torn page]</span> taken thereon 
            <span class="torn page">[torn page]</span>
            Court may seem meet, &amp; the laws have provided. And your petitioners will forever pray
            &amp;c:
         </p>
         
         
         <p>John Law
            <br></br>
            
            <del class="Petitrs.">Petitrs.</del> soler.
         </p>
