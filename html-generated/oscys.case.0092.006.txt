         <p>To the Marshall,</p>

         <p>Sir,</p>

         <p>If in the trial of the case of Matilda &amp; her children for his freedom agst Mr George Mason &amp; myself the verdict of the Jury shd be agst the Petitioner, I request that you will take &amp; keep her in custody &amp; Mr Mason &amp; myself will be responsible for her Jail fees.</p>

         <p>29 Apl 1823.<br/>
A Moore</p>

         <p>F.Moore</p>
