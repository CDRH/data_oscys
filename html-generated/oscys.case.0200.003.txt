         <p>If from the evidence the Jury shall be of opinion that 
            <del class="M">M</del> Andrew B. McLean was the bona fide owner of the said petitioner, and while such bona
            fide owner resided as an officer 
            <span class="illegible">[bound?]</span> in the employment of the United States for a series of years at Fortress Monroe within
            the Territory of the United States, and not within the 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> of any state of this Union, and brought from the said Territory into the District
            of Columbia, 
            <del class="when">when</del> with his family when he removed here to reside, 
            <del class="thence on">thence on</del> then although the Jury shall further find that the said petitioner was sold within
            three years after such removal into the said District she is not entitled to her freedom
            under such sale and removal, unless they shall further find that such residence at
