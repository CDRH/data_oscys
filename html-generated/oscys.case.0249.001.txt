      <div class="body">

         <p>Personally appeared before the Subscriber a Justice of the peace for the County aforesaid, Mary C. Rooker, and made Oath in due form of Law, that Negro Woman Charlotte has for the last twenty two years previous to the marriage of her mistress to Mr John Graeff <span class="unclear illegible">[Jr?]</span> resided in Alexandria, and she verily believes that she (Negro Charlotte) never did at any time reside in any other place than Alexandria; and this Deponent further states that her sister (Mrs Grarff) did for the last four years previous to her marriage reside chiefly with her in Alexandria.</p>   

         <p>Given under my hand &amp; Seal this 4th day of November 1822.<br/>
Robert Young</p>
