         <p>District of Columbia....to wit,</p>
                
         <p>The United States of America, to John Travers</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
                
         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the
                    county of Washington, in
                    the city of Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer the <del>bill of complaint</del> Petition for freedom of 
                    Isaac Davis &amp; <del>John</del> Edgar Davis in the said court exhibited against you.</p>
                
         <p>Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p> 
                
         <p>Witness, William Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the first day of Feby 18<del>0</del>10.</p>
                
         <p>Issued the eleventh day of February 18<del>0</del>10.</p>
                
         <p>Wm Brent C.</p>
                
         <p>E. J. Lee</p>
