<div class="caption ">
                
<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>
                
<p>The Petition of Ann Davis in behalf of herself and her children David, Sarah, Frederick &amp; Hanson— Humbly Sheweth</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>That your Petitioner and her Children above named are entitled to freedom, yet they are detained in Slavery by Charles Minifee who resides within the Jurisdiction of this Court; she therefore prays process may issue in behalf of herself and Children that they may assert their right and,</p>
                
<p>Your Petitioners will ever pray Ann Davis for herself and Children in their proper persons</p>
                
<p>8th Aug 1809</p>
                
<p>At the Trial of this <span class="illegible">[fact?]</span>, I shall appear Council for the Petitioners.<br></br>
Henry Hiort</p>
                
<p>To Wm Brent Esq Clerk of the Court.</p>
