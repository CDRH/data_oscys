<p>District of Columbia, Washington County ss:</p>

<div class="body ">
                                                 
<p>To John Hepburn Greeting: you are hereby cited and admonished to be and appear at a Supreme Court of the United States to be holden at the City of Washington, on the first Monday of August next, pursuant to a writ of error, filed in the Clerk's office of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington in a cause wherein Mina Queen &amp; Louisa her child <del class="w">w</del> are Petitioners and Appellants and you are Defendant to shew cause if any there be why the Judgment rendered against the said Petitioners should not be reversed, &amp; why the errors complained of should not be corrected &amp; why speedy justice should not be done to the parties in that behalf: Witness the Honorable William Cranch Chief <del class="Justice">Justice</del> Judge of <del class="said">said</del> the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia this twenty ninth day of June in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred &amp; ten.</p>
                
<p>Allowed.<br></br>
W. Cranch</p>

</div>

<div class="caption ">
                
<p>District of Columbia Washington County ss.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>Personally appeared before me one of the Justices of the Peace for Washington County afsd Elextius Spalding &amp; made oath that on the 29th day of this month of June he delivered to John Hepburn's wife at  the House of said Hepburn  a true copy of the above citation. Sworn before me this 29th day of June A.D. 1810</p>
                
<p>Saml N Smallwood</p>
