      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of negro William humbly sheweth that your petitioner is entitled to his freedom, and is unjustly held in bondage &amp; claimed as a slave by a certain         Alford now of Washington County aforesaid. Wherefore he prays that the United States writ of subpona may be issued, directed to the said Defendant Alford, that he may be &amp; appear before your Honors, &amp; that your Petitioner may be released from servitude &amp; obtain such other &amp; further relief as to your Honors may seem right &amp; according to law.</p>

         <p>FSKey for Petr</p>

      </div>
