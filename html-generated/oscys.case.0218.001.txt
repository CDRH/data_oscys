<p>To the Honourable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Fanny Blue a person of colour humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honourable Court that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly bend in bondage by John W Williams, Mary S Williams his wife and William S. Colquhoun who claim your Petitioner as their slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the said Honourable Court to grant a subpoena to the said John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law</p>

<p>John J Dermott atty for Petitioner</p>

</div>
