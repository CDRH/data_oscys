         <p>If the jury shall find from the whole evidence, that Mr. Todd from the year '46 made
            this District his place of residence till the time of his death &amp; made his will here,
            they may infer, that he abandoned his domicil in Va &amp; acquired it here, and if they
            so find the Petrs are entitled to their freedom
