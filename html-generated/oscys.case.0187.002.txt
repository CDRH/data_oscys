<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Edmund E. Richards if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del class="first">first</del> third Monday of October next, to answer the <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition of negress Jane for her freedom filed in Court here against him <del class="and others">and others</del>.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness the Hon. Wm Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 15th day of July 1847.</p>

<p>Wm Brent clk</p>

<p>Williams</p>
