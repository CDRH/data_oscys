<p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
                
<p>The United States of America— greeting.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>You are hereby commanded to Summon James Cassin if he shall
                   be found within the County of Washington that all excuses
                   and delays set aside, he be and appear before the Court here immediately, to
                   answer the Petition of Davy Davis in this Court filed
                   against him for his freedom. Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then
                   &amp; there this Writ. Witness the hon. Wm
                       Cranch Chief Judge of our said Court the 17th day of
               June 1809.</p>
                
<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
                
<p>Filed the 17th day of June 1809.</p>
                
<p>E. J. Lee</p>
