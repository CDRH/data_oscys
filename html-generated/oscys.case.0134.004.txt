         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br> 
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia,Greeting:
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Zachariah Cox if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on the first Monday of May next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the petition of freedom of Negro Gerrard filed in Court here against him and
            others
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 17th day of February 1832</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent clk</p>
         
         
         <p>(K. &amp; D.)</p>
