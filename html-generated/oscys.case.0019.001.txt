         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of George Davis Humbly sheweth</p>
         
         
         <p>That your Petitioner is entitled to freedom, yet he is detained in Slavery by Mary
            Young who resides within the Jurisdiction of this Court; he therefore prays that process
            may issue so that he may assert his rights and your Petitioner will every pray
         </p>
         
         
         <p>George Davis in his proper person.</p>
         
         
         
         <p>8th Augt 1809</p>
         
         
         <p>I undertake to appear at the Trial of this Cause for the Petitioner
            <br></br>
            Henry Hiort
         </p>
         
         
         <p>To Wm Brent Esq  Clerk of the Court</p>
