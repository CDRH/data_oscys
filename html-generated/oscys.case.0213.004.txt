         <p>Washington Cty April 8 1843</p>
         
         
         <p>This agreement made this 8th day of April 1843 between CS &amp; R Coltman of the one part
            and John Dooley of the other part
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Witness that the said John Doolen hereby Hires to the said CS &amp; R. Coltman his negro
            man Trueman Giberson at the rate of sixteen Dollars &amp; fifty cents per month (all less
            time to be deducted) the hire to be paid months to commence from the 15 day of April
            &amp; terminate on the 15 day of October.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Allso to hire his said man Trueman from the 15 day of October to the 15 day of April
            1844 to the said CS &amp; R Coltman at the rate of eight dollars per month, sickness &amp;
            unnecessary absences only to be deducted wages to be paid monthly.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Jno. Dulin
            <br></br>
            C. S. &amp; R. Coltman
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Note. The meaning of "unnecessary absence" is should Trueman leave the employment
            of C.S &amp; R. Coltman between the 15th of October 1843 and the 15th of April 1844 without
            their consent or knowledge then the time to be deducted
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Jno. Dulin
            <br></br>
            C. S. &amp; R. Coltman
         </p>
