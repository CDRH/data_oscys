<div class="body ">

<p>If the Jury shall believe from the said evidence that J. L. Pickett was a citizen of Kentucky, and a being associated to a public office and in the General Government and removed to Washington for the purpose of holding said office, and without any other <del>except</del> intention, and did in fact remove from Washington upon the expiration of his office, then the Jury may and ought to infer that he the said Pickett did not remove here to the county of Washington with the bona fide intention of settling therein.</p>

<p>Given nem con Ap. 21, 1841</p>
