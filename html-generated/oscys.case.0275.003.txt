      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To The Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The Petition of James Thomas Humbly sheweth</p>
     
         <p>That your Petitioner was born entitled to Freedom; yet he is detained in slavery by Thomas <del>I</del> Beatty Jr. who resides within the jurisdiction of this Court; he therefore prays that process may issue from your Honorable Court, so that he may assert his right, and</p>

         <p>Your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray</p>

         <p>James Thomas as in propria personae<br/>
8th Augst 1809</p>

         <p>At the Trial of this Claim I will appear as Counsel for James Thomas<br/>
Henry Hiort</p>

         <p>To Wm Brent Esqr<br/>
Clerk of the Court</p>
     
      </div>
