         <p>District of Columbia to wit
            <br></br>
            The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greeting
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Gilbert Docker if he shall be found within the
            County of Washington, that all excuses &amp; delays set aside, he be and appear before
            the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to held for the County of Washington
            at the City of Washington on the first Monday of June next, to answer the Petition
            of Henrietta (commonly called Henny) Henry (commonly called Harry) and Stephen their
            Brother for their freedom, against the said Docker.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril &amp; have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the hon: Wm Cranch Chief Judge of our said Court, the 4th day of Febry in
            the year 1809.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 8th day of Feby 1809.</p>
         
         
         <p>(Hiort)</p>
