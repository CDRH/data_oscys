         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Francis Bowling &amp; Clarissa Gardner, admins of Richard B. Gardner late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and here <del>safely keep,</del> so that you have their bodies before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom Margaret &amp; Sarah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins by David Atkins their next friend. </p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 12th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven</p>

         <p>Issued the 15th day of Jany 1847.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Bradly</p>
