         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Sarah E Linton if she shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            
            <del class="at rules">at rules</del> to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> fourth Monday of November next, to answer the
