         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear
            before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of
            Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint">bill of complaint</del> petition for freedom of Walter Thomas &amp; Dennis Thomas in the said court exhibited
            against you.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, William Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the fourth day of
            January 18
