<p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court for the District of Columbia, for Washington County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Mary E. Simmes, by the next friend Joseph Alexander, respectfully sheweth unto your Honors, that she the said Mary E. Simmes is unlawfully held in servitude by one John Marlow, and she humbly prays that your honors may summon said John Marlow, and that she may have a fair trial as to the question whether she be a slave or not and your &amp;c</p>

<p>J. W. Sheahan<br></br>
atty for petr.</p>
     
</div>
