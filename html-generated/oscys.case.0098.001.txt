<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Cola</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Your Petitioner Adam Wigle Humbly Sheweth unto your Honors that he is Justly entitled to his freedom and that he is unjustly held in bondage by Thomas Grimes.</p>

<p>Therefore he prays your Honors to take his case into consideration and grant him such relief in the premises as to your Honors may seem just;</p>

<p>And your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>Coxe &amp; Smith for Petr</p>
