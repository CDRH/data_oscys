         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the

         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Prince Gray Humbly sheweth; That your petitioner is illegally detained
            in slavery by Joel T Gustine, though he is entitled to his freedom, as he is prepared
            to verify. He therefore, humbly prays that this Honorable Court will take his case
            into consideration, and direct such process and steps

            to be taken thereon, as to his Honorable Court may seem proper and the Law has provided,
