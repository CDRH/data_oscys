         <p>Washington City. This is to certify that Letty Brown, the bearer of this is my property,
            that I in 1817 or 1818 purchased her from Miss Mary Greenfield, then her mistress.
            I further certify, that I have received from her (Letty) at sundry times for hundred
            and six Dollars, and that there is now due on the purchase money &amp;c to me directly
            four Dollars and ninety four cents, on payment of which, I hereby promise and bind
            myself that she (Letty) should be free her life time from bondage to any person or
            persons given under my hand this twenty first day of February in the year eighteen
            hundred and twenty three.
         </p> 
         
         
         <p>John H Lowe</p>
         
         
         <p>Balance due and to be paid J H B $64,,94</p>
         
         
         <p>Washington City 8th October 1823 Recieved from Letty through the hands of Mr. Bingey
            Twenty Dollars in party payment of the above claim
            <br></br>
            John H. Lowe
         </p>
