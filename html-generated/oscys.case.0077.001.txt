         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States 
for the District of Columbia</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>Your Petitioner Travers Dixon respectfully represents to your Honors that 
he is entitled to his freedom, and that he is illegally and unjustifiably held in bondage 
By William Herbert Esq. the administrator of his former master 
George F. Thornton deceased. Wherefore he prays your Honors to enquire 
into his case, and by Judicial interposition to extend to him that relief to which he is 
properly entitled. and your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c</p>
