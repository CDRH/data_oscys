      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>The Petition of <del>Archibald Mc</del> Negro James, otherwise called James Currill, humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Archibald McMullin, who claims your Petitioner as a slave.</p>
    
         <p>Wherefore he humbly prays the United States writ of subpoena to issue to the said Archibald McMullin directed that he may appear &amp; answer hereunto. &amp; such other relief as to your Honors may seem just.</p>
    
         <p>F S Key for Pet</p>

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia for Washington County</p>
