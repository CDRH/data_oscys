<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Elizabeth Smith Respectfully Sheweth: That your petitioner is entitled to her freedom, and is held in bondage by <del>Stephen Franklin</del> James Birch John Armfield who are about to send her out of, and beyond the District aforesaid. </p>

<p>Your Petitioner therefore prays You Honors to grant her the United States Writ of Subpoena to be directed to the said Franklin &amp; Armfield, and such other &amp; further relief in the premises as your Honors may deem just and right, and she as in duty bound will ever pray.</p>

<p>G. L. Giberson for Petitioner.</p>

</div>
