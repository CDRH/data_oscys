         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Bridget McLaughlin if she shall be found in the County of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, <del>at rules to be held for the County of Washington,</del> at the City of Washington, on the <del>first</del> fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>to bill of complaint of</del> the petition of freedom of Margaret Lewis for herself &amp; children Andrew, James &amp; Margaret Junr. filed in Court here against her <del>and others</del>.</p> 

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 28th day of December 1854.</p>

         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>

         <p>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.</p>
