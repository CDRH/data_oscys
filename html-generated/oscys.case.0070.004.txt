         <p>The petitioner having fled to this Court for protection, and having on oath asserted
            his right to freedom, &amp; having filed his petition claiming such right, it is order'd
            that, the Marshall take him into custody for safe keeping and that a subpona in the
            
            <del class="usual">usual</del> form usual in like cases be issued returnable immediately against the said     Alford,
            Jno Coburn &amp; George Milburn &amp; that the Marshall serve the same as soon as possible.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>By order of Court</p>
         
         
         <p>27. May 1826.</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
