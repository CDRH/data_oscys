         <p>June the 17th 1809</p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>In obedience to the within writ I herewith produce the body of Negro Ben and do state
            the cause of his detention in Jail to be a Commitment (by John Ott Esqr a Justice
            of the Peace for Washington County in the District of Columbia) a Copy of which is
            hereunto annexed.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Washington Boyd
            <br></br>
            Marshal D.C
         </p>

         <p>District of Columbia
            <br></br>                
            County of Washington set
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Whereas it is represented by Sabrett Scott to the Subscriber a Justice of the Peace
            that his negro Ben has runaway from his service &amp; otherwise treated him ill and fears
            that the said negro Ben will runaway again &amp; therefore prays a Commitment for Safe
            Keeping. There are Wherefore to authorize &amp; require you to receive the said Ben &amp;
            him safe keep in your Gaol  until his said master releases him therefrom or he be
            otherwise legally Discharged. Given under my hand &amp; seal this 23rd day of March 1809.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Signed
            <br></br>
            John Ott (Seal)
         </p>
         
         
         <p>The Marshal of D.C.</p>
         
         
         <p>True Copy from the Original</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Boyd</p>
