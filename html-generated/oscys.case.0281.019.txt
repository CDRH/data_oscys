      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honbl. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Colbia:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>The Petition of Milly Henderson respectfully represents unto your honors. That your petitioner is entitled to her freedom. That she is unjustly held in bondage by one William Sexsmith and prays that a subpoena may issue to the said William Sexsmith, and her rights be inquired into by your honors. &amp; she will ever pray &amp;c.</p>
 
         <p>Wm B.B. Cross<br/>
Atty: for Petitioner</p>
