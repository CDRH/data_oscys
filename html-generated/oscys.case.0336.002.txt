         <p>Circuit Court, June Term, 1812</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Quandell that all excuses and delays set aside, he be and appear before the court here immediately, to <del>testify for</del> answer the petition of Negro Fanny &amp; her three children exhibited against him in our said Court for freedom</p>

         <p>Issued the 17 day of June 1812</p>

         <p>By order, Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.</p>
