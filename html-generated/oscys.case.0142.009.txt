         <p>In the above cases it is agreed that Benjamin F Rittenhouse will prove that he first
            new Frank the Petitioner in the family of Dr James Laurie one of the Defts in the
            fall of 1828 or the winter of 1828 and 1829 That the said B F Rittenhouse has been
            acquainted with the defendants for the last eleven years and during that period has
            never known the Defendants to have any interest in any other slaves of the name of
            Henry Frank &amp; Sarah that the Petitioners above named. This agreed that the above shall
            be read in evidence as if the
