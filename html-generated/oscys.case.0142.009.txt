<p>This is to Certify that I have brought from Virginia, for my own use &amp; not for Sale, the following named slaves. Vizt Henry, George &amp; Sarah, which slaves I acquired by marriage, with my wife.</p>

<p>Given under my hand this 20th day of Decr 1824.</p>

<p>James Laurie</p>

<p>True Copy<br/>
Test Wm Brent Ck</p>

<p>Recorded 20th Decr. 1824.<br/>
Liber W. B. No. 13 folio 180.</p>

<p>I have brought from Virginia, not for Sale, but for my own use a negro Boy, a Slave for life, named Frank aged about ten years which Boy I possess in consequence of my marriage.</p>

<p>Given under my hand this 21st day of April 1830.</p>

<p>James Laurie</p>

<p>True Copy<br/>
Test Wm Brent Ck</p>

<p>Recorded 21st Apl 1830.<br/>
in Liber W. B. No. 29 fol 503</p>
