<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of negroes Gabriel the elder, Nelly his wife, <del class="and">and</del> Gabriel the younger and Rachel respectfully shows that your petitioners are entitled to their freedom, and are unjustly held in bondage by John <del class="G.">G.</del> Stuart, who claims them as slaves. Your petitioners therefore pray for the United States writ of subpena to the said John <del class="G.">G.</del> Stuart directed, and that he may be compelled to appear and answer thereto, and that your petitioners may be discharged, &amp;c.</p>

<p>P R Fendall for the Petitioners</p>
