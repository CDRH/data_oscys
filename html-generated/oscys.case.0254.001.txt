      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William C. Greenleaf Guardian of children of John
            L. Brightwell if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear
            in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County
            of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of March next to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Mary Jane Wayne &amp; Joseph C. Wayne, William R. Wayne, Daniel
            Wayne &amp; Harriet Wayne, her children filed in Court here against him 
            <del class="and others">and others</del>.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 20th day of Novr 1846.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Bradley</p>
