         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William C. Greenleaf Guardian of children of John L. Brightwell if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del>first</del> 4th Monday of March next to answer the <del>bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Mary Jane Wayne &amp; Joseph C. Wayne, William R. Wayne, Daniel Wayne &amp; Harriet Wayne, her children filed in Court here against him <del>and others</del>.</p>
    
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
    
         <p>Witness the Hon. W Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
    
         <p>Issued the 20th day of Novr 1846.</p>
    
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
    
         <p>Bradley</p>

         <p>
            <del>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above sub. poena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.</del>
         </p>
