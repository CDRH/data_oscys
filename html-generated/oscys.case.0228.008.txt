         <p>In this cause JM Carlisle now appears for the Petitioner and states to the court that
            
            <del class="the">the</del> 
            <del class="the">the</del> Mr May the counsel for Petitioner who initiated the suit has been prevented during
            the whole term from attending the court by ill health: and that in consequence of
            
            <del class="that">that</del> absence &amp; ill health of Mr May the present counsel cannot have access to papers &amp;
            information necessary to the trial of the cause.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>And upon these facts he moves the court for a continuance.</p>
         
         
         <p>J. M. Carlisle 
            of consul for Petr
