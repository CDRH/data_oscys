         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States for the District

         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Priscilla King a colo woman, humbly sheweth unto your Honors that
            she is entitled to Freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by William Wilkinson, who
            claims your 
            <del class="petit">petit</del> petitioner as his Slave, wherefore your petition prays your Honors to grant a subpoena
            directed to the said William Wilkinson and such other and further relief in the premises
            as maybe in accordance with Law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Ratcliffe &amp; Kennedy
            <br></br>
            Attys for petitioner.
         </p>
