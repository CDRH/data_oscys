         <p>The case of Negroe Milly.</p>

         <p>The facts set forth in the annexed petition appear to be correctly stated and according to those facts, It is my opinion that the said negroe Milly <del>is</del> according to the laws of Virginia, now the law of the County of Alexandria, is entitled to her freedom, which is respectfully submitted.</p>

         <p>Edm: J Lee<br/>
4th Decemr. 1805</p>
