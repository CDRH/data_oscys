      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honorable to Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Nelly Lemon for herself and her infant children, Thomas, Lavinia, Betsey, Dennis and Amy, respectfully sets forth to your honors.</p>

         <p>That she is a woman of color and entitled to her freedom, and also that her aforementioned five children are entitled to their freedom, that they are all now held in a state of slavery by one John H. Bayne contrary to law and your petitioner's just rights. Your Petitioners therefore prays your honors to grant her &amp; her children such relief as she &amp; they may be lawfully entitled to and such process of your honorable Court against the said John H Bayne as is usual in such cases to compel the attendance of the said John H Bayne in your Honorable Court to answer this petition, and to enter the usual security &amp; recognizance not to remove your petitioner beyond the Jurisdiction of your honorable Court  and is duty bound your petitioners will ever pray &amp;c &amp;c</p>

         <p>Coxe &amp; Smith<br/>
for Petitioners</p>

      </div>
