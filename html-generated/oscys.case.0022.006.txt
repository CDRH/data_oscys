         <p>I do hereby Emancipate and set free the following Negroes after they have served their respective Terms of Servitude hereafter mentioned to wit Negro Jack his wife Patience from the date hereof and her future increase forever Negro Michael after he has served the term of five years from the first day of January next, Negro Nancy after she has served the term of Eight years from the first day of January next and her increase forever. Negro John after he has served the term of fifteen years from the first day of January next; Negro Letty after she has served the term of thirteen years from the first day of January next; and Negro Bobadil after he has served the term of twenty three years from the first day of January next And I for myself my Heirs, Executors and Administrators for ever grant all claim to any further service of the aforementioned negroes their increases after they shall have served their respective terms of servitude above mentioned</p>
   
         <p>In Testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal this twenty first day of July seventeen hundred ninety Eight<br/>
Overton Carr (seal)</p>   

         <p>Executed in the presence of J T. Frost one of the Justices of the Peace for Prince George County</p>  

         <p>Enrolled July 22 1798</p>

         <p>Maryland set</p>

         <p>I hereby Certify that the foregoing is truly taken from Liber JRM No. 6 page 389 one of the Land Records of Prince Georges County <span class="unclear illegible">Md[?]</span>
         </p>

         <p>In Testimony thereof do hereto subscribe my name and affix the seal of Prince Georges County Court this twenty third day of May Eighteen hundred fourteen<br/>
John Read Magruder Clk PG CC</p>
