<p>To the Honorable the judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Milly Ann Robertson and of Henrietta Robertson, <span class="ink">[ink]</span> her infant under 21 years of age by the said Milly Ann, her mother &amp; next friend, humbly represents, that your petitioners are now held in bondage and claimed as his slaves by one Alexander Waugh. That your petitioners are entitled to their freedom &amp; therefor pray your Honors to grant unto them the United States writ of subpoena to be directed to the said War &amp; such other relief in the premises as your Honors may deem just &amp; proper &amp;c</p>

<p>Richard Wallach for petitioners</p>

</div>
