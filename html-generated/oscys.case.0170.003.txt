         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Benjamin Buck late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that he be &amp; appear <del>you have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Maria Kennedy filed in the said Court against him for her freedom.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the second day of May Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and thirty five.</p>

         <p>Issued the 20th day of May 1835.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>(Bradley)</p>
