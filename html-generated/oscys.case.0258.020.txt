         <p>Prince Georges County Court April Term 1797</p>

         <p>Peter Thomas<br/>
a.<br/>
Samuel Hamilton</p>

         <p>Petition for Freedom<br/>
Trial <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> Plaintiff called on return of Jury &amp; makes default<br/>
Judgment of <em class="term">non pros</em>.</p>

         <p>Test<br/>
Jno Read Magruder Jr. Cl<br/>
PGCyC</p>

         <p>It appears from the petition filed in the above Case that the Petitioner Peter Thomas <del>is</del> descended from a white woman That his mother, was Peg Thomas, who was the Daughter of Peg Thomas who was the Daughter of Betty Mingo who was the Daughter of a free white woman.</p>

         <p>Jno R Magruder Jr. Cl</p>

John Read Magruder Jr. Cl<br/>
