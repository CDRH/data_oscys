         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon Jane W Stevenson late of Washington County, if she shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and her safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> she <del>have her body</del> be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforsaid, at the City of Washington, on the Fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for Freedom of Benjamin Qualls</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington the 1st day of December Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty five</p>

         <p>Issued the 26th day of December 1845.</p>

         <p>Test Wm Brent, Clk</p>

         <p>C S Wallach</p>
