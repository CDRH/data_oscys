         <p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for
            the County of Washington.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Matilda Gray respectfully sheweth, that your petitioner &amp; her child
            William who is about the age of two years are entitled to their freedom, and are nevertheless
            unjustly held in bondage by a certain Mary Anne Pic of Georgetown in Washington County
            aforesaid; wherefore your petitioner prays that your honors will order a subpœna to
            be issued, directed to the said Mary Anne. &amp; that you will afford such other relief
            in the premisses as may be agreeable to law and as to your honors may seem just &amp;
            right. and your petitioner as in duty Bound will ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F.S. Key for Petitioner</p>
