         <p>Charles Scott, Senior.</p>

         <p>Brot Lonnon from Meryland into the town of Allexandria Aug <del>182</del> 1802 and Lived in said town <del>with him</del> untill about a weak before Christmas as his property at which time he the Said Scott died. <span class="gap crossed out">[crossed out]</span> After which Lonnon Continued to Live in Said town till Some time in the Spring unmolested by Any. At which time Chars. Scott Son of C: Scott Senior Claimed him, And in this Situation remains till this Day Apl. 30th 1804.</p>

         <p>Witnesses for Lennon.</p>

         <p>Robert Brocket to prove wen he hired Lonnon &amp; from what person.</p>

         <p>Josa. Doens to prove the same and how Long he continued with him.</p>

         <p>John Shepherd to prove the same after his Masters Death and when his Masters Son Claimed him as his property.</p>

         <p>Mr. Thomas Butler to prove that Lonnon Came from Meryland and What time &amp; who Claimed him then.</p>

         <p>Wm. Woolford to prove that Charles Scott Jnr. Lived with him When he first Came to town.</p>
