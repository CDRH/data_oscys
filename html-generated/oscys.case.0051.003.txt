<p><strong><span class="roman">District of Columbia,</span></strong> to wit:<br/></p>

<p>The United States of America,</p>

<p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon
John Lambert late of Washington County
if he shall be found in the county of
Washington,
that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear
before the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia, to
be held for the county of Washington, in
the city of Washington, on the second Monday of April
next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of Freedom of Esther Humpreys filed in Court here filed against him.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 12th day of November 1823</p>
  
<p>K &amp; D</p>
 
<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

</div>
