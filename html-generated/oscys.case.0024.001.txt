<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit court of the United States for the District of Columbia &amp; county of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>The petition of Rachael Lyons on behalf of herself &amp; her children Nicholas Peggy Lucy Nancy Polly Charles &amp; Nelly all under age &amp; colored peoples humbly represents, That your petitioner &amp; her children are claimed by Richard M. Johnson, to whom they are informed they were held by a certain Joseph White Claggett, who unlawfully detained them in slavery until the said sale, to Richard M Johnson who now holds them in slavery. That your petitioner &amp; her children are lawfully entitled to their freedom being descended from a certain woman named Mary Lyons, who was brought into this country as an indented servant from Ireland to serve a short period of service being a white woman. Your petitioner therefore prays that her claim may be properly examined, &amp; a subpœna therefore issued to the said Richard M Johnson to appear &amp; such order &amp; proceedings may be had upon the premises, as the laws have proceeded &amp; as to your Honors may seem meet And your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.</p>
                
<p>George Wattenton Atty for petitioner</p>
                
</div>
