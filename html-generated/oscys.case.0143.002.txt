         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Priscilla Gant &amp; Basil E. Gant late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely</del> keep, so that <del>you</del> they be and <del>have</del> appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto the Petition for freedom of Hester Johnson Martha Bronson &amp; Randolph Bronson filed in the said Court against them</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 7th day of <del>May</del> June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.</p>

         <p>Issued the <del>10th</del> 22d day of May June 1847.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Ratcliffe</p>
