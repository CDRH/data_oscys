         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> summon Priscilla Gant &amp; Basil E. Gant late of Washington County, if they shall be
            found within the County of Washington in your said District, 
            <del class="and safely">and safely</del> keep, so that 
            <del class="you">you</del> they be and 
            <del class="have">have</del> appear 
            <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid,
            at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto the
            Petition for freedom of Hester Johnson Martha Bronson &amp; Randolph Bronson filed in
            the said Court against them
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the 7th day of 
            <del class="May">May</del> June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 
            <del class="10th">10th</del> 22d day of May June 1847.
         </p>
