<p>District of Columbia....to wit.</p>
                    
<p>The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                    
<p>You are hereby commanded to summon, Ann Tingoy if 
                    she shall be found in the county of Washington,
                    that (all excuses set aside) she be and appear before the circuit court of 
                    the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, at the city of Washington, on the 18th day of June instant
                        <del class="next,">next,</del> to testify on the part of Matilda Gray agt. Mary Ann Pic</p>
                    
<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
                    
<p>Witness Wm Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court.</p>
                
<p>Issued the 3d day of June 1813</p>
                                    
<p>Wm Brent Cl</p>
