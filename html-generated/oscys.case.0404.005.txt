         <p>We command you, that you summon Jeremiah Brashers <span class="unclear illegible">Sd[?]</span> to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, <del>on the</del> immediately <del>day of</del>
