         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you, That you summon Ann H. Lee to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the town of Alexandria, on the eleventh day of the present Court, which commence on the Monday in November to testify, and the truth to say, on behalf of Alexander Moore in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro Winny Plaintiff vs: Alexander Moore Defendant and this she shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33. And have then there this Writ. Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 1st day of December 1818.</p>

         <p>Edm: J. Lee Cl: Cu:</p>

      </div>
