         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear
            before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to
            be held for the county of Washington, on the first Monday of June
            next, to answer petition for freedom 
            <del class="the bill&#xA;                        of complaint">the bill
               of complaint
            </del> of Joe
            Thompson, Nelly Thompson &amp; their
            infant child Sarah Anne in the said court
            exhibited against you. Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary
            at your peril.
         </p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness, Wm Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said
            court, the 19 day of April 1815
