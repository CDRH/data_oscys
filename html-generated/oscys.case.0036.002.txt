<p>District of Columbia....to wit:</p>

<p>The United States of America, To Walter Clarke</p>
    
</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer petition for freedom <del>the bill of complaint</del> of Joe Thompson, Nelly Thompson &amp; their infant child Sarah Anne in the said court exhibited against you. Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p> 

<p>Witness, Wm Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the 19 day of April 1815</p>

<p>Issued the 31 day of May 1815</p>

<p>FSKey</p>

<p>Wm Brent, Cl</p>
