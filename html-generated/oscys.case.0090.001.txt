<div class="caption ">

<p>To the Honble the Judges of the circuit
court of the District of Columbia for the county of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of John (colored man) Lee humbly
sheweth unto your honors that he is a free 
man and that he is illegally &amp; unjustly detained 
in bondage by one William Dent who claims 
your petitioner as a slave for life. And your 
petitioner is credibly informed &amp; veryly
believes that the said Wm Dent is now about to sell 
him as a slave for life to some distant 
state</p>

<p>Your petitioner therefore prays 
your honors to consider his case and 
grant him such relief as to your honors 
shall seem meet</p>

<p>R Beale for Petitioner</p>

</div>
