<p>To the Hon: the Judges of the Circuit Court of the 
District of Columbia for the County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Your petitioner Richard Hall (a black Man) respectfully 
represents that he is improperly held as a slave by a certain 
Abraham VanMetre and prays that your Honours 
will take his case into consideration and grant such relief 
as you may think best.</p>
