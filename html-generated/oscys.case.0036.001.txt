         <p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the
            District of Columbia for Washington County
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Joe Thompson Nelly
            Thompson his wife and Sarah Anne their Infant
            Child Humbly Sheweth to your Honours. That your Petitioners persons of Colour are
            of right entitled to their freedom yet nevertheless 
            <span class="illegible">[are?]</span> held and unjustly detained in Slavery
            by a Certain Walter Clarke of Washington
            County and District of Columbia
            aforesaid. Wherefore your Petitioners pray your Honours to grant that the
            United States writ of Subpoena may issue to the said
            Walter Clarke directed commanding him to be and
            appear before your Honours to answer your Petitioners in the premises
            and that your Honours would grant such other and further relief as the
            nature of your Petitioners Case may require &amp; your Petitioners will
            ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FSKey for Petrs</p>
