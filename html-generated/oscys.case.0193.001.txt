      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States for the County of Washington in the District of Columbia</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Rebecca Hobbs a <del>neg</del> colored woman now in the County of Washington Dist. of Columbia, represents that she is illegally detained in confinement by a certain Thomas Magruder a negro trader and a certain Washington Robey, and that the said Thomas Magruder claims her as his slave for life and intends to carry her beyond the Jurisdiction of your Honors Court and that she is entitled to her freedom and is being oppressively and illegally claimed as a slave by the said Thomas Magruder. Your Petitioner therefore prays yr Honors that the United States writ of subpona may issue to the said Thomas Magruder and the said Washington Robey, as the Law directs and as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c.</p>

         <p>Wm L Brent Petitioners atty</p>

      </div>
