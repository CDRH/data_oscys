         <p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Amelia otherwise called Ammy of Washington County humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom &amp; is held in bondage as a slave for life by Elias B. Caldwell contrary to law, wherefore she prays a sbpa to the said Defendant directed that he may appear &amp; answer hereunto &amp; that your Petitioner may obtain her freedom &amp; such relief in the premisses as she is entitled to by law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr.</p>

      </div>
