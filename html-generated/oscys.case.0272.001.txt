         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District <del>Court</del> of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of negro Milly humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Basil Warring of Washington, who claims &amp; holds your Petitioner as a Slave.</p>

         <p>Wherefore she prays the United States writ of subpoena to the said Basil Warring the Defendant directed, commanding him to appear &amp; answer hereunto, that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for the Petr:</p>
