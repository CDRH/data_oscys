<p>To the Honorable the Judges of The Circuit Court of The District of Columbia 
                for the County of Washington</p>

<p>The Petition of Sarah Davis Humbly sheweth</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>That Your Petitioner is detained in Slavery by a certain James Clark Lee who 
                resides within the Jurisdiction of this Court. That your Petitioner is advised that she is entitled to her 
                freedom having been born of Parents entitled to the Same right; she therefore Prays your Honors that process may issue 
                so that she the said Sarah may maintain her pretensions. And also Prays that she 
                    may be permitted to assertain her claim</p>
                
<p>H Hiort for Complainant</p>
                
<p>Pray issue the Subpeana immediately &amp; send it to me</p>
                    
<p>13th April 1809</p>
