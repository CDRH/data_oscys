To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Nelson Conrad late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> he <del>have</del> be &amp; appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition of Jane Lewis and others for their freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 18th day of May Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty one</p>

<p>Issued the 3d day of Augt 1841.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>B &amp; Brent</p>
