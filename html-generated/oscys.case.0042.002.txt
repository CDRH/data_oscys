         <p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal
            of the District of Columbia greeting
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hearby commanded to summon 
            Mrs Janet Lingan that all excuses and
            delays set aside she be and appear before
            the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia
            for the County of Washington, on the 
            fourth Monday of December next, to answer the Petition of Jack 
            Garretson, Richard Luckett, 
            Richard Johnson and Rachel Garretson 
            exhibited in the said Court 
            <del class="against">against</del> against her for their freedom.
            Hereof fail not at your peril and hence you then and there
            this Writ. Witness the Hon Wm Cranch chief
            Judge of the said Court the 14th day of June
            Anno Domini 1817.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 2d Day of Augt 1817</p>
         
         
         <p>(Travers)</p>
