         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon John Boswell if he shall be found in the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the Second Monday of April next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> Answer the Petition for Freedom of Nace Humphreys by Dolly Humphreys his next friend</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril: and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 10th day of November 1823</p>

         <p>W. Brent clk</p>

         <p>(Caldwell)</p>

      </div>
