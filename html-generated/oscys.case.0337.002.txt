      <div class="body">

         <p>Intergies: to Walter H. Middleton.</p>

         <p>1. When &amp; by whom were you informed of the condition under which you state Amelia was taken to Fredksburg?</p>

         <p>2. <del>Did</del> When &amp; by whom were you first informed of her dissatisfaction in Fredksburg - If by Petr: what did she say.</p>

         <p>3. From whom did you obtain the information relative to the agreement of Hy. O. Middleton that she should return &amp; get a master in the Distt. were you present at that agreement &amp; what was it?</p>

         <p>4. Where did she stay in Fredrksburg or the neighbourhood &amp; how long after such agreement &amp; the order for her to come to Washington?</p>

         <p>5. When &amp; where was she sold or disposed of to Mr Caldwell?</p>

         <p>FS Key for Petr.</p>
