      <div class="body ">

         <p>(Copy.)</p>

         <p>I have this day bargained sold and delivered, and by these presents do bargain sell and deliver, to Henry Clay, for and in consideration of four hundred and fifty dollars, a negro female slave named Charlotte, aged about nineteen, which said slave I warrant and defend to said Clay against the claim of all and every person whatsoever; and I likewise warrant her to be sound.</p>

         <p>Witness my hand and seal this 12th May, 1806</p>

         <p>(signed) Jas. Condon (<span class="unclear illegible">[L.S.?]</span>)</p>

         <p>Teste.<br/>
Isaac Wells.</p>
