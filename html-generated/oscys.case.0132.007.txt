         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Peggy Barnes if she shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the
            
            <del class="first">first</del> fourth Monday of March next, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> the Petition for freedom of George Crowner &amp; Daniel Over filed in Court here against
            her 
            <del class="and others. for their freedom">and others. for their freedom</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. Wm Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 27th day of Jany 1843.</p>
