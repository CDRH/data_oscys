      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Hon: the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The Petition of Louisa Woodward humbly sheweth to you Honors that your Petitioner is now illegally and unjustly held in slavery in the District and County aforesaid by Jane Munroe, and that in fact your Petitioner is free from all terms of slavery. And your Petitioner respectfully prays that your Honors will hear and determine her case and grant her the relief she is entitled to and your Petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>C. Car &amp; J E Addison for Petitioner</p>
