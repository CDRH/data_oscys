<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Negro Willis otherwise called Willis Johnston humbly sheweth unto your Honors that his is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in <del>slavery and</del> bondage by James Laurie and James C Hall who claim your Petitioner as a slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant subpoenas to the said James Laurie and James C Hall directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law</p>

<p>John J Dermott atty for Petitioner</p>

<p>Mr Brent will please enter me as the security which the law requires and give the bearer the subpoena which I will give the Marshal<br/>
John J Dermott</p>

</div>
