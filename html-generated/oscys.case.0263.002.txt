         <p>To the Honble The Judges of the Circuit Court of The District of Columbia for the

         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Humble Petition of Henrietta (commonly called Henny) Henry (commonly called Harry)
            and Stephen their Brother Sheweth
         </p>
         
         
         <p>That Your Petitioners are born free but are detained in slavery by a certain Gilbert
            Docker, now, or late an inhabitant of this County, they therefore pray that process
            may be awarded against the said Gilbert Docker, so that your Petitioners may be permitted
            to assert their freedom, And
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Your Petitioners as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c.</p>
         
         
         <p>H. Hiort for the Petrs.</p>
         
         
      </div>
