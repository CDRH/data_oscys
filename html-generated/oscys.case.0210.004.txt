<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Elizabeth Smallwood if she shall be found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) she be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City of Washington, on fourth Monday of November next, to <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of freedom of Syvia Lee filed in Court here against her</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the <del class="30th">30th</del> 7th day of <del class="July">July</del> Augt. 1832.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>K &amp; D</p>
