<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Jesse <del>Wilson</del> Nelson humbly sheweth, that your Petitioner is free, and is held in bondage and claimed as a slave by one John Cornell a resident of said County, and he prays that his rights may be enquired of by your Honors according to Law.</p>

<p>Ennis &amp; McCutchen attys for Petitioner<br/>
Dec 3, 1852</p>
