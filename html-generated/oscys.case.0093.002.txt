<p>District of Columbia to wit</p>

<p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia greeting</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Crawford, that all 
excuses set aside, he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for 
the County of Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to 
answer the Petition of William Ethington for his freedom, exhibited 
in the said Court against him, the said William Crawford. hereof fail not at your peril, &amp; have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p> Witness the hon William Cranch, chief Justice of the Circuit Court of 
the District of Columbia, the 9th Day of Jany 1816.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Issued the 20th Day of May 1816.</p>

<p>Key.</p>
