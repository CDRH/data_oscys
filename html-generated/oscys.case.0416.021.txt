      <div class="caption">

         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of <del>Columbia</del> Maryland, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Soper Charles Nevitt, John Landsbury, Simon Campbell &amp; Enoch Campbell if they shall be found in the <del>county of Washington</del> your said District, that (all excuses set aside) they be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, <del>to be</del> now sitting <del>held</del> for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on <del>the</del> Friday 28th day of May Inst <del>next</del> to testify on the part of Rachel Shorter &amp; others against Ann Cassanave on their petition for freedom</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of our said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 25th day of May 1824</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
