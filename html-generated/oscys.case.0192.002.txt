<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Rachael Turner otherwise called Rachael a coloured woman humbly complaining sheweth unto your Honors that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by Innis B Palmer who claims your Petitioner as his slave</p>

<p>Wherefor your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to the said Innis B Palmer directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law</p>

<p>John J Dermott atty for Petitioner</p>

</div>
