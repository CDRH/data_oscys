         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit court of the United States for the district
            of Columbia &amp; county of Washington.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>The petition of Priscilla Graham on behalf of herself &amp; her six children named Catharine,
            Philip, Betsey, Nelly, Levi, &amp; Fanny humbly represents, That 
            <del class="she">she</del>, your petitioner together with her children above named are free &amp; in every respect
            entitled in law to their freedom, but she is informed that a certain Redmond Grace
            claims her as well as her children as his slaves, &amp; by unlawful means wishes to hold
            her &amp; her children as his property in servitude Wherefore your petitioner prays that
            a subpoena may issue in her case against the said Redmond, to bar his claim, &amp; that
            she may receive the Justice her case demand &amp; that the said Redmond may be compelled
            to recognize &amp; comply with the provisions of the law in such case made &amp; proceeded
            in order to a fair trial thereof
         </p>
         
         
         <p>And as in duty bound your honor will pray</p>
         
         
         <p>Elias B Caldwell &amp; John Law petrs atts</p>
