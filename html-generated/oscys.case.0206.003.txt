         <p>Mr Marbury,<br/>
Dr Sir,</p>

         <p>I have seen and conversed with Mr Ryland, who have been good enough to give me the enclosed certificate which I send you. I am sorry I did not give you and Mr Cox your fee before I left the City Hall. I will be up in a few days.</p>

         <p>respectfully yours<br/>
George Kirby</p>

         <p>April 17th 1837.</p>
