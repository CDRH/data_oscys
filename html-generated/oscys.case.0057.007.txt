         <p>It is agreed as follows—</p>
         
         
         <p>Genl Parker shall pay for Dennis, the
            sum of $300—
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Denis shall serve Genl P. the full Term of six years from the payt of
            the money.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Mr. Taylor shall execute a Deed in 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> of Genl P. for Dennis to serve six
            years and after that term to be free. To be specified in the Deed to Genl
            P.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Dennis shall execute an Indenture to
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/thumbnails/p002.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            Genl P. to serve him six years, by way of shewing his assent and in considn that
            he has secured his freedom after the six years.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>The 
            <span class="illegible">[writ?]</span> shall then be struck off the Docket.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>In witness of this agreement</p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p003.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p003.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
               <img src="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/thumbnails/p003.jpg" class="display"></img>
            </a>
         </span>
         
         
         <p>Thos Randall Esq. has signed the same for Mr
            Taylor, Genl Parker for himself and
            Augt Taney for Dennis, this 11th day of Octr AD 1823.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Thomas Randall atty for Rd Taylor
            <br></br>
            D Parker
            <br></br>
            Augt Taney atty for Dennis
