      <div class="body">

         <p>It is agreed as follows—</p>

         <p>Genl Parker shall pay for Dennis, the
sum of $300—</p>

         <p>Denis shall serve Genl P. the full Term of six years from the payt of
the money.</p>

         <p>Mr. Taylor shall execute a Deed in <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> of Genl P. for Dennis to serve six
years and after that term to be free. To be specified in the Deed to Genl
P.</p>

         <p>Dennis shall execute an Indenture to

<span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.007.002.jpg"
                  rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]"
                  title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/large/oscys.case.0057.007.002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://cdrhsites.unl.edu/figures/oscys/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.007.002.jpg"
                       class="display &#xA;            "/>
               </a>
            </span>

Genl P. to serve him six years, by way of shewing his assent and in considn that
he has secured his freedom after the six years.</p>

         <p>The <span class="unclear illegible">writ[?]</span> shall then be struck off the Docket.</p>

         <p>In witness of this agreement</p>

         <span class="pageimage">

Taylor, Genl Parker for himself and
Augt Taney for Dennis, this 11th day of Octr AD 1823.</p>
