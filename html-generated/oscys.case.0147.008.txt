         <p>In the name of God Amen I, Maria Anne T. Greenfield of Prince Georges County in the
            State of Maryland being sick and weak in body but of sound and disposing mind memory
            and understanding do make and publish this my last will and testament in manner and
            form following that is to say
         </p>
         
         
         <p>
            <span class="illegible">[Tesit?]</span> and principally I commit my sole into the hands of Almighty God and my body to the
            earth to be decently buried at the discretion of my executor hereinafter named. And
            after my debts &amp; funeral charges are paid I give and bequeath as follows.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Item I give and bequeath to my niece Amelia T 
            <span class="illegible">[Dorsett?]</span> the sum of seventy dollars to be paid to her by my executor hereafter named.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I give and bequeath to my overseer John Grimes one hundred and forty dollars to be
            paid to him by my executor hereafter named.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Item I give &amp; bequeath to my nephew Gerrard T Greenfield my negro slave Ned to serve
            him eight years then to have his freedom during his life.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I give &amp; bequeath to my nephew Gerard T. Greenfield Marias three children namely James,
            Alfred, and Eliza to serve him until they arrive at the age of thirty eight years
            old. then to be free from servitude during life.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I give and bequeath to my Nephew Gerard T. Greenfield my negro slave Chrissy to serve
            him untill she arrives to the age of sixteen years old then to be free from servitude
            during life.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I also give and bequeath to my nephew Gerard T. Greenfield the whole of my part of
            my sister Sabrina I Greenfield decd Estate that may belong to me when a decision of
            the said estate may take place
         </p>
         
         
         <p>(a) [I also give and bequeath to my nephew Gerard
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0147.008.002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0147.008.002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0147.008.002.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            I Greenfield all my negro slaves namely Ben, Mansa, James, Henry, George, Lewis, Rebecca,
            Kitty, Sophia, May, Elizabeth, Nathaniel, and Maria also Toney, Billy Betty, and Anne.
            Provided he shall not carry them out of the state of Maryland, or sell them to any
            one in either of which events I will and desire the said negroes to be free for life.]
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I give and bequeath to my nephew Gerard T Greenfield the residue of my estate not
            heretofore bequeathed in this will.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I hereby constitute and appoint my nephew Gerard T. Greenfield sole executor of this
            my last Will and Testament revoking all other wills by me heretofore made and ratifying
            and confirming this to be my last will as testament. In Testimony Whereof I have hereunto
            set my hand &amp; affixed my seal this sixteenth day of September in the year of Our Lord.
            Eighteen hundred and twenty four
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Maria Anna T Greenfield (seal)</p>
         
         
         <p>Signed sealed published and declared by Maria Anna T Greenfield as and for her last
            Will and Testament in presence of us who at her request &amp; in her presence &amp; in the
            presence of each other have subscribed our names as witnesses thereto.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Clement Hollyday. Ann Swaine</p>
         
         
         <p>Prince Georges County towit October 19 1824</p>
         
         
         <p>Then came Clement Hollyday one of the subscribing witnesses to the aforegoing will
            and made oath on the Holy Evangely of Almighty God that he did see Maria Anna T Greenfield
            the testatrix therein named sign &amp; seal this will and heard her

            the time of her so doing she was to the best of his apprehension of sound and disposing
            mind memory and understanding and that he together with Ann Swaine the other subscribing
            witness respectively subscribed their names as witnesses to this will in the presence
            of the testatrix at her request and in the presence of each other
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Sworn before Trueman Tyler Regr Wills for Pr Geo Cty</p>
         
         
         <p>Maryland Prince Georges County Set</p>
         
         
         <p>I Philimon Chew Register of Wills do hereby certify that the foregoing is a true copy
            of the last will and Testament of Maria Anna T Greenfield duly proven filed &amp; recorded
            in my office.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>In Testimony whereof I have hereunto subscribed my name &amp; affixed the seal of the
            Orphans Court of Prince Georges County this 29th Octo 1838.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Phil Chew Regr Wills PGCty</p>
