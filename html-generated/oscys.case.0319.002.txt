Douglass <del>late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October Inst <del>next</del>, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom Mary Lucinda Someby by her next friend Samuel Someby &amp;c.</p>
