<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon George Miller Jr Late of Washington County if he shall be found within the county of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the first Monday of December next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the petition of freedom of Negro Ann Williams and children exhibited against him</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril; and have you then and there this Precept.</p>

<p>Witness William Cranch, Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 9th day of July 1831</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk </p>   

<p>K &amp; D</p>
