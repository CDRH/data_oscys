<p>To the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>
  
</div>

<div class="body ">
     
<p>The humble Petition of Susan Overton sheweth that your petitioner is detained in slavery by Collimore Pope Fielder Pope and Nelly Jenkins; and as she is entitled to his freedom having lineally descended in the female line from a free woman, she prays that process may be awarded to compel the appearance of the said Collimore Pope, Fielder Pope and Nelly Jenkins, to answer her complaint &amp;c.</p>
    
<p>E. M. Lowe<br></br>
for Petitioner</p>
    
</div>
