<p>District of Columbia....to wit:</p>

<p>The United States of America, To Electius Spalding</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside you be and
appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the
county of Washington, on first Monday of June
next, to answer <del>the bill of complaint</del> petition for freedom of James
Butler in the said court exhibited against you. Hereof
you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p> 

<p>Witness, Wm Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said
court, the 19 day of &gt;April 1815.</p>

<p>Issued the 31 day of May 1815.</p>
   
<p>Wm Brent Ck</p>

<p>FS Key</p>
