<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon John R. Ratcliffe late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and safely">and safely</del> keep, so that <del class="you">you</del> he be &amp; appear <del class="have bod">have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition for Letty Lucas for her freedom</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 12th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.</p>

<p>Issued the 13th day of Jany 1847.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Carlisle</p>
