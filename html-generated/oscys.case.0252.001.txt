<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br> 
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> William Mills late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3d Monday of October next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the petition for freedom of Primos Garner &amp;c.</p>
    
<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
    
<p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court at the City of Washington, the 13d day of May Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 51.</p>
    
<p>Issued the 24th day of May 1851.</p>
    
<p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>
    
<p>Morgan</p>
