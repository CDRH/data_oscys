         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Negro Anne of Washington County humbly sheweth that she is a free
            person &amp; unjustly held in bondage by a certain Elisha W. Williams of Washington County
            &amp; she therefore prays your Honors that as she is entitled to her freedom a subpoena
            may be issued to the said Elisha W. Williams commanding her to appear &amp; answer hereunto
            &amp; that your Honors will grant such relief in the premises to your Petitioner as to
            your Honors seem proper &amp; careful.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>
            <del class="J S Morsell">J S Morsell</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F S Key for Petr.</p>
