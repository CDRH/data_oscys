<div class="body ">

<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia</p>
     
<p>Your petitioner respectfully represents unto your Honors that she is free &amp; is unjustly detained in slavery by said defendant and prays that defendant be summoned to appear before your Honorable Court to make answer hereto and upon the hearing give judgment against defendant &amp; grant your petitioner such other &amp; further relief as your petitioner may appear to be entitled to &amp; your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>M. Blair for petitioner</p>
