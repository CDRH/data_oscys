         <p>Friday October 10'' 1806 Court met accordg to adjournment</p>
         
         
         <p>Present
            <br></br>
            The Honble William Cranch Esq chief Judge
            <br></br>
            Nicholas Fitzhugh &amp; Allen B. Duckett Esqs ass Judges
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Sabrett Scott appears in Court under a Subpoena issued for that Purpose &amp; Enters into
            the following Recognizance
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="caption ">
         
         
         <p>United States
            <br></br>
            vs
            <br></br>
            Sabrett Scott
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Recog in $600 conditioned that Scott does not remove Negro Ben out of this district,
            nor obstruct him from attending the Court from time to time in support of his Petition
            for Freedom Exhibited in this Court against said Scott &amp; in the mean time to feed,
            clothe &amp; use him well.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
