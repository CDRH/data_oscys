         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Easther Humphreys humbly sheweth that she is a free person &amp; is unjustly
            held in bondage by a certain John B. Lambert. Wherefore she prays your Honors to grant
            her the United States writ of subpoena to the said Defendant Lambert directed, that
            he may be compelled to appear before your Honors &amp; answer hereto &amp; that your Petitioner
            may be discharged &amp; obtain such relief as to your Honors shall 
            <del class="shal">shal</del> seem right.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr.</p>
