      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Honorable the judges of the Circuit Court for the District of Columbia in the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of Patty respectfully sheweth that she is entitled to her freedom, but is held in slavery by Charles Ratcliffe administrator of Benjamin R. Davis deceased, &amp; the heirs of Benjamin R. Davis, contrary to law Wherefore she prays, that she may be <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> from said slavery &amp; bondage of said Charles Ratcliffe administrator as afsd. &amp; the heirs &amp; Representatives of said Benjamin R. Davis, and that a subpoena may issue to said Charles Ratcliffe admr. as afsd. &amp; the other Representatives of said B. R. Davis decd. and your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>E. B. Caldwell atty for Petr.</p>

      </div>
