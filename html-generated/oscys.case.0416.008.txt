         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of The District of Columbia, Greeting.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you, That you summon Ann Spalding, Enoch Campbell, and William B. Chapple to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, <del>on the</del> immediately <del>day of Court which commences on the Monday</del> to testify, and the truth to say, on behalf of Daniel Wells in a certain matter of controversy, in our said court depending and undetermined, between Daniel Wells &amp; Benja. F. Lewis and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cts. And have then there this writ. Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 27 day of Novemr in the year of our Lord 1824.</p>

         <p>Edm. J. Lee. C.C.</p>

         <p>Henry Pittman, print...Alexandria.</p>
