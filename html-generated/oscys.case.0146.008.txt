      <div class="caption ">

         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you take Wm Glover late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body that he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of November next, to answer unto The Petition of Jack Rhodes &amp; Kitty Green exhibited in said Court against him for freedom</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness, Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 5th day of August Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 39</p>

         <p>Issued the 13th day of Augt 1839</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Key</p>
