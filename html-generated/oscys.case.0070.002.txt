         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of negro William humbly sheweth that your petitioner is entitled to his
            freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain         Alford and John Coburn
            &amp; George Milburn or by one of them, who has purchased your Petitioner &amp; unlawfully
            holds him in their or his possession, claiming to hold him as a slave.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Your Petitioner therefore prays that he may receive legal &amp; proper relief as your
            Honors may think right. and that the United States writ of subpona may be issued to
            the said Defendants directed commanding them to be &amp; appear before your Honors to
            
            <span class="torn page">[answer?]</span> hereunto. And your Petitioner further States that the said Defendants who now hold
            your petitioner is immediately preparing to remove him out of the reach of this Court
            to a distant state, where he will be unable to assert &amp; substantiate his claim. he
            therefore prays that the subpona maybe made immediately returnable before you Honors
            and as your Petitioner is now present 
            <span class="torn page">[torn page]</span> your Honors in Court, where he hath come for refuge &amp; protection from the attempts
            of Deft so to take him off &amp; remove him, he prays that his case may be summarily enquired
            into, upon the appearing of the Deft and that he may 
            <del class="require that petitioner">require that petitioner</del> be (discharged)
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/thumbnails/p002.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            discharged from the Deft's possession &amp; receive such protection &amp; relief to which
            he may be lawfully entitled.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FSKey for Petr</p>
         
         
         <p>On this 26 May '26 personally appeared in open Court the within named negro William
            the petitioner herein named &amp; made oath in due form that the facts &amp; circumstances
            herein stated are true to the best of his knowledge &amp; belief.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Sworn to in open court
            <br></br>
            W. Brent Clk
