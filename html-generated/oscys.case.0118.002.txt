         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Charles Botts late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep, so that you</del> that he be and appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> The Petition of Eliza Evans and son exhibited at the said Court here for freedom.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 39</p>

         <p>Issued the 25 day of Janury 1839</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Ck</p>

         <p>Bradley</p>
