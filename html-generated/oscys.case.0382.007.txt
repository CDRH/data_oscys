      <div class="body">

         <p>Be it remembered that one this 4th Day of March 1814. Personally Came before me the undersigned Justice of Peace for said County, Zacha. Neale late of the County of Prince George in the State of Maryland, who removed from thence into the County Aforesaid within the last sixty Days, bringing with him the Five following Slaves Vizt. Negroe Jim aged about 25 years, Esther about 50 years, Nace about 14 years, Cloe about 20 years, and Mary about 4 years all his own Property, and the said Zachariah did then and there before me the said Justice, take the Oath prescribed and set forth in the fourth Section of the Statute Intitled an "Act to reduce into one the Several Acts Concerning Slaves free Negroes &amp; Mulattoes</p>

         <p>Given under my Hand &amp; Seal this 4th Day of March 1814 as aforesaid</p>

         <p>A Faw (Seal)</p>
