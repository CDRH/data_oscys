         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Martha A. Scott if she shall be found in County
            of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the
            
            <del class="first">first</del> third Monday of October Inst. 
            <del class="next">next</del>, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> petition of freedom of Lucy Crawford filed in Court here against her 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 18th day of October 1845.</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent c</p>
         
         
         <p>(Hellen)</p>
         
         
         <p>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on
            which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.
         </p>
