<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit court of the United States for the district of Columbia &amp; county of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
     
<p>Your Petitioner Peter Thomas respectfully represents, that your Petitioner is unlawfully detained in slavery by Christiana Hamilton &amp; that is at law entitled to his freedom in as much as he is descended from a free white woman. He therefore prays that your Honors would take his case unto consideration &amp; direct a subpoena to be issued to the said Christiana &amp; such proceedings to be had, as the law has provided for the trial of his claim &amp; the enforcement of his right to freedom.</p>
    
<p>And your petitioner will forever pray &amp;c.</p>
    
<p>John Law.<br></br>
Atty for Petr.</p>
