<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Robert H. Perkins if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, <del class="at rules">at rules</del> to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of March next, to answer the <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of William Lewis filed in Court here against him <del class="and others.">and others.</del></p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 20th day of Decr 1845.</p>

<p>W. Brent Ck</p>

<p>Rd Wallach</p>
