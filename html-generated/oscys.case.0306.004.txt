         <p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>George Gray of the County aforesaid file this his Petition against Stephen Howison administrator of all &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> the Goods &amp; chattels, rights &amp; credits of Mary Ann Potts decd &amp; represents that your Petitioner is now held in slavery by the said Stephen Howison administrator for as aforesaid &amp; that in fact your Petitioner is by law entitled to his freedom &amp; respectfully prays your Honors to grant him the usual subpoena &amp;c. &amp; to hear &amp; determine this his petition &amp; your Petitioner will ever pay &amp;c.</p>
    
         <p>Chas S Wallach<br/>
for Petitioner</p>
