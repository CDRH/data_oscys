<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Henry Robertson late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you have bod</del> he be and appear <del>before</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Sally Robertson exhibited in the said Court against him for her freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 22d day of January Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

<p>Issued the 3d day of February 1838.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Bradley.</p>
