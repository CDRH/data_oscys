<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the Dist. of Cola. for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Milley humbly sheweth That she is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held and claimed as a Slave be a certain <del>Samuel</del> Thomas Massey of this County &amp; District. Wherefore she prays the United States writ of subpoena to the said <del>Samuel</del> Thomas Massey directed, that he may be compelled to be and appear before your Honors &amp; answer hereunto. So that your Petitioner may be discharged &amp; obtain such other &amp; further relief as to your Honors shall seem right.</p>

<p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petrs:</p>     
     
</div>
