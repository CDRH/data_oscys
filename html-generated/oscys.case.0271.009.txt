         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia in the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of Nace Humphreys by Dolly Humphreys his next friend respectfully sheweth, That said Nace Humphreys is entitled to his freedom, but is held in slavery as a slave for life by John Boswell. Your petitioner therefore prays, that he may be released from slavery, and that a supa. may issue to said John Boswell commanding him <span class="unclear illegible">[&amp;c?]</span> in the usual form. and your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Caldwell for Petr</p>
