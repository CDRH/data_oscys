         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington the Petition of Negro Cato humbly sheweth</p>

         <p>That your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom but is nevertheless held &amp; detained in bondage as a slave by a certain Thomas Offutt Junior; and your Petitioner therefore prays a subpoena to be issued directed to the said Thomas Offutt Junior commanding to appear &amp; answer to your Petitioner in the premisses, and that your honours will grand him such other relief as may seem just &amp; right; and your Petitioner &amp;c.</p>

         <p>F.S. Key for Petitioner</p>
