         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Your Petitioners Adam Wigle Rachel Wigle, Nace Johnson, Nancy Johnson, William Forrest
            and Harry Wigle, humbly sheweth unto your Honors that they are justly entitled to
            their freedom under the 
            <del class="w">w</del> last will of their late Master John B Kirby decd and that they are unjustly held
            in bondage by George Kirby executor of said John B Kirby
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Therefore they pray your Honors to take their case into consideration and grant them
            such relief in the premises as to your Honors may seem just;
         </p>
         
         
         <p>And your petitioners will ever pray &amp;c.</p>
         
         
         <p>Coxe &amp; Smith attys for Petitioner
            <br></br>
            Jany 15th 1829
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
