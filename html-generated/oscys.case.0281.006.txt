<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Sexsmith if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, <del>at rules to be held for the County of Washington,</del> at the City of Washington, on the <del>first</del> fourth Monday of March next, to answer to <del>bill of complaint of</del> Answer the petition of freedom of Milly Henderson filed in Court here against him <del>and others.</del></p>  

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 28th day of December 18<del>4</del>54.</p>

<p>Jno A. Smith Clk</p>

<p>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.</p>
