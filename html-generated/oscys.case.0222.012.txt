<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you take Isaac Buckingham late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto William. H McKenney <del class="in a plea of trespass on the case and so forth">in a plea of trespass on the case and so forth</del> on his petition for freedom</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said court, at the City of Washington, the 22 day of May Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 52</p>

<p>Issued the 17 day of July. 1852.</p>

<p>John A. Smith Clk.</p>

<p>(S. &amp; S.)</p>
