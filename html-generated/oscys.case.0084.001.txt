<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the
District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Christopher Harris humbly sheweth that your
Petitioner is now illegally held in bondage by one Penelope B. Alexander who claims your petitioner as his slave. That your petitioner is entitled to his freedom and prays your Honors to grant unto him the U.S. writ of subpona to said Penelope directed &amp;c &amp;c</p>

<p>Richd Wallach for Petitioner</p>

</div>
