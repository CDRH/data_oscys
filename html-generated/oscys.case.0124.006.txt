<div class="body ">

<p>The above named Henry B. Robertson one of the Defendants to the above petition for freedom comes here into Court in proper person this 24th day of April 1845 &amp; disclaims &amp; renounces all claim to hold the said petitioners in slavery, &amp; as he has no interest in this cause he prays the Court to have his name stricken out as Defendant.</p>

<p>H B Robertson</p>
