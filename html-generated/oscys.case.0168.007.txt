         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Francis Bowling &amp; Clarissa B Gardner Administrators of Richard B Gardner late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and th safely keep,</del> so that <del>you have</del> be &amp; appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the Fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Margaret Norah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins their friend for their freedom</p>
