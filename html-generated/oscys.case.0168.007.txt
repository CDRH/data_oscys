<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Francis Bowling &amp; Clarissa B Gardner Administrators of Richard B Gardner late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and th safely keep,">and th safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you have">you have</del> be &amp; appear <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the Fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition of Margaret Norah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins their friend for their freedom</p>
