         <p>In the case of negroe Ben and Sabrett Scott</p>

         <p>It is <del>agreed by the parties</del> by consent of parties ordered by the Court that untill the decision of the writ of error in the Supreme Court, <del>w</del> S. Scott may hire Ben to any person, approved of by F. S. Key Esq, to be employed by the person so hiring him, to go to any part of the <del>United States,</del> 
            <del>out of the</del> World, by Land or Water, and upon Ben's being so carried out of the Dist. of Columbia, in the service of any person so hiring him, it shall not be a breach or forfeiture of the rocognizance entered into by the said Scott.</p>

         <p>F S Key<br/>
W Jones Jr.</p>
