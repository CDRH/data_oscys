         <p>In the case of negroe Ben and Sabrett Scott</p>
         
         
         <p>It is 
            <del class="agreed by the parties">agreed by the parties</del> by consent of parties ordered by the Court that untill the decision of the writ of
            error in the Supreme Court, 
            <del class="w">w</del> S. Scott may hire Ben to any person, approved of by F. S. Key Esq, to be employed
            by the person so hiring him, to go to any part of the 
            <del class="United States,">United States,</del> 
            <del class="out of the">out of the</del> World, by Land or Water, and upon Ben's being so carried out of the Dist. of Columbia,
            in the service of any person so hiring him, it shall not be a breach or forfeiture
            of the rocognizance entered into by the said Scott.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F S Key
            <br></br>
            W Jones Jr.
