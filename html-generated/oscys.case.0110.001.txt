         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon R. Harbin if he shall be found in the County of
            Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the
            
            <del class="first">first</del> 3d Monday of October next, to answer the
