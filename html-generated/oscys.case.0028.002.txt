         <p>District of Columbia....to wit</p>

         <p>The United States of America, To Henry W. Ball</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be 
and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to
be held for the county of Washington, <del>on the</del>
first Monday of
June next, to answer the <del>bill of complaint</del> Pettition for Freedom of Sally Henry by William Henry her father &amp; next friend in the said court exhibited against you.</p>
                
         <p>Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p> 
                
         <p>Witness, William Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the 30th day of Jany 18<del>0</del>13</p>
                
         <p>Issued the 23rd day of March 18<del>0</del>13</p>
                
         <p>Wm. Brent Cl</p>
                
         <p>FSKey</p>
