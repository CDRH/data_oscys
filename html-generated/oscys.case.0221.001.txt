         <p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of William Crowner humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is a free man
            born of a free white woman &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain George B. Scott
            of Washington County.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore he prays that sbpa may be issued to said Scott, &amp; that he may be compelled
            to appear &amp; answer hereto, &amp; that he may be discharged from his service &amp; adjudged
            free according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FS Key for Petr.</p>
