<p>To the Hon. Judges of the Circuit Court of the U. States, of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of <del>Nancy</del> Basil Fletcher and Nancy Fletcher his wife, and of Henrietta Fletcher a minor by her next of kin; the said Basil all of Washington Co. Dist of Columbia represents that the said Nancy and the said Henrietta are illegally and unjustly held in slavery by Henry T. Weightman Yeoman, late of said County of Washington, who <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> to claim said Nancy and Henrietta as his slaves and property, when in fact they are free by the Laws of the United States in such cases <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> and <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> Your Petitioners therefore pray yr Honors that the said Henry T. Weightman may be summoned to appear as the Law directs and that your Petitioners Nancy and Henrietta may be discharged from his illegal detention and declared to be free and as in duty bound they will ever pray &amp;c. and further that their case be tried by a Jury.</p>

<p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioner</p>

</div>
