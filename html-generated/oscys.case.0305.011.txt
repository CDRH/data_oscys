      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Charles Bell humbly sheweth that he is claimed to be held in slavery by a certain Luther Jones, that your Petitioner is a freeman and he prays process against the said Jones, and that his right to freedom may be inquired of by the Country.</p>     

         <p>Bradley &amp; Bradley<br/>
for petitioner.</p>

         <p>True Copy</p>

         <p>Test.<br/>
Jno. A Smith Clerk.</p>
