<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Mary Jackson for herself &amp; her two infant children Samuel &amp; Billey, humbly sheweth that she and her children have been seized &amp; are held in slavery by a certain Benjamin Davis and other persons &amp; her unknown, and she and her said children is free, and she prays process against said Davis &amp; that she be not carried out of this District.</p>

<p>Bradley &amp; Bradley<br/>
for petitioners</p>
