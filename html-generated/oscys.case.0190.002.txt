      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Nelson Conrad &amp; Benjamin Homans if they shall be
            found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) they be and appear
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of
            Washington, in the City of Washington, on fourth Monday of March next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition for freedom of Olivia Lee &amp; her infant John Lee and of Betsey
            Lee for herself and her children Milly Lee, Charles Lee, William Lee, George Lee,
            &amp; Caroline Lee, filed in said Court against them.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court. </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 30th day of Jany 1841.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>B &amp; B.</p>
