<p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

<p>The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of
Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon
Henry Roberts
if he shall be found in the county of
Washington,
that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit
Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the
county of Washington, in
the city of Washington, on the third Monday of December next, to <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>
answer the petition of freedom of James
Humphreys by Dolly Humphreys his next friend</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 

<p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 10 day of August 1824</p>

<p>W. Brent Clk</p>

<p>Caldwell</p>
