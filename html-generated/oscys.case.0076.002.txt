      <div class="caption ">
  
         <p>District of Columbia County of Washington set</p>

         <p>The United States of America To Tench Ringgold Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>Whereas John Lee, Lizettee Lee, Janett Lee hereto filed <del>by</del> their petition <del>filed</del> against a certain Augustus Preuss in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington and for certain consideration therein setforth you <del>soforth</del> are hereby strictly prohibited and injoined from suffering the said petitioners or either of them to be taken from the prison of the County of Washington now under your charge and you are directed to retain them in your custody until the further order of this Court in the premises. Hereof fail not as you will answer the contrary at your peril</p>

         <p>Witness W Cranch Esqr Chief Judge of our said Court</p>

         <p>Issued the 25th April 1827</p>

         <p>W. Brent Clk</p>

      </div>
