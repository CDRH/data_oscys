         <p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>Harriet Humphreys by Matilda Butler her next friend files this her petition &amp; respectfully represents unto your Honors that one Robert W Hunter now holds &amp; unjustly detains your petitioner in bondage as a slave and your petitioner further represents to your Honors that she is in fact &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> a free negro woman &amp; entittled to her <span class="unclear illegible">uncontrolled[?]</span> freedom and she prays your Honors to grant her the usual subpoena commanding the said Robert W Hunter to be &amp; appear before your Honors &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> &amp;c &amp; hear &amp; determine her case according to law.</p>

         <p>Chas S Wallach<br/>
for Petitioner</p>

      </div>
