      <div class="caption"> 

         <p>Negro Daniel v. Ann Kincheloe</p>

      </div>

      <div class="syllabus"> 

         <p>From a power to hire out a slave, and receive his wages, the jury cannot infer a power to sell him.</p>

         <p>If the importation of a slave into this county be with intent that he should be hired out for a limited time only, it is not such an importation as is forbidden by the <span class="bibl code">Maryland Act of 1796, c. 67 § 1</span>.</p>

      </div>
