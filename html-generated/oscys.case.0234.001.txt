         <p>To the Judge of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Lucind Anderson respectfully represents to your Honors that she is
            entitled to her freedom and that she is illegally detained &amp; held in bondage by Jonathan
            Magarity.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>She therefore prays your Honors to enquire into the matter and afford such other relief
            as your Honors may seem proper..
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Danl Ratcliff
            <br></br>
            Atty for petitioner
         </p>
