         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon James M. A. Mushett to be <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition of freedom of Mary, Henry, Emily &amp; Ida Taylor by their next friend Sandy Alexander filed in Court here against him</p>
