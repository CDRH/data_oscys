<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington. The Petition of negro Sally (an infant by William her father &amp; next friend) humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Henry W Ball who holds &amp; claims your Petitioner as a slave.</p>
                
<p>Wherefor your Petitioner prays that a subpona may be issued directed to the said Henry Ball that he may appear &amp; answer unto the premisses, &amp; your said claim to freedom &amp; be allowed all such other &amp; futher relief in the premisses as may be agreeable to law &amp; may seem right to your Honors.—</p>
                
<p>FSKey for Petr</p>
