         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.-</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Sylvia Lee humbly sheweth that she is a free person unlawfully held in bondage by one Elizabeth Smallwood, who claims your Petitioner as a slave. Wherefore she prays that sbpa may issue to said Elizabeth that she may appear &amp; answer hereunto according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr.</p>
