To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon James Knox <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 1st Monday of May next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Samuel Stacey</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
