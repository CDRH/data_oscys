<p>District of Columbia, To wit:</p>

<p>The United States of America,</p>

<p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon
Henry Talbert if he shall be found in the county of
Washington,
that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit
Court of the District of Columbia, <del>to be held</del> for the
county of Washington, in
the city of Washington, on the <del>first</del> <del>day of</del> 
immediately <del>monday of December</del> <del>next,</del> to <del>testify on the part of</del>
answer the Petition for freedom of Henry Ober, William Ober, Emily Ober, Francis Washington Ober, Thomas Ober and David Ober, in said Court exhibited against him</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril ; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 

<p>Witness Wm. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 3rd day of June 1828</p>

<p>Wm Brent, Clk</p>

<p>Beale</p>
