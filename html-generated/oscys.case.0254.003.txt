      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>The Petition of Mary Jane Wayne, &amp; Joseph C. William R. Daniel &amp; Harriet Wayne her children - respectfully represent unto your honors that your petitioners are entitled to their freedom; that they are unjustly held in bondage by one William C. Greenleaf Guardian of of children of John L. Brightwell and pray that a subpoena may issue to the said William C. Greenleaf, Guardian &amp;c. and their rights enquired into by your Honors</p> 

         <p>Jos. H. Bradley<br/>
for Petrs</p>
