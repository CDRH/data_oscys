         <p>District of Columbia....to wit, 
            <br></br>
            The United States of America, To Henry Moscross
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United
            States for the District of Columbia in the
            County of Washington. The Petition of negro
            John and negress Serena infants under
            the age of twenty one years by John Stevens their next
            friend, humbly complaining, sheweth unto your honors, that they are unjustly
            &amp; illegally held in slavery by Henry Moscross, contrary
            to law &amp; against justice Equity &amp; good conscience. Your petitioners
            therefore pray, that by the judgment order or Decree of this Honorable Court
            they may be discharged from the bondage or servitude of the said Henry
            Moscross, and may be 
            <span class="illegible">[returned?]</span> to that freedom to which in 
            <del class="law&#xA;                                    justly">law
               justly
            </del> law &amp; Equity they are justly entitled. And may
            it please your honors to grant unto your petitioners a summons as in such cases
            is usual directing the said Henry Moscross to appear in
            this hnorable &amp; to enter into such recognizance, &amp; with such security as
            in cases of petition for freedom 
            <del class="is">is</del> by law established &amp; as to
            your honors shall seem meet &amp; proper, &amp; to grant to your petitioners such
            further &amp; other relief in the premises as to your honors shall seem meet &amp;
            according to justice Equity &amp; good conscience. &amp; your petitioners will
            ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Negro John
            <br></br>
            Negress Serena
            <br></br>
            By John Stevens their next friend
         </p>
         
         
         <p>EB Caldwell att for Petitioners</p>
         
         
         <p>May 6th 1811</p>
