         <p>Upon hearing the motion for an attachment made in this cause by the 
            <del class="counsel for">counsel for</del> council for the petitioner, it is this 7th day of April 1835 ordered, that unless
            the said Henry Howison, who has been duly summoned to answer the said petition shall
            on or before the 13th day of April instant appear in this Court and produce before
            the Court the body of the said petitioner or enter with recognizance in the usual
            form with good security in the sum of four hundred dollars with the usual condition
            to have the said petitioner in this Court 
            <del class="at the tri">at the tri</del> to attend the trial &amp;c. The attachment be issued, in the usual form against the said
            Henry Howison.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>By order of the Court</p>
         
         
         <p>Test W. Brent 
            7 Aprl. 1835
         </p>
