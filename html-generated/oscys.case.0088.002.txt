      <div class="body">

         <p>William Smith duly sworn, maketh oath that the petitioners above named are in danger of being taken off and sold out of their county by Mrs Gibson who sets up a claim to the said petitioners as her slaves. That the said Mrs Gibson has offered a reward for the apprehension of the said petitioners. And he the said Smith further states that he is a subscribing witness to the deed of manumission under with the petitioners claim their freedom.</p>

         <p>Sworn <del>to before</del> in open Court<br/>
8th Dec 1827<br/>
Wm Brent Clk</p>
