<p>State of Virginia Fairfax County Set</p>

<p>By consent of parties by their Attorney's. In a suit pending between Negroes Sam and Edmond and the Heirs of Gabriel D. Childs, The subscriber a Justice of the Peace for Fairfax County Virginia proceeded to take the depositions of Jane St Clare Emily Ager &amp; Ann Ager at the House of said J. St Clare on the fourth day of January eighteen hundred and thirty two. The above named Jane St Clare Emily Ager &amp; Ann Ager (having been first sworn upon the Holy Evangelists) Say upon oath, that the Answers given to each and several the Interrogatories propounded by the parties is the whole truth so far as they know and believe.</p>

<p>Given under my hand and seal the day and state above stated.</p>

<p>Mottrom Ball (S.S)</p>
