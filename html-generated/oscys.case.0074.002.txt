         <p>District of Columbia to wit:</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America,</p>
         
         
         <p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Augustus Preuss
            if he shall be found within the county of
            Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city
            of Washington, on the First Monday of
            May next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the petition for freedom of Nancy Lee by Lizette Lee her mother &amp; next friend,
            filed in said Court against him
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril: and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 8th day of March 1827</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent, Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Beale</p>
