      <div class="caption">

         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Aikin if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of John Lee and Ann Maria Lee <del>filed</del> exhibited in the said Court against him</p>

         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness, Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 23d day of August 1839.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Wallach</p>

      </div>
