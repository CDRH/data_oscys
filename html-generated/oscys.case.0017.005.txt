         <p>Letitia Davis v Rebecca Forrest and Anne Davis &amp; others v Charles Minifee</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>At the trial of these causes</p>

         <p>The Petitioners council offered to read a record of recovery in the case of <span class="bibl case">Susan Davis v Caleb Swan</span>, which record is hereto annexed; and the Petitioner's council offered evidence to prove that the said Susan Davis in the said record <span class="unclear illegible">[mentioned?]</span>, is the sister of the Petitioner; and the Deft's council objected to the admission of said record as evidence in this cause; But the court overruled the objection and admitted the said record in evidence; To which the Defts except &amp; pray this their Bill of Exceptions may be sealed &amp; inrolled &amp; it is done accordingly.</p>
                
         <p>N Fitzhugh (Seal)<br/>
B. Thruston (Seal)</p>
