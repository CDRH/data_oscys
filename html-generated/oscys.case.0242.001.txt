         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Susan Armistead is she shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia,
            at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the
            
            <del class="first">first</del> 3d Monday of October next, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Harriet Bell by her next friend Daniel Bell filed in Court
            here against her 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
