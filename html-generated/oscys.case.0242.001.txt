         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,</p>

         <p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Susan Armistead is she shall be found in the County of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del>first</del> 3d Monday of October next, to answer the <del>bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Harriet Bell by her next friend Daniel Bell filed in Court here against her <del>and others.</del>
         </p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the first day of July 1846.</p>

         <p>W. Brent Ck</p>

         <p>Carlisle</p>

         <p>
            <del>Memorandum: The Defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confess</del>
