      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon
            Janet Lingan
            if she shall be found in the county of
            Washington,
            that (all excuses set aside) she be and appear before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the
            county of Washington, in
            the city of Washington, on the Second Monday of
            April next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>
            answer the Petition of Freedom of Richard Lucket
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 11th day of November 1823</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>(Taney)</p>
