         <p>Memorandum of Twelve Negroes which Arrived at Second Creek the 23rd October 1794. the property of Joseph Slater. (Viz)</p>

         <p>Thomas born in 1765<br/>
Florah Do. Do. 1772<br/>
Daphne Do. 1789<br/>
Hagar Do. 1791<br/>
Ben Do. 1794<br/>
Cyrus Do. 1781<br/>
Polly Do. 1776<br/>
Joe Do. 1785.<br/>
Jenny Do. 1768<br/>
Sophiah Do. 1788<br/>
Daphne Do. 1793<br/>
Dick Do. 1757</p>

         <p>Novr. 17th 1794<br/>
This day Joseph Slater came before me William Haynes a Justice for Greenbrier County and made Oath that the Within is a True list of the Negroes Brot By him from the State of Maryland into Virginia Greenbrier County<br/>
Will Haynes</p>

         <p>February Greenbrier Court 1795.<br/>
This list of slaves of Joseph Slater Gent. proved before Wm Haynes Gent &amp; Lately Brought in his Family from the State of Maryland was presented in Court &amp; Ordered to be Recorded.</p>
 
         <p>Test<br/>
John Stuart Cy C</p>

         <p>A Copy from Record</p>

         <p>Test<br/>
Lewis Stuart Cy C</p>
