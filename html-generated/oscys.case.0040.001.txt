      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District
of Columbia the petition of James Butler Humbly Sheweth.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>That your Petitioner a person of Colour is of right entitled to his freedom and
yet nevertheless is held and unjustly detained in slavery by a certain
Electius Spalding of Washington
County aforesaid. Wherefore your Petitioner humbly prays your
Honours to Grant that the United States Writ of Subpoena
may issue to the said Electius Spalding directed,
commanding him to be and appear before your Honours, to answer your petitioner
in the premises and that your Honours would grant him such other and further
relief as the nature of his case may seem to require &amp; your Petitioner will
ever pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>FS Key for Petitioner</p>

      </div>
