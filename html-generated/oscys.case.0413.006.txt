      <div class="caption">

         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you, That you summon Rebecca Hattersby, Aaron Musgrove and Peter Hewitt to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the 4th (Monday the 29th inst) day of the Court, which commences on the first Monday in this month to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Cleary Moore in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined between said Cleary Moore &amp; Thomas Jacobs and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents—And have then there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 5th day of October in the year of our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and thirty two.</p>

         <p>Edm: J. Lee CC</p>
