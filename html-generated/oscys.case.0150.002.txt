         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br> 
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Roberta Slye if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on the fourth day of November next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the petition for freedom of Joe Crawford filed in said court against him.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 4th day of Sept 1833.</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Wallach</p>
