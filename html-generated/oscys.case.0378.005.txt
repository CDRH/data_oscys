         <p>Negroe Chiny<br/>
vs<br/>
Benja: Patterson</p>

         <p>Charge agt. defendant holding Complainant in Slavery Contrary to Law</p>

         <p>Alexandria County sst</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>Thos: Dockins, John Smith and Leond: Cook sumd. as Witnessess for defendant</p>

         <p>John Smith and Leonard Cook being Sworn According to Law. That they have known Negroe Chiny in the service of the said Patterson as a Slave in the Town of Alexandria for two or three years, and that they understood she was brought from Maryland as a Slave</p>

         <p>The said Patterson admitted that she was a Slave to his Father in Maryland and that she was not entered and recorded According to Law</p>

         <p>Given under my Hand this 22d Day of December 1809<br/>
A Faw</p>
