         <p>District of Columbia....to wit,<br/>
The United States of America, To Christiana Hamilton</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded, that, all excuses and delays set aside, you be and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, on the first Monday of June next, to answer the <del>bill of complaint</del> petition for freedom of Peter Thomas in the said court exhibited against you.</p>

         <p>Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p>
    
         <p>Witness, William  Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the 25. day of Jany. 18<del>0</del>10.</p>
    
         <p>Issued the 25. day of Jany 18<del>0</del>10.</p>
    
         <p>Wm. Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Law</p>
