         <p>To the honourable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of William Kennedy sometimes called William Condy represents that he is held in slavery by a certain I Purnell Pendleton of the State of Virginia, whereas he avers he is entitled to his freedom under the laws in force in the District of Columbia &amp;c Wherefore he prays that process of subpoena may issue to said I. Purnell Pendleton commanding &amp;c. &amp; such other &amp; further relief in the premises as may seem just &amp; proper</p>

         <p>William Kennedy</p>
