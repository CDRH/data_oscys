The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon William Glover late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep, so</del> that <del>you</del> he be and appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Jack Rhodes by Kitty Green his next friend filed in the said Court against him.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 13th day of July Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 37.</p>

<p>Issued the 21st day of July 1837</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Key.</p>
