         <p>Court Room
            <br></br>
            22 Feby 33.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Dr Sir,</p>
         
         
         <p>I send a petition, which the bearer, will swear to, or Smoot if necessary. I will
            thank you to see who has committed the negro &amp; advertizes him, &amp; fill it up in the
            blank in the petition. &amp; shew the woman to Judge Cranch.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>If her affidavit will not do, send her to Smoot &amp; let him swear to it &amp; send it to
            the Judge.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Yrs
            <br></br>
            F S Key
         </p>
         
         
      </div>

         <p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia sitting for
            the County of Wash.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Negro Noah humbly sheweth to your Honors that he is entitled to his
            freedom and that he is now unjustly held in Servitude by a certain John H. Riley.
            Wherefore he prays your Honors to grant him such relief as he is entitled under the
            law of the land
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F. S. Key for Petnr.</p>
