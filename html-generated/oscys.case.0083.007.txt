         <p>Mayors Office Washington City District Columbia 28th April 1827</p>
         
         
         <p>To John Battaile</p>
         
         
         <p>Take notice that I shall on Tuesday next the first day of May 1827 at 12 Oclock AM
            at the Office of the Mayor of the City of Washington in the City Hall proceed to take
            the deposition of Charles A Stoker who is about to go out of the District of Columbia
            to a greater distance than one hundred miles, to be read in evidence for Thomas Miller
            in a cause of petition of freedom in which you John Battaile are the petitioner against
            said Thomas Miller pending in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington at which time &amp; place you are 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> to be present or appear by counsel at the taking of the same &amp; to put interrogatories
            if you or your attorney shall think fit
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Roger C Weightman
            <br></br>Mayor
         </p>
