         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Nancy Jones a coloured woman humbly complaining sheweth unto your
            Honors that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly held in bondage
            by Alexander Hunter marshal of the District of Columbia aforesaid who claims and holds
            your Petitioner as a slave
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to the said Alexander
            Hunter directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law
         </p>
         
         
         <p>John J Dermott atty for Petitioner</p>
         
         
      </div>
