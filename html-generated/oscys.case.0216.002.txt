         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Jno. W. Williams Mary S Williams &amp; William S Colquhoun
            if they shall be found in the County of Washington, that they be and appear in the
            Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington,
            at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of Nov next, to answer the 
            <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Charles Blue filed in Court here against them 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 3d day of June 1843.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Ck</p>
