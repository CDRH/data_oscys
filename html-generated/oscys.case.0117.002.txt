            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br> 
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon John Stephenson if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on fourth Monday of November next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition for Freedom of Daniel Bell filed in the said Court against him
            and so forth.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 24th day of September 1835.</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm. Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Dermott</p>
