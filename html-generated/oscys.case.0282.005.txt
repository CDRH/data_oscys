         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> sum: Georgiana Munroe <del>late of Washington County, if she shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and her safely keep, so that you have her body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer unto Sarah H. Medula &amp; others. their petition for freedom &amp;c.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 31st day of May Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 54.</p>

         <p>Issued the 7th day of Aug: 1854</p>

         <p>Jno. A. Smith Clk</p>

         <p>Carlisle</p>
