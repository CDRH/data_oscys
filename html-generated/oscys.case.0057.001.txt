      <div class="caption ">
         
         
         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court for the County of Washington</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Dennis Wright, now in the City of Washington,
            humbly sheweth unto your Honours that your petitioner conceives himself entitled
            to his freedom under the Act of Assembly of Maryland 1796 · ch. 67 ·
            s. 1. Your petitioner humbly sheweth that he was born in the
            State of Virginia; the slave of a certain Mrs
            Sarah Triplett.
         </p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
               <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.002.jpg" class="display"></img>
            </a>
         </span>
         
         
         <p>That about the year 1800 your petitioner was removed by Mrs Sarah
            Triplett, his Mistress &amp; owner into the County of
            Washington District of Columbia,
            contrary to the said Act of Assembly of 1796 · ch. 67 ·
            s. 1. That your petitioner was not brought to the said County &amp;
            District under any of the provisions or exceptions contained in the said
            Act or the suppliments thereto, 
            <span class="illegible">[showing?]</span> the rights of the said Mrs Sarah
            Triplett would have been 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span>  to her. That your petitioner
            was sold about the year 1811 by the said
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.003.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.003.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.003.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            Mrs Sarah Triplett unto a certain Samuel
            Robinson &amp; was by the said Samuel Robinson
            removed from the 
            <del class="State of">State of</del> Dt. Cola. to the
            State of Virginia &amp; there remained with occasional
            absences from the State, until within the last year the said Samuel
            Robinson died— That if your petitioner had not claim under
            the Maryland Act referred to, he thinks it likely he should have claim under his
            removal from the Dt. Cola. to Virga.
            and he therefore does not hereby wave or abandon any
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.004.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.004.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.004.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            such claim but under this petition prays to have all his pritinsions
            heard—
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Since the death of the said Samuel Robinson your petitioner
            has been held &amp; hired a part of the time by Mrs Eliza
            Robinson the widow of the said Samuel
            Robinson &amp; a part of the time by a certain
            Richard Taylor who your petitioner is informed claims your
            petitioner under some mortgage from the late Samuel
            Robinson to him the said Richard
            Taylor—
         </p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.005.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.005.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
               <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.005.jpg" class="display"></img>
            </a>
         </span>
         
         
         <p>Your petitioner prays your Honors that Subpoenas may issue for the said
            Eliza Robinson and the said Richard
            Taylor &amp; that your petitioner may have a trial according to
            law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Augt Taney for Petitioner</p>
         
         
         <p>Feby 8. 1822.</p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.006.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.006.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
               <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.006.jpg" class="display"></img>
            </a>
         </span>
         
         
         <p>On this 9th day of February AD 1822 personally
            appears Dennis Wright, the petitioner named above &amp;
            maketh oath &amp; saith that he verily believes the said Mrs Sarah
            Triplett removed from the State of Virga.
            &amp; resided in the County of Washington for about
            
            <span class="illegible">[three?]</span> years 
            <span class="illegible">[but?]</span> she removed
            your petitioner from the
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.007.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.007.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.007.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            State of Virga. into the said County— That your
            petitioner cannot exactly remember years nor dates &amp; cannot therefore state
            them as he would wish— but is clear
            
            
            <span class="hr"> </span>
            <span class="pageimage">
               <a href="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.008.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/large/oscys.case.0057.001.008.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
                  <img src="http://jetson.unl.edu/oscys_figures/thumbnails/oscys.case.0057.001.008.jpg" class="display"></img>
               </a>
            </span>
            
            in his belief &amp; recollection of the fact stated in this Affidavit.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Sworn before me.—
            <br></br>
            Charles H W Wharton (seal)
         </p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">

         <p>Mr Brent
            <br></br>
            You will only file this &amp; issue Subpas on security
            being given for all fees &amp; costs of
            suit.
            <br></br>
            A.T.
         </p>
