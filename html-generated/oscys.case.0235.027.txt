         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of Lucy Bosy respectfully represents to your Honors that she is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly &amp; illegally held in Bondage by Ann Maria Fitzhugh</p>

         <p>Your petitioner therefore prays your Honors to enquire into the matter</p>
     
         <p>D Ratcliffe<br/>
Atty for Petr</p>
