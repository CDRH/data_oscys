<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
     
<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Elizabeth Young <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found with the County of Washington, in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3rd Monday of January next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Christina Pinckney</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 12t day of Novr. Anno DOmini one thousand eight hundred and 58</p>

<p>Issued the 13t day of Novr. 1858</p>

<p>Jno. A. Smith clk.</p>

<p>C &amp; L</p>
