         <p>This day the parties before named appeared in Court and filed this Petition stating that they were entitled to their freedom and setting forth the facts on which their title rested and <span class="gap stikethrough">[stikethrough]</span> that they were illegally detained in slavery by <del>Stephen</del> Arthur P. West. And praying that they may be permitted to sue for the recovery of their freedom in forma pauperum</p>

         <p>Whereupon R. I. T. is assigned their counsel to state and report their case to the Court. And the <span class="unclear illegible">said[?]</span> counsel thereupon reported the case of the said Petitioners and that in his opinion entitled to their freedom.</p>
