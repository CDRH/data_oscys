         <p>To the Honble The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
                
         <p>The Petition of Ann Shorter in behalf of her son Michael.</p>

         <p>Humbly Sheweth.</p>
                
         <p>That the said Michael is detained in Slavery by a certain Geo: Fenwick. Your Petitioner therefore prays that process may issue against him, so that the right of the said Michael to freedom may be asserted &amp; proved, &amp; Your Petitioner shall ever pray.</p>
                         
         <p>H: Hiort for the Petitioner</p>

         <p>28 June 1808.</p>
