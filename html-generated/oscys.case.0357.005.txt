         <p>Negro Daniel<br/>
vs<br/>
Anne Kincheloe</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>It is agreed in this case that the Petr: shall remain in jail for safe keeping until Mr Swann shall write to FSKey or A. Taney stating that the Deft hath complied with the terms upon which by consent the verdict is taken in this cause.</p>

         <p>Which terms are as follows</p>
 
         <p>The Petr: shall be sold to a respectable &amp; settled resident in Alexa: Coty: in this District, who shall be known to be such to T. Swann, upon condition that he be kept within the District of Cola: &amp; never sold thereout, unless T. Swann &amp; FSKey shall concur in stating that they are satisfied the Petr: from his bad conduct &amp; character deserves no longer the benefit of this restriction. And such Purchaser shall <span class="gap strikethrough">[strikethrough]</span> satisfy the said T. Swann that he makes the said purchaser fairly &amp; bonâ fide subject to these <span class="gap strikethrough">[strikethrough]</span> conditions.</p>
               
         <p>May 4th 1822.</p>

         <p>Tho Swann for the claimants<br/>
FSKey for Petr:</p>
