<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Sarah Stewart humbly sheweth That she is free and is held in bondage and claimed as a slave by James C. McGuire administrator with the will annexed of John P Todd late of the County aforesaid deceased, and she prays that her right to freedom may be fully enquired of by your Honors according to law.</p>

<p>Jos. H. Bradley<br></br>
Atty for Petitioner</p>
