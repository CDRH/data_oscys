<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court Dist. of Columbia for Washington County Set</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of William Green humbly sheweth— That he is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain Elisha Jewell, who claims to hold your petitioner as a Slave.— Wherefore he prays your Honors that subp&#x9c;na may issue to the said Elisha Jewell that he may be compelled to appear &amp; answer hereunto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged from his service &amp; adjudged free according to law.—</p>

<p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr</p>
