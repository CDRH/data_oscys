<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of William Jordan humbly sheweth that he is a free person, &amp; is unjustly held in bondage and claimed as a slave by a certain Lemuel Sawyer of Washington County in this District — Wherefore he prays your Honors to grant him the United States's Writ of Subpena to the said Sawyer directed, that he may appear and answer hereunto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged &amp; obtain such other relief as to your Honors shall seem right.</p>

<p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr</p>
   
</div>
