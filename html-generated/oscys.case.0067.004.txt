         <p>May Term 1826</p>

         <p>John Strother<br/>
Ads<br/>
David Africana</p>

         <p>On a Petition for freedom the Deft's prays for a New trial for the following reasons to wit:</p>

         <p>First</p>

         <p>No competent evidence sufficient to establish petitioner's illegal importation, or title to freedom on any other ground.</p>

         <p>2d. verdict without proof, &amp; contrary to Evidence.</p>

         <p>3. surprise, misdirection from court to jury, &amp;c. &amp;c.</p>
