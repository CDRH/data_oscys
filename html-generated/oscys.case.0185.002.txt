         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,</p>

         <p>To The Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you take Benjamin O. Sheckles late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you his body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3d Monday of October next, answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Mary Duckett &amp;c</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City Washington, the 8th day of April Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty eight</p>

         <p>Issued the 10th day of April 1848</p>

         <p>W Brent, Clk</p>

         <p>D.A. Hall</p>
