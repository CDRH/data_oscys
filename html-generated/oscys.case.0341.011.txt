      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for Washington County,</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>Humbly complaining the Petitioner negro woman Rebecca for herself &amp; infant children William, Ann &amp; Margaret, represents to your Honors that she &amp; her children above named are entitled to their freedom. That nonetheless <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> in slavery &amp; bondage by a certain Lloyd Pumphrey who refuses them that liberty &amp; freedom, which of right is theirs. She therefore in behalf of herself &amp; children above named prays that a subpoena may issue directed to the said Lloyd commanding him to be &amp; appear before your Honble Court at the coming term, there to answer this petition &amp; to be made defendant thereto. She also having want to apprehend a sale &amp; removal immediately from the jurisdiction of your Honble Court prays a writ of injunction, injoining &amp; forbidding the said Lloyd &amp; his agents from removing the petitioner &amp; her children as aforesaid. Hoping such other relief as may be meet in the premises, she thus ever prays &amp;c</p>

         <p>Tho: Turner solr for Petrs</p>

      </div>
