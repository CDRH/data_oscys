         <p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Negro George humbly sheweth that he is <del>ent</del> a freeman &amp; unjustly held in bondage by one Gabriel Adams. He prays your Honors that sbpa may be issued to compel the appearance of said Adams before your Honors that he may <del>be</del> answer hereto &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged from his service &amp; obtain such relief in the premisses as the law entitles him to.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr.</p>
