         <p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>The Petition of Negroe Joe humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is a free man agreeably to the laws &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain George Chapman who claims your Petitioner as a slave. Your Petitioner therefore prays to be discharged from his said servitude, &amp; that a subpoena may issue to the said George Chapman commanding him to appear before your Honours &amp; answer to the premisses; &amp; your Petitioner being prepared to prove his said claim to freedom prays your Honors that the same may be duly enquired into &amp; that he may be granted such relief in the premisses as to your Honors may seem just &amp;c</p>

         <p>F S Key for Petr.</p>
