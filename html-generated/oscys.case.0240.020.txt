         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon Virginia Munroe <del>late of Washington County, if she shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and her safely keep, so that you have her body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the first Monday of May next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for Freedom of Rebecca Madella</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, James Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 19th day of Feb'y Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 58</p>

         <p>Issued the 20 day of Feby 1858</p>

         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>

         <p>C &amp; M</p>
