         <p>To the Hon. the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Alexandria County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>The Petition of Julia Roberts sheweth unto your honors that a certain Austin L. Adams claims your petitioner as his slave &amp; does hold her in slavery by authorising his sister Mrs Anne E Harding to keep your petitioner in slavery. <del>Wherefore</del> Your Petitioner sheweth that she is in law entitled to her freedom &amp; that the claims of said Adams in this behalf, are wholly groundless. Wherefore she prays that her right to freedom may be investigated &amp; that she may be discharged from the <del>dominion</del> illegal restraint imposed upon her by the said Adams &amp; by the said Ann E. Harding acting for the said Adams And as in duty bound your Petitioner will ever pray.</p>

         <p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioner</p>

      </div>
