      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court for Alexandria County.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>Your petitioner Thomas Morris humbly represents to your Honors, that he is now confined in the Jail of Washington County, in this District, where he was originally placed at the instance of       Burch of Alexandria and is now detained for the payment of jail fees, and, he believes, for no other cause. Your petitioner believes his confinement to be illegal &amp; unjust; and prays the Court to grant him the U. States writ of Habeas Corpus, directed to the Jailor of Washington County, commanding him to bring the body of your petitioner before your Honors, in order that he cause of his said detention may be enquired into, &amp; your petitioner discharged. Your petitioner begs leave to refer your honors to the certificate of said Jailer hereto annexed.</p>

         <p>Thos. Morris<br/>
            <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> Wm <span class="unclear illegible">Stabler[?]</span>
         </p>

         <p>Set the writ issue by order of the Court 12th Novr. 1832.</p>
