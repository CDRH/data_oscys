      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Negro Betsy humbly sheweth that she is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by George McCandless who she prays may be made a Deft. hereto.</p>

         <p>Wherefore she prays subpoena to be issued in the usual form, commanding him to appear &amp; answer hereunto &amp; that she may be released from his possession &amp; custody according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop<br/>
for Petr.</p>

      </div>
