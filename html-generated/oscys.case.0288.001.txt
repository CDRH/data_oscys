         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon John B Armistead if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, so that he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of
            Columbia for the County of Washington on the first Monday of June next to answer the
            Petition of Milford Foote in the said Court exhibited against him, for his freedom.
            Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ. Witness the
            Hon
