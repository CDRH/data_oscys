<p>Sir, Received your note in answer to mine of last Sunday <span class="illegible">[sateing?]</span> your price of Abraham to be $500 and the <del class="balance of the">balance of the</del> <span class="illegible">[year?]</span> <span class="illegible">[hire?]</span>, Mr Lyons will give you a fair price for your man as he think he considers him not a prime hand, prime hand can be bought for $500 Abraham is small and weakly, he has Directed me to give you 400 <del class="after">after</del> when the money paid the hire to stop by paying for what time he has served which I believe it is most he will give if you will take that sum you will write by Abraham so that I can make the arrangement with him for the money. Am yours most Respectfully</p>

<p>Elijah Chamblin<br></br>
27th of June 1813</p>
