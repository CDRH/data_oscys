         <p>To the Honorable Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, sitting in and for Washington
            County
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Elisa Wigle a minor of tender age, by her next friend Sarah Wigle
            all of the County of Washington in the District of Columbia, respectfully represents,
            that your Petition Elisa is free and was born so, and that she is illegally detained
            by Henry Nailor admr of George Nailor decd of same County who claims to hold her as
            his slave.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Yr Petitioner therefore prays yr Honors that a summons may issue yr said Henry Hailor
            to appear before your 
            <del class="Honors">Honors</del> to answer this Petition and that yr Petioner the said Elisa may be returned to her
            liberty and the 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> claim of said Defendant 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> to be null &amp; void and that he be compelled to pay all costs and as in duty bound
            will ever pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioner</p>
         
         
      </div>
