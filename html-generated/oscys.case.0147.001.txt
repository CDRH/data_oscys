      <div class="caption ">

         <p>The Petition of James Ash to the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>Humbly sheweth that the said James Ash <del>is a free man, that he</del> entitled to his freedom, and he hath filed <del>in</del> his petition in the Prince George County Court, and State of Maryland; that the said Petitioner is still pending in the said County of Prince George County, that a certain John Grimes hath arrested your petitioner, and now holds him in custody, and hath confined him in the private jail of William H. Williams of the City of Washington.</p>

         <p>Your petitioner prays that subpoena may be issued for the said John Grimes and the said William H. Williams, and that your petitioner may have a fair trial of his said petition in your Honorable Court.</p>

         <p>Jos H Bradley for Petn.</p>

      </div>
