<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Elizabeth Whiting late of Washington County, if she shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del class="and her safely keep,">and her safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you have her body">you have her body</del> she be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition of John Peterson for his freedom filed in the said Court.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 28th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

<p>Issued the 23d day of July 1838</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Key.</p>
