<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon William M. Lewis late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del class="and safely keep,">and safely keep,</del> so that <del class="you have">you have</del> he be &amp; appear <del class="have his bod">have his bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition of Lidia Harris filed in the said Court against him, for her freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 28th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

<p>Issued the 21st day of July 1838.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Dandridge</p>
