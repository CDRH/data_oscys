         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Your Petitioner Frederick Mortimer would respectfully represent to your Honble Court
            that he is entitled to his freedom, but that he is illegally &amp; unjustly held in servitude
            by one Hudson M. Garland, whom your Petitioner peas may be summoned to answer to this
            his Petition. &amp; that the liberty to which he is entitled may be adjudged to him &amp;
            he be discharged from the illegal restraint of said Garland.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>All which is respectfully submitted.</p>
         
         
         <p>Fendall &amp; Smith
            <br></br>
            Attys for said Petitioner
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
