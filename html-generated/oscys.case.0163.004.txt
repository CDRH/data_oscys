         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon William M Lewis late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and <del>safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> he be and appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer unto Lidia Harris's petition for freedom filed in the said Court agt her</p>
