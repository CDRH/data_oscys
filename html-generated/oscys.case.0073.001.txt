         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon
            Augustus Preuss
            if he shall be found in the county of
            Washington,
            that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the
            county of Washington, in
            the city of Washington, on the First Monday of
            May next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>
            answer the petition for freedom of Janette Lee filed in court against him
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 9th day of March 1827</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent, Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Beale</p>
