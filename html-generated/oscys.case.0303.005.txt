<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon James Robinson late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Stephen Ambush.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness, Jas. Dunlop Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 1st day of July Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 56.</p>

<p>Issued the 5t day of Augt. 1856.</p>

<p>Jno: A. Smith clk</p>

<p>Bradley<br/>
Ratcliff</p>
