      <div class="body">

         <p>Jane alias Jane Williams complains of Richard H Love in custody &amp;c. In a plea of trespass on the case. For that the said Richard H Love at the County aforesaid on the first day of april 1816 with force &amp; arms assaulted the plaintiff and her then &amp; there took and imprisoned and restrained of her liberty and held her in servitude from the said first day of April 1816 until       against the law of the land &amp; against the wish of the plaintiff and other injuries the said Richard H Love within that time did to the plaintiff against the peace &amp; dignity of the United States and to the damage of the plaintiff of one hundred dollars &amp; therefore she brings suit &amp;c.</p> 

         <p>Thomson F. Mason pro que</p>
