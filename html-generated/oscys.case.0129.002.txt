<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Hudson M. Garland that he be appear before the Court here, <del>immediately, as Witness for</del> on the fourth Monday of Novr next to answer the Petition for Freedom of Frederick Mortimer and so forth.</p>

<p>Issued 2 day of May 1842.</p>

<p>By Order, W. Brent. C</p>

<p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia.</p>
