         <p>To the Honourable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Evaline Blue a person of Colour humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honourable Court that your Petitioner is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun who claim your Petitioner as slave Wherefore your Petitioner prays the said Honourable Court to grant a subpoena to the said John W Williams Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun directed and such relief in the premises as may be according to law</p>

         <p>John J Dermott<br/>
atty for Petitioner</p>
