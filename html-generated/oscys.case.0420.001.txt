         <p>District of Columbia to wit<br/>
The United States' of America<br/>
to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>We command you, that the bodies of Rebecca Jackson &amp; Arnold Jackson, or by whatever name they may be called, detained by you, or under your custody as it is said, together with the day, &amp; cause of their being taken &amp; detained, you have immediately before the Hon Judges of our Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at the Court house in the town of Alexandria, to do, receive, &amp; submit to all &amp; singular those things which shall then &amp; there be considered of them in this behalf.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon: Wm Cranch, Chief Judge of our said Court this 3d day of October 1831.</p>

         <p>Ed: J Lee CC</p>
