         <p>District of Columbia—to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you summon Jacob Hoffman &amp; Joseph Riddle to appear before the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House, in the town of Alexandria, <del>on the</del> immediately <del>day of the</del> 
            <del>Court, which commences on the</del> 
            <del>Monday in</del> to testify, and the truth to say on behalf of Charles Slade in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro Lucy and Charles Slade, and this he shall in no wise omit, under penalty of Dolls. 333 33 Cts. And have then there this writ. Witness William <del>Kilty</del> Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 15th day of July 1807.</p>

         <p>G. Deneale CC:</p>
