         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia 
            for Washington County.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Wood Humphreys humbly sheweth that he is
            entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain
            William Berry who claims your Petitioner as a slave.
            Wherefore he prays the United States writ of sbpa to the
            said Defendant directed that he may be compelled to appear &amp; answer
            hereunto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr</p>
