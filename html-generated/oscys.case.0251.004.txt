         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Bridget McLaughlin if she shall be found in the
            County of Washington, that she be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District
            of Columbia, 
            <del class="at rules to be held for">at rules to be held for</del> the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> third Monday of October next, to answer 
            <del class="the bill of complaint of">the bill of complaint of</del> the petition of freedom of Margaret Lewis for her self &amp; children 
            <del class="filed in Court here against and others.">filed in Court here against and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 17th day of July 1852.</p>
         
         
         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on
