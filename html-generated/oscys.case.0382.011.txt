      <div class="caption">

         <p>District of Columbia—towit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia— Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you summon Susan Hodgkin and Henson Moreland to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, <del>on the</del> Thursday the 4th day of the Court, <del>which commences on the</del> Monday to testify, and the truth to say on behalf of Chloe Ann Marter in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between the said Chloe A. Marter and Elizabeth Neale and this you shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333.33 Cts. And have then there this Writ.</p>

         <p>Witness—William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 24th day of Apl. in the year of our Lord, 1816.</p>

         <p>G Deneale CC</p>

      </div>
