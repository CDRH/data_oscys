<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Cushing Daniel if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that he be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of March next, to answer the <del class="bill of complaint of">bill of complaint of</del> Petition for freedom of Polly Parker filed in Court here against him <del class="and others.">and others.</del></p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness of Hon. W Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

<p>Issued the 6th day of January 1845.</p>

<p>W. Brent Ck</p>

<p>C.S. Wallach</p>

<p><del class="Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.">Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the day on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.</del></p>

<p>I authorise &amp; depute       to serve this precept March 22nd 1845</p>
