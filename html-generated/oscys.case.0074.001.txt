<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of United States for the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Nancy an infant by her mother and next friend Lizette Lee humbly sheweth unto your honors that she is entitled to her freedom &amp; is unjustly &amp; illegally held in bondage by one Augustus Preuss who holds your petitioner &amp; (as she believes &amp; is informed) means to sell her as a slave for life. Whereupon she prays your honors that a subpoena may be issued to the said Augustus Preuss directed that he may appear and answer hereto &amp; that your petitioner may be discharged form her service &amp; obtain such relief in the premises as to your honors may seem meet</p>

<p>R Beale for Petitioner</p>
