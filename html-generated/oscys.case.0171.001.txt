         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for
            the County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Maria Watts respectfully sheweth to your Honors that she is now held
            in unlawful custody as a slave by Raphael Semmes of said County of Washington and
            that in fact your petitioner is free and your petitioner prays your Honors to take
            into consideration her case and hear and determine the same, and to grant the U S
            writ of subpoena directed to the said Semmes. And your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>R S Cox &amp; D Ratcliffe
            <br></br>
            atty for Petr
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
