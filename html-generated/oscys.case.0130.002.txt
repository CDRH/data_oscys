         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon John 
            <del class="G.">G.</del> Stuart late of Washington County if he shall be found in the County of Washington,
            that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District
            of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City of Washington, on
            the fourth Monday of March next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> Answer the Petition Freedom of Negroes Gabriel the elder and Nelly his wife 
            <del class="and">and</del> Gabriel younger and Rachel filed in Court here against him
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 20th day of February 1833.</p>
