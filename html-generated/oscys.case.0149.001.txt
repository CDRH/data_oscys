<p>To the Hon. Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Jeffrey Peters represents that he is a freeman and by the Laws of the county entitled to act as such, notwithstanding which he is now confined in jail and claimed by a certain Joseph McKinstrey as his slave.</p>

<p>Yr Petitioner therefore prays yr Honors to issue subpoena as the Law directs for said Joseph and that yr Petitioner may have his right to his freedom established by a decision of yr Hon. Hourt and as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioners</p>

</div>
