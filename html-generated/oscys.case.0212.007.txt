      <div class="body">

         <p>Interrogatories on the part of the Defendant</p>

         <p>1. Do you know the Parties, Petitioners, or Defendant, and how long have you known them?</p>

         <p>2. Did you know John Dales who formerly owned Tom &amp; his wife, and did you know him when he lived in Montgomery County, &amp; afterwards in George Town, and how long did he live in George Town, and in what year or years; and had he then Tom &amp; Sarah with him, as slaves?</p>

         <p>3. Do you know that he brought Tom &amp; Sarah with him when he removed from Montgomery County to George Town?</p>

         <p>4. Do you know how long &amp; in what year Dales lived in George Town &amp; what was his occupation?</p>

         <p>5. Did you know him when he was a tenant to Mrs. Thompson — to Matthew Kennedy — When he lived with one Flaut — and with Christian Getzandanner — and with William Duvall; and in what year; and was he engaged with Christian Getzendanner in a Grocery Store, and was he in the capacity
