<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Neale &amp; Simpson late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep, so</del> that <del>you have bod</del> they be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> The Petition of Charles Cross exhibited in said Court</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 14 day of January Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 39</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
