         <p>To the Hon. Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for Washington County</p>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We do not oppose Hiram Hunt in <del>opposing</del> getting his freedom and we have no claim to him and we by these presents do hereby quit claim to him, as our slave and we further absolve any right to him, in the <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> of any person whomsoever and do not wish further to defend the suit brought by him against us.</p>

         <p>A Newton (seal)<br/>
W. Gadsby (seal)</p>

         <p>Witness 30 April 1841.</p>

         <p>William Crowe</p>
