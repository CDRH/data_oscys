         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>The Petition of James White a free negro, respectfully sheweth to the Court that he is illegally detained by the Jailor of this County Your petitioner prays the court to grant him a writ of Habeas Corpus returnable forthwith to be <span class="unclear illegible">directd[?]</span> the Jailor of this County commanding to bring your petitioner before the Court with the cause of his detention, that the court may inquire into the legality of his imprisonment.</p>

         <p>Edm: J Lee for Petitioner
8th Novr 1841</p>
