         <p>District of Columbia, to Wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> sum Thomas Roger adr. of d. b. n. of Ann Rozel <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have</del> to <del>bod</del> appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4 Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for freedom Henry Addison</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 2d day of November Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.</p>

         <p>Issued the 3d day of Nov 1847.</p>

         <p>W Brent clk</p>

         <p>Wood.</p>
