<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the Dist of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of John Peterson humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage and claimed as a slave by a certain Elizabeth Whiting</p>

<p>Wherefore he prays that sbpa may be issued to her to appear &amp; answer hereto before your Honors, &amp; that he may be discharged &amp; declared free according to law.</p>

<p>FSKey for Petr</p>

</div>
