         <p>To the Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
                
         <p>The humble Petition of Catherine Killigrew on behalf of herself and her Children, Christopher Killigrew, Anny Killigrew, Louisa Killigrew, and an Infant unnamed</p>
                
         <p>Sheweth</p>
                
         <p>That Your Petitioners are detained in Slavey by Mary Ann Fenwick; She therefore on behalf of herself &amp; Children pray; that as she can shew &amp; prove they are entitled to their freedom that the usual process may issue, so that they may be permitted legally to assert their pretension</p>
                
         <p>And Your Petr as in duty bound will ever pray</p>
                
         <p>H: Hiort for Petrs</p>
                
         <p>18 July 1808</p>
