         <p>In the name of God Amen I Maria Anne T Greenfield of Prince Georges County in the
            state of Maryland being sick and weak in body but of sound disposing mind, memory
            and understanding, do make and publish this my last will and testament in manner and
            form following: that is to say: First and principally I commit my soul into the hands
            of Almighty God and my body to the earth to be decently buried at the discretion of
            my Executor herein after named, and after my debts and funeral charges are paid I
            give and bequeath as follows: Item I give and bequeath to my niece Amelia T. Dorsett
            the sum of seventy dollars to be paid to her by my Executor hereafter named. I give
            and bequeath to my Overseer John Grimes one hundred and forty dollars to be paid to
            him by my Executor  hereinafter named Item I give &amp; bequeath to my Nephew Gerard T.
            Greenfield my negroe Slave Ned to serve him eight years then to have his freedom during
            his life also I give &amp; bequeathe to my Nephew Gerard T. Greenfield Maria's three children
            namely James Alfred and Eliza to serve him until they arrive to the age of thirty
            eight years old then to be free during life I give &amp; bequeath to my Nephew Gerard
            T Greenfield my Negroe slave Chrissy to serve me til she arrives to the age of sixteen
            years old then to be free during life.
         </p>
         
         
         <span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]" title="&lt;a href=&#34;http://rosie.unl.edu/data_images/projects/example/figures/large/p002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">

         <p>I also give, bequeath to my Nephew Gerard T. Greenfield the whole of my part of my
            Sister Sabina T. Greenfields Estate that may be belong to me when a decision of the
            said Estate may take place
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I also give &amp; bequeath to my Newphew Gerard T. Greenfield all my Negroe Slaves namely
            Ben Mansa James Henry George Lewis Rebecca Kitty Sophia Mary Elizabeth Nathaniel &amp;
            Maria also Toney Billy Betty &amp; Anny  promised he shall not carry them out of the State
            of Maryland or sell them to any one in either on which events I will and desire the
            said negroes to be free for life
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I give &amp; bequeath to my Nephew Gerard T. Greenfield the residue of my Estate not here
            to fore bequeathed in this will. I hereby constitute &amp; appoint my Nephew Gerard T.
            Greenfield sole Executor of this my last will &amp; testament

            my last will &amp; testament in 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> whereof I have herein to set my hand &amp; affixed my seal the sixteenth day of Septr
            1824.
         </p>

            Testatrix as and for her last will &amp; testament in the presence of us who at her request

            same to be her last will and testament and that at the time of her so doing she was
            to the best of his apprehension of sound &amp; disposing mind memory and understanding
