         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The Petition of Nace Humphreys junr: humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain William Berry, who claims your Petitioner as a slave for life. Wherefore he prays your Honors that sbpa may issue, to the said William Berry directed, commanding him to appear &amp; answer hereunto, that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop<br/>
for Petr:</p>
