      <div class="body">

         <p>Winney (<del>a pauper)</del> in forma pauperis) complains of Alexander Moore in custody &amp;c. For that the said Alexander Moore on the first day March in the year 1818 at the County aforesaid with force and arms made an assault upon the said Winney &amp; then &amp; there with stones &amp; sticks did beat &amp; evil entreat her, and imprisoned and illegally restrained her of her liberty. And then &amp; there did her other injuries against the peace &amp; dignity of the United States and to the damage of the said Winney of one hundred dollars &amp; therefore she brings suit &amp;c.</p>

         <p>Thomson F. Mason<br/>
pro quœ</p>
