<p>Washington D.C. Decr. 14th, 1837.</p>

<p>Sir,</p>

<p>In compliance with your request I herewith send you a Certificate of Mr. Kirby's marriage with Mrs. Elenor Moore, not Miss Naylor as mentioned in your notice.</p>

<p>Your's respectfully<br></br>
Wm Ryland</p>


<p>This is to certify to all whom it may concern, that John B Kirby was united in Holy Matrimony to Elenor Moore, on February 19th 1828: by me.</p>

<p>Given under my hand December 14th, 1837</p>

<p>Wm Ryland</p>

</div>

Washington D.C.</p>
