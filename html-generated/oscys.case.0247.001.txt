<p>To the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit COurt of the United States of the District of Columbia &amp; County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Negro Isaac respectfully sheweth that your petitioner is held in bondage &amp; claimed by Alexander Talbot of said County as a slave, Altho' your petitioner is justly entitled to his freedom. Your petitioner therefore, prays that your Honors will take his case into consideration and that a subpoena may issue commanding the said Alexander to appear &amp;c &amp;c &amp; grant him such further &amp; other process &amp; relief as to your Honors may seem meet &amp; his case requires and as in duty bound he will ever pray.</p>

<p>R Wallach<br/>
for petitioner</p>
