         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas D. Owens &amp; Jesse Brown if they shall be
            found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) they be and appear
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of
            Washington, in the City of Washington, on fourth Monday of November next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the petition for freedom of Henry Shelby filed in said court against them
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 19th day of June 1833.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Giberson
            <br></br>
            Elkins
         </p>
