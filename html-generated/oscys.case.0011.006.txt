         <p>Mima Queen &amp; child
            <br></br>
            vs.
            <br></br>
            John Hepburn.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>In the trial of this cause the Marshall returned as a Talisman Joseph Thomas &amp; upon
            the said Thomas's being called to the book, the Petr's Counsel rose &amp; stated to the
            Court that the said Juror was 
            <del class="not">not</del> a resident of the County of Alexandria within the District of Columbia &amp; that he
            would himself state that fact to the Court. which he did &amp; claimed to be exempted
            from serving on the Jury.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>The Court thereupon decided that the Juror's residence being out of the County was
            no exemption 
            <del class="objection to his being sworn">objection to his being sworn</del> from serving on the Jury, &amp; ordered the said Juror to be sworn.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Whereupon the said Juror was sworn before any further objection was made 
            <del class="&amp; the Petitioners Counsel called out &#34;Challenge&#34; to the said Juror but not till after he had signed the book.">&amp; the Petitioners Counsel called out "Challenge" to the said Juror but not till after
               he had signed the book.
            </del> The Petrs. Counsel then immediately after the said Juror was sworn, 
            <span class="ink">[ink]</span> excepted to the opinion 
            <del class="before">before</del> given by the Court before the said Juror was sworn, viz that non-residence in the
            County was no legal 
            <del class="objection">objection</del> exemption to the said Juror 
            <del class="being sworn">being sworn</del> from serving &amp; being sworn on the Jury, &amp; to the order of the Court directing the
            said Juror to be Sworn, &amp; prayed the Court to sign &amp; seal this bill of exceptions.
            which are done accordingly
         </p>    
         
         
         <p>N. Fitzhugh (Seal)
            <br></br>
            B. Thurston (Seal)
         </p>
