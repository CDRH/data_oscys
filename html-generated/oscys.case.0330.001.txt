         <p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the country of Washington The Petition of Rachel a negro woman in behalf of herself and her infant child named Tom humbly sheweth, That your Petitioner and her said child named Tom are entitled to their Freedom and are nevertheless unjustly held in bondage as slaves by a certain James Torrance and your Petitioner therefore prays <del>that</del> your honors <del>will tender</del> that a subpoena may be issued directed to the said James Torrance commanding him to appear at the circuit court of the District aforesaid for the county aforesaid to be held at Washington on the fourth monday of December next to answer to your Petitioner in the premisses and that your honors will grant to your petitioner &amp; her said child such other relief therein as may seem just to your honors &amp; your Petr. &amp;c.</p>

         <p>F. S. Key for Petr.</p>
