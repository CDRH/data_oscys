         <p>The petition of Jane (negress) represents unto your honors that your petitioner is
            entitled to her freedom; and that she is held in 
            <del class="bondage">bondage</del> slavery by one Edmund E Richards of the said County, and prays that a subpoena may
            issue to said Richards, and his rights be enquired into by your honors.
         </p>
