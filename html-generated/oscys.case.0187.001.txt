<p>To the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Court of the district of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Jane (negress) represents unto your honors that your petitioner is entitled to her freedom; and that she is held in <del>bondage</del> slavery by one Edmund E Richards of the said County, and prays that a subpoena may issue to said Richards, and his rights be enquired into by your honors.</p>

<p><span class="unclear illegible">[S. S.?]</span> Williams for Petitioner</p>
