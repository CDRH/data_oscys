      <div class="caption"> 

         <p>The Commonwealth of Virginia,<br/>
To the Serjeant of the Court of Hustings in the Town of Alexandria, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Morris Whorrel, John Halbright, Benja. Hawkins &amp; John Ellwood to appear before the Justices of the said Court of Hustings, at the Court-House in Alexandria, on the <del>Friday after the third</del> first Monday August to testify, and the truth to say, in behalf of Negro Silvia in a certain matter of controversy in our said Court depending and undetermined, between Negro Silvia Plf &amp; George Coryell Def and this they shall in nowise omit under the penalty
of 100 pounds, and have then there this Writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, <del>Peter Wagner,</del> Geo. Deneale Clerk of the said Court, this 19th Day of July 1799.</p>

         <p>G. Deneale</p>
