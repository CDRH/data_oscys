         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>We command you, that you summon George Minor, William Minor &amp; Smith Minor to appear before the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the Court-House in the Town of Alexandria, on the seventh day of June <del>Court, which commences on the Monday</del> next to testify and the truth to say, on behalf of Adams &amp; Harding in a certain matter of controversy, in our said Court depending and undetermined, between them &amp; Julia Roberts and this they shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33 cents.—And have then there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness William Cranch, Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court, this 29' day of May in the year of our Lord 18<del>3</del>44.</p>

         <p>C. F. Lee CC</p>
