      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of Washington County Court.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
                
         <p>The Petition of negro Lucy on behalf of herself &amp; her two infant children Airy and Louisa humbly sheweth that your Petitioner &amp; her said two children are entitled to their freedom by virtue of the laws of the State of Virginia &amp; of the laws in force in this County <del>in</del> &amp; that they are unjustly held in bondage by a certain John Somers and a certain Nathan Somers of said County who claim your said Petitioner &amp; her said Children as slaves.</p>

         <p>Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to issue the United State writ of Subpoena to the said John &amp; Nathan directed that they may be compelled to appear before your Honors, &amp; that your Petitioner may have just relief in the premisses as to your Honors may seem right &amp; agreeable to the laws &amp; proceedings in such cases.</p>

         <p>FSKey for Petr.<br/>
Oct 17. 1812</p>
                        
      </div>
