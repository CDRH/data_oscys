      <div class="caption">

         <p>District of Columbia towit</p>
    
         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Daniel Carroll of Duddington that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held in and for the county of Washington at the City of Washington on the first Monday of June next to answer the petition of William Killegrew for freedom in the said Court exhibited against him.</p>
    
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then and there this writ.</p>
    
         <p>Witness the Honorable William Cranch Esq. Chief Judge of the said court</p>
    
         <p>Issued the 6th day of January 1809</p>
    
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
    
         <p>Hiort</p>
