<p>To the Honrble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Nelly Lemon for herself and her infant children Thomas, Lavenia, Betsey, Dennis, Amy, Harriet, Kitty, respectfully sets forth to your Honors that she is a woman of color and entitled to her freedom and also that her aforementioned seven children are entitled to their freedom that they are all now held in a state of slavery by a John H Bayne contrary to law &amp; your petitioners just rights. Your petitioner therefore prays you honors to grant her &amp; her children such relief as she &amp; they may be lawfully entitled to and such process of your Honbl court against the sd John H Bayne as is usual in such cases to compel the attendance of the said Bayne in your Honlb Court to answer this petition &amp; to enter the usual security &amp; recognizance not to remove your petitioners beyond the jurisdiction of your Honlb Court and as in duty bound your petitioner will ever &amp;c</p>

<p>F. S. Key &amp; BP Smith attys for Petitioners</p>

</div>
