         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Hickey Lee for herself &amp; child Virginia humbly represents that yr petitioner is now held in bondage by one Frederick Goulding in the said County. That yr petitioner is entitled to her freedom and she therefore prays your Honors to grant unto her the U S writ of subpoena &amp;c &amp;c</p>

         <p>Richard Wallach for petitioner</p>
