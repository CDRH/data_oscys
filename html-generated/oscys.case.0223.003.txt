<p>Winchester. Jany 12th 1837</p>

<p>This is to certify that I have given permission to my servant William, a colored man, to hire himself by the month in this state, (namely Virginia), or in the District of Columbia, and not out of the above named places.</p>

<p>To any one wanting an active, honest, faithful and intelligent servant, I can recommend the said William, as such, as I have known him all my life.</p>

<p>William is as good waiter and a good driver; in fact he would be a desirable servant to any gentleman or any family.</p>

<p>I. Purnell. Pendleton</p>

<p>Winchester. Jany 12th 1837</p>
