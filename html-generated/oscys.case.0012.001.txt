         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington
            County
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Alexis Queen of Washington County humbly sheath that your petitioner
            is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage as a slave by a certain John
            Davis of said County.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore he prays your honors to grant him relief in the premises &amp; as he apprehends
            he may be removed out of the county &amp; district before the next term of this court
            she prays a subpoena to issue to the said John Davis returnable immediately
         </p> 
         
         
         <p>F S Key for Pet</p>
         
         
      </div>
