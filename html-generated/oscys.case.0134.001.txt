      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the Distt. of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of negro Gerrard humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom &amp; is held in bondage as a slave by a certain Zachariah Cox.</p>

         <p>Wherefore he prays your Honors to grant him a sbpa to the said Cox directed, that he may appear and answer hereunto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged from his service &amp; obtain such other relief as may be according to law. &amp; your Petitioner will ever pray</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr.</p>

      </div>
