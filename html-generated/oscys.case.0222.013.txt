         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command that you <del>take</del> Summon Isaac Buckingham to appear <del>late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Wm. H. McKinney filed in Court here against him</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court at the City of Washington, the 21 day of December Anno Domini on thousand eight hundred and fifty two</p>

         <p>Issued the 23d day of December 1852.</p>

         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>    

         <p>(S. &amp; S.)</p>
