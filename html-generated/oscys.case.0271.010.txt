         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Nace Humphreys humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain John B Lambert who claims your Petitioner as a slave.</p>

         <p>Wherefore your Petitioner prays subpoena to said Defendant directed that he may appear &amp; answer hereto &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop<br/>
for Petr:</p>
