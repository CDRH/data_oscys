         <p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
                
         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia Greeting</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
                
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon George Fenwick that all excuses and delays set aside he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington to be held at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of December next to answer the petition of Ann Shorter on behalf of her son Michael for his freedom in the said court exhibited against him. Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then and there this writ Witness Wm Cranch Esq. Chief Judge of our said Court the 28th June 1808. Issued the 1st July 1808</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Hiort</p>
