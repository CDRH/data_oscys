<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Joseph W. Neale &amp; Oliver Simpson late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> they be and appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for freedom of Charles Cross filed in the said Court against them.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 13th day of July Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 37.</p>

<p>Issued the 21st day of July 1837.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Hoban</p>
