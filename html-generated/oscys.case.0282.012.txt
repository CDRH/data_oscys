<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Georgiana Munroe <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of January next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Freedom of Sarah H.
