         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br>
            The United States of America, 
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> summon William Aiken late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County
            of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid,
            at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer 
            <del class="unto">unto</del> the petition of John Lee and Ann Maria Lee by their mother Hickey their mother for
            their freedom, filed in said Court against him.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the 27th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty one.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 10th day of February 1841</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Wallk.</p>
