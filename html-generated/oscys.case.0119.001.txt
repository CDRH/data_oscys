         <p>To the Honorable Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, sitting in and for Washington County</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Elisa Johnson of said County of Washington respectfully states that she is a free woman of color and that Thomas B. Belt of said County has taken her into his possession, has confined her in jail of said County. <del>and</del> claims her as his slave and that he has done it <span class="unclear illegible">with[?]</span> 
            <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> of disposing of yr Petitioner <del>out</del> as his slave, contrary to her rights and she therefor prays yr honors to cite said Thos <del>B.</del> I. Belt as the Law directs and that yr Petitioner's case may be inquired into and that she may be returned to her liberty as a free woman and as in duty bound will every pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>Brent &amp; Brent atty for Petitioner</p>

         <p>5 Aug 1840</p>

      </div>
