         <p>We command you that you summon William Patterson to appear before the honorable the Judges of the circuit court of the District of Columbia, for the county of Alexandria, at the court house in the town of Alexandria, on the 20th day of the December <del>court, which commences on the</del> next <del>Monday</del>
