         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:</p>

         <p>The United States of America,</p>

         <p>To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Hayman administrator of John Wetzel, Deceased
if he shall be found within the county of
Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county of Washington, in the city of Washington, on the Third <del>day</del> of
Monday of May next, to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the petition of Negress Eliza for her freedom in said Court filed against him</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril: and have you then and there this precept.</p> 

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 15th day of January 1825</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Smith</p>

      </div>
