         <p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting.</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Augustin Saira that all excuses and delays set
            aside, he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be
            held for the County of Washington 
            <del class="on then">on then</del> at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of December next, to answer the Petition
            of Philis Butler for freedom in the said Court filed against him.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the honble Wm Cranch esq. Chief Judge of our said Court the 28th day of June
            1808.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 25th July 1808.</p>
