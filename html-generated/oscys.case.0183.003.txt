         <p>To the Honorable the Judge of the Circuit Court of the United States for the District
            of Columbia and County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Nathan Jackson a Coloured person, humbly sheweth unto the said Honorable
            Court that you petitioner is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage
            by Joseph Parker, who claims your petitioner as his Slave, wherefore your petitioner
            prays that said Honorable Court to grant a subpoena directed to the said Joseph Parker
            and such other relief in the premise as may be according to the Law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>James M Carlise &amp;
            <br></br>
            Daniel Ratcliffe
            <br></br>
            Attys for Petitioner
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
