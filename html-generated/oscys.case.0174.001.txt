      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of negro Mary and       her infant child humbly sheath that she is entitled
            to her freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by Lewis Talburt, who claims your Petitioners
            as slaves.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore she prays your Honors to grant sbpa to said Talburt directed &amp; such relief
            in the premisses as may be according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petrs</p>

         <p>Personlly appears David I Waters in open Court and makes oath that he did on the 25th
            Instant serve the within on Lewin Talburt by leaving a copy of the within notice at
            his House with a Young Lady who appeared to be at home there &amp; who promised to deliver
            it to said Talburt.
         </p>
