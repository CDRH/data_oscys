      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Joseph McKinstrey if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> Answer the Petition for freedom of Jeffrey Peters against him and so forth
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 26th day of Decr 1838.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
