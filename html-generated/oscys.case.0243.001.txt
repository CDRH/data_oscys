         <p>You are hereby to summon Susan Armstead if she shall be found in the County of Washington,
            that her be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules
            to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> fourth Monday of November next, to answer 
            <del class="to a bill of complaint of">to a bill of complaint of</del> the Petition for Freedom of Eleanora Bell by her next friend Wm Simms filed in Court
            here against her 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the Hon. W Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 13th day of Sept 1844.</p>
         
         
         <p>W. Brent, Ck</p>
         
         
         <p>CS Wallach</p>
