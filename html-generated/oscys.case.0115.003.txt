<p>Negress Chloe Ann, who calls herself Chloe Ann Johnson a black woman of light complexion, about 44 yrs of age &amp; about 5.1. high, stout &amp; well made, having a scar on her left eye brow, one near the left corner of her mouth occasioned by a fall, one on her upper lip, one between the second &amp; third joints of the fore finger of her left hand and one on her right arm between the wrist &amp; elbow joints occasioned by a burn.</p>

<p>Identified by M. Foye. <span class="unclear illegible">[Oath?]</span> given 15 May 1834.</p>
