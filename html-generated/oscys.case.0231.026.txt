      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The petition of Fanny Hamilton a coloured woman, humbly sheweth unto your Honors, that she is entiled to her freedom, and is unjustly detained and held in bondage, by one Mary Roberson of the state of Virginia, who claims your petitioner as her slave, wherefore your petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to be directed to the said Mary Roberson and such other relief in the premises as may be according to Law and your petitioner will as in duty bound ever pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>Fanny Hamiliton<br/>
by D Ratcliffe her atty</p>

      </div>
