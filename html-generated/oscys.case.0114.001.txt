         <p>To the honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington.
         </p>
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Respectfully sheweth to your honors your Petitioner Charles Cross that he is entitled
            to his freedom and unjustly claimed and held in bondage by Joseph John W. Neale &amp;
            Oliver Simpson from whom he prays to be released and your orator, as in duty bound
            will every pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>James Hoban for Petitioner</p>
         
         
         <p>March 22. 1836</p>
