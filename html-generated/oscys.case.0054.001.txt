      <div class="body ">

         <p>The petition of James Humphreys by Dolly
Humphreys his 
next friend, respectfully shewth, that said James
Humphreys is entitled to his freedom, but is held in slavery as a slave
for life by Henry Roberts. Your petitioner
therefore prays that he may be released from the bondage of said
Henry Roberts, and hat subpa may issue to said
Henry Roberts commanding him &amp;c in the usual form. And
your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Caldwell for Petrs</p>
