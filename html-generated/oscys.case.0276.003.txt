<p>To the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Parmelia Adams and Sylvester Isabella &amp; Truelove Adams children of the said Parmelia, respectfully represent that they are held in unlawful bondage by one James H. Holmes, a resident of the City of Washington D. C. and they pray that your Honors may order a subpoena to be issued against the said Holmes, and that relief may be afforded to your petitioners &amp; as in duty bound &amp;c</p>

<p>John F. Ennis<br/>
atty for petrs</p>

<p>Oct. 1, 1852</p>
     
</div>
