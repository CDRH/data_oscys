<p>To the Honourable The Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Wallace Blue a person of colour humbly complaining sheweth unto the said Honourable Court that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom and is unjustly held in bondage by John W Williams, Mary S Williams his wife and William S Colquhoun directed and such relief in the premises as my be according to law</p>

<p>John J Dermott<br/>
atty for Petitioner</p>
