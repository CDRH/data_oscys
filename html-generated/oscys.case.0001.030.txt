<p>To Negro Ben Greeting: You are hereby acted &amp; admonished to be and appear at a Supreme Court of the United States, to be holden <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> the City of Washington on the first monday in <del>August</del> February next permanent <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> a writ of error filed in the Clerk's office of the Circuit Court of the United States for the District of Columbia in the County of Washington wherein Negro Ben is Petitioner &amp; Sabritt Scott is Defendant, to shew cause if any there be why the Judgment rendered against the said Sabrett Scott as in the said writ of error mentioned should not be reversed, &amp; why the error complained of should not be corrected &amp; why speedy Justice should not be done to the parties in that behalf</p>

<p>Witness W. Cranch, chief Judge of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia this <del>22th day of June 1807</del> 11th day of January 1808.<br/>
W. Cranch</p>

<p>On the 13th day of January 1808 John A Wilson personally appeared before me the subscriber a Justice of the Peace for the County of Washington District of Columbia &amp; made oath on the holy evangels of almighty god that he the deponent served the within citation on the within named Negro Ben on the twelfth day of instant and left with said Negro <span class="unclear torn page">[Ben?]</span> a true copy of the same at the time afsd.</p>

<p>Robt Brent.</p>
