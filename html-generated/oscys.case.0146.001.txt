         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Jack Rhodes by Kitty Green his mother &amp; next friend humbly sheweth
            that he is entitled to his freedom being born of a free woman, and is unjustly claimed
            as a slave for a limited period or as a bound apprentice, he knows not which, by a
            certain William Glover. He desires the right of said Glover to hold or claim your
            Petitioner &amp; prays to be released thereupon &amp; to be established in his right to freedom
            &amp; such other relief as to your Honors may seem right &amp; according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>FSKey for Petr.</p>
         
         
      </div>
