         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:
            <br></br>
            The United States of America,
            <br></br>
            To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>We command you that you take Jane W Stevenson late of Washington County, if she shall
            be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and her safely keep,
            so that you have her body before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to
            be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of
            March next, to answer unto Benjamin Qualls his petition for freedom &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the 7th day of December Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty seven.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 6th day of January 1848.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>C.S. Walh</p>
