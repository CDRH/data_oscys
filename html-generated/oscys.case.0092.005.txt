            as aforesaid do not impose upon the defts the necessity of proving by substantive

            <div class="bibl code">act of the Genl. Assembly of Virginia passed in the year 1785. ch. 77 </div>, within the time prescribed by that act: and that the jury, upon the said state of
            facts, ought not to find for the petitioners, for the want of such substantive process
            on the part of the defts.
