         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington
            County—
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Priscilla Queen of Washington County humbly sheath that your petitioner
            is entitled to her freedom is unjustly held in bondage as a slave by a certain Francis
            Neale of said County—
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore she prays your Honors to grant her relief in the premises &amp; as she apprehends
            she may be removed out of the County &amp; district before the next term of this Court
            she prays a subpoena to issue to the said Francis Neale reable immediately.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F S Key for Petr.</p>    
         
         
      </div>
