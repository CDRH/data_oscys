<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br> 
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>We command you that you <del class="take">take</del> summon Henry T. Weightman late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District <del class="and safey">and safey</del> keep, so that <del class="you have bod">you have bod</del> appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer unto Nancy &amp; Henrietta Fletcher's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 6th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 35</p>

<p>Issued the 29th day of October 1835</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>B &amp; B</p>
