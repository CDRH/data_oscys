<p>We command you that you summon <del class="take">take</del> Bridget McLaughlin &amp; James Sheehy. <del class="late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have">late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that you have</del> to be &amp; appear <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of October next, to answer <del class="unto">unto</del> the Petition for freedom of Margaret Lewis, &amp; her children Andrew Lewis, James Lewis &amp; Margaret Lewis &amp;c.</p>

<p>Issued the 27th day of April 1850.</p>

<p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>
