         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> summon Francis Bowling &amp; Clarissa B. Gardner admnrs of Richard B. Garnder late of
            Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington in your
            said District, and them safely keep, so that you have their bodies before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City
            of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer unto the petition for freedom
            of Margaret Norah Atkins &amp; Alfred Atkins by David Atkins their father &amp; next friend
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington,
            the 14th day of Jany Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 27th day of January 1843.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Bradley</p>
