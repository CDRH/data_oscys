         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon
            Janet Lingan
            if she shall be found in the 
            <span class="placeName">county of
               Washington
            </span>,
            that (all excuses 
            <del class="set aside">set aside</del>) She be and appear before the Circuit
            Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the
            county of Washington, in
            the city of Washington, on Second Monday of
            April next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>
            answer the petition of Freedom of Richard Johnson filed 
            in Court here agst. her
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 7th day of Decr. 1822</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
