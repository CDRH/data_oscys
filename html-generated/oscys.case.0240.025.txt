         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Virginia Munroe <del>late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his body</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the third Monday of January next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition for freedom of Rebecca Madella</p>
