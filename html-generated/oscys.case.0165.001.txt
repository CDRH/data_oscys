<p>The Petition of Lucy Crawford against Martha A Scott humbly represents to your Honorable Court that she is a woman of Colour and entitled to her freedom and that she is unjustly claimed and held as a slave by the said Martha she therefore prays that she may be discharged from such claim by the judgment of this Honorable Court<br/>
J Hellen</p>

<p>To the Judges of the Circuit Court for the District of Columbia &amp; County of Washington sitting as a Court of Common Law</p>
