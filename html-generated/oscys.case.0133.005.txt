         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/> 
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia -- Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Olivia Kean and Silas Burke Exors of James Kean Jr. late of Washington County, if they shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and him safely keep, so that <del>you</del> they <del>have his body</del> be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of George Kean filed in the said Court for his freedom</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ:</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 22d day of January Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 38.</p>

         <p>Issued the 2d day of February 1838.</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Dermott</p>
