<p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
                
<p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
                
<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Keene late of the County of Washington if he shall
                    be found in the County <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> District, that all excuses and delays set <span class="unclear torn image">[aside?]</span> he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held for the county of Washington to be held at the city of Washington
                    on the fifth Monday of <span class="unclear torn image">[June?]</span> instant to answer the petition of Elizabeth Bentley for herself and on behalf of her children to wit Daniel Bentley, William Bentley, Susanna Bentley, Dennis Bentley, Johnson Bentley, <del>William</del> Lee Bentley, Nelson Bentley, Nancy Bentley, Rebecca Bentley, Anne Bentley and Eleanor Bentley for their freedom in this Court exhibited against him. Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then and there this writ. Witness the hon. William Cranch Esqr chief Judge of our said court. the 13th day of April 1809.</p>
                
<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
               
<p>Issued the 3d day of June 1809.</p>
                
<p>Morsell</p>
