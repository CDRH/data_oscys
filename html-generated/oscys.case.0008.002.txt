         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Thomas Keene late of the County of Washington if
            he shall
            be found in the County 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> District, that all excuses and delays set 
            <span class="torn image">[aside?]</span> he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held
            for the county of Washington to be held at the city of Washington
            on the fifth Monday of 
            <span class="torn image">[June?]</span> instant to answer the petition of Elizabeth Bentley for herself and on behalf of

            Johnson Bentley,

            your peril and have you then and there this writ. Witness the hon. William Cranch
            Esqr chief Judge of our said court. the 13th day of April 1809.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
