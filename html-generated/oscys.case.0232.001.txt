         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Henry Addison respectfully sheweth to your honors that he is now held
            in unlawful custody as a slave by one Thomas Rogers Administrator de bonis non of
            Miss Ann Raszel late of Loudoun County State of Virginia deceased and that in fact
            your petitioner is free and your petitioner prays your honors to take into Consideration
            his Case and hear and determine the same and your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>William R Woodward
            <br></br>
            Atty for Petitioner
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Henry
