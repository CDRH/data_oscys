         <p>District of Columbia—To wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon Augustus Pruce Late of Washington County if he shall be found within the county of Washington, in your said district <del>and</del> that he <del> safely keep so that you have </del> be &amp; appear <del>bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the first Monday of December next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition of Lizett Lee filed in said Court against him.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p> 

         <p>Witness William Cranch esquire, chief judge of our said court at the City of Washington, the 21st day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and twenty six</p>

         <p>Issued the 16th day Septr 1826</p>

         <p>William Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Beale</p>
