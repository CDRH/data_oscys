         <p>We command you that you 
            Summon Augustus Pruce Late of Washington County
            if he shall be found within the county of
            Washington, in your said district 
            <del class="and">and</del> that he 
            <del class="safely keep so that you have">safely keep so that you have</del> be &amp; appear 
            <del class="bod">bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county aforesaid,
            at the City of Washington, on the first Monday of
            December next, to answer 
            <del class="for">for</del> the Petition of Lizette Lee filed in said Court against him
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness William Cranch esquire, chief judge of our said court at the City of Washington,
            the 21st day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and twenty six
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 16th day Septr 1826</p>
