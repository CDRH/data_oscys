         <p>Saint Mary's County ss the 15th day of May 
            <span class="torn page">[torn page]</span> Thompson Executors of John Thompson late of Saint Mary's County, deceased, and made
            Oath on the Holy Evangels of Almighty God that the aforegoing is a true and perfect
            Inventory of all and singular the goods, Chattels and personal Estate of the Said
            deceased, that hath come to their hands or possession at the time of the making thereof,
            and that, what hath since or shall hereafter come to their hands or possession they
            will return in an additional Inventory that they know of no Concealement of any part
            or parcel of the deceaseds estate by any person whatsoever and that if they shall
            hereafter discover any Concealement or Suspect any 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> be, they will acquaint the Justices 
            <del class="of process">of process</del> for the time being of the Orphan's Court in St Mary's County or the Regr Wills for
            Saint Mary's County, with such Concealemen or cause of Suspicion that it may be enquired
            into according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Certified 
            <span class="illegible">[illegible]</span> Jeremiah Jordan Reg Wills for St Mary's County
         </p>
         
         
         <p>In Testimony that the aforegoing is truly taken from record I have set my name this
            15 June 1816
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Jas Forrest Reg Wills</p>
