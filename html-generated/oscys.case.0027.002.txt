         <p>District of Columbia....to wit,</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America,  To The Marshal of the District of Columbia — 
            Greeting —
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded, to summon Mary Ann Pic that, all excuses and delays set
            aside, 
            
            <del class="you">you</del> she be and appear before the circuit court of the district of Columbia, to
            be held for the county of Washington, on the fourth Monday of December next, to answer
            the 
            <del class="bill of complaint">bill of complaint</del> petition of Matilda Gray on behalf of herself and her Child William in the said court
            exhibited against you.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof you are not to fail, as you will answer the contrary at your peril.</p> 
         
         
         <p>Witness, William Cranch esquire, chief judge of the said court, the twentieth day
            of October 18

         </p>
