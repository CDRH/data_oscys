         <p>Aris Buckner one of the Defts in the above cases, makes oath in open court that he has good reason to think &amp; believe, from credible &amp; certain information, as well as from circumstances in his own knowledge, and does verily think &amp; believe, that a fair &amp; impartial trial of the said suits, or of any of them, cannot be had in the County of Washington, where such suits are depending: Wherefore he prays that the same be removed into the Court holden in Alexandria County.</p>

         <p>Test W. Brentk Clk<br/>
24 Decr. 1833</p>
