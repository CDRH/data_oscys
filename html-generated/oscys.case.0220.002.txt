<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon John W. Williams, Mary S. Williams &amp; William L. Colquhoun if they shall be found in the County of Washington, that they be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, o the <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of Novem next, to answer <del class="to bill of complaint of">to bill of complaint of</del> the <del class="Bill">Bill</del> Petition for freedom of Wallace Blue filed in Court here against them <del class="and others.">and others.</del></p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p> 

<p>Issued the 3d day of June 1843.</p>

<p>W Brent Cl</p>

<p>Dermott</p>
