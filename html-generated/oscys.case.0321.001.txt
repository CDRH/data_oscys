      <div class="caption ">

         <p>To the Hon the Judges of The Circuit Court of Washington County District of Columbia</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>The petition of John Butler of said City and District, who sues by his mother and next friend Elizabeth Butler of said City &amp; District &amp; a free woman of colour respectfully represents that he has been free from the period of his birth. And that he is unjustly held in custody and claimed by one William D. Bell of Prince George's County state of <del>Virginia</del> Maryland, formerly of Washington City District of Columbia.</p>

         <p>And your petitioner would therefore pray that the said Bell and his agents may be prevented from removing him beyond the jurisdiction of your Hon" Court until the said matter is tried and determined by your Honours.</p>

         <p>And that the said question may be tried by your Honours as law and equity demand. And <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> your petitioner's freedom may be decreed by your Honours and such other and further relief granted to your petitioner as his case in <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> of all the circumstances may require. And as in duty bound he will ever pray.</p>

         <p>Edward C. Carrington<br/>
Atty for petitioner</p>
