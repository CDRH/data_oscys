<p>To the Hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Joe Crawford humbly represents that your petitioner is now illegally held in bondage by one Roberta Slye. That yr petitioner is entitled to his freedom and he prays your Honors to grant unto him the U S writ of subpoena &amp; such other relief as yr Honors may seem just &amp; proper.</p>

<p>R Wallach for petitioner</p>
