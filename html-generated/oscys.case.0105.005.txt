         <p>On the 19 day of July 1832 appeared Doctor B. King before me one of the Justices of
            the Peace in and for Washington County in the District afores and made oath on the
            holy Evangells of Almighty God that he served three days as a witness at the June
            Term of the Court for the County aforesaid as a witness in a case wherein Ann Williams
            &amp; children were plaintiffs against George Miller Defendant.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Will: Webb</p>
