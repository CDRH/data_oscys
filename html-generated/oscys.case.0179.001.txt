<p>The Petition of Moses Graham humbly complaining, sheweth unto your Honors that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom, and is unjustly held in bondage by Richard B Alexander, who claims your Petitioner as his slave. Wherefore your Petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena, to the said Richard B Alexander directed and such relief in the <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> according to law <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span></p>
