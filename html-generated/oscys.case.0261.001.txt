<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">
 
<p>The Petition of Negro Charles and Nan his wife and Eliza Juda &amp; Stephen her children by the said Charles their next friend all of Washington County aforesaid humbly sheweth that your said Petitioners Nan &amp; her said Children are unjustly &amp; illegally held in bondage by a certain Daniel Moxley Elizabeth Crow Edward Edelin &amp; Harriet Beans of said county who claim your said Petitioners as Slaves; that your Petitioners are free &amp; by law are entitled to their freedom &amp; to be discharged from the said defendants, which they are ready to make appear to your Honors. Wherefore they pray a subpoena to the said Defts. directed that they may appear &amp; answer here unto &amp; that such proceeding may take place as your Honors may direct in the premises agreeably to the laws in such cases provied &amp;c.</p>

<p>F S Key for Petrs.</p>

<p>16 Jany 1809</p>

<p>Appeared before me in open Court the above named Petr. Charles &amp; made oath that he had just ground to apprehend &amp; does truly believe that the Petrs. his wife &amp; children above named are about to be removed by the persons claiming them without the District of Columbia</p>

<p>Sworn to in open Court.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
