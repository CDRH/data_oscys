<p>To the Honble Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of Henrietta Henson, by her father &amp; next friend Andrew Henson Humbly Sheweth.</p>

<p>That she is held in bondage by Mary Ann Cox executrix of William Cox, deceased. And that she is justly entitled to her freedom.</p>

<p>Wherefore she prays that the United States Writ of Subpoena may be directed to issue commanding the said Mary Ann Cox Executrix &amp;c &amp;c</p>

<p>Carlisle for Petr</p>

<p>28 Aug 1839</p>
