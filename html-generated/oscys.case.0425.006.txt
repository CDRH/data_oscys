         <p>District of Columbia,
Alexandria County, to wit.</p>

         <p>The United States of America<br/>
To the Marshal of the E. District of <del>Columbia,</del> Virginia Greeting.</p>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon <span class="gap strikethrough">[strikethrough]</span> Daniel Kimler to appear before the Honorable the United States' Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Alexandria, at the court-house in the town of Alexandria, on <del>the</del> Tuesday <del>day of</del> the fourth <del>court, which commences on the</del> day of <del>Monday in</del> November next, to testify and the truth to say on behalf of John Armfield in a certain manner of controversy, still pending and undetermined, between Rachel Brent (a pauper) &amp; him and this he shall in no wise omit, under the penalty of $333 33: and have then there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon. William Cranch, Chief Judge of our said Court, this 28th day of October 1834.</p>

         <p>Edm: J. Lee C.C.</p>

         <p>I acknowledge service of the above subpoena this third day of November 1834<br/>
Danl Kimler</p>
