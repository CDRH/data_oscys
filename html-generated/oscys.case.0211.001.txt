         <p>To the Honble the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>The Petition of Thomas Over &amp; Francis Over humbly sheweth that your petitioners are free and were born of a free white woman, and are unjustly held in bondage by a certain Venerand Pulizzi of Washington County.</p>

         <p>Wherefore they pray that subpa may be issued to said Venerand Pulizzi &amp; that he may be compelled to appear and answer hereto. And that they may be discharged from their service and adjuged free according to Law</p>

         <p>F. S. Key for Petr</p>
