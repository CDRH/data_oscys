<p>To the Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for Washington County</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>Rachel Ann Williams of the District &amp; County aforesaid represents that she is held in slavery by Samuel Stettinius of said District &amp; County, and that she, <del>is entitled</del> the said Rachel, is entitled to her freedom, and humbly prays your honors to inquire into, &amp; do, or cause justice to be done in the premises; and your petitioner will ever pray &amp;c.</p> 

<p>M. Thompson<br/>
Atty for Petitioner</p>

<p>Washington Apl. 1st 1853</p>
