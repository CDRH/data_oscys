         <p>District of Columbia to wit</p>
         
         
         <p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting</p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Alexander Spalding late of the County of Washington,
            if he shall be found in the said County that all excuses set aside, he be and appear
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held for the County aforesaid
            at the City of Washington on the first Monday of June next to answer the petition
            of Sarah Green for her freedom, exhibited in the said Court against him.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril &amp; have you then and there this writ</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness the hon Wm Cranch Chief Judge of the said Court, the 30th Day of Jany 1813.</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>F. S. Key</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 19th day of Febry 1813.</p>
