      <div class="body">

         <p>1813. October 12th.</p>

         <p>Fielder Neale Exor of Sarah Neal Dr.</p>

         <table>
            <tr>
               <td>To balance due on your final account passed this day</td>
               <td>$4462.01</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td>Distribution of the above balance is as follows towit.</td>
               <td/>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td>To the deceased's two daughters Eleanor and Susanna one half of the above balance of $4462.01 is</td>
               <td>$2231.0½</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td>To her son Theodore Neale a negro boy named Bob, apraised to</td>
               <td>275.00</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td>To her son Fielder Neale a negro boy named Charles appraised to</td>
               <td>
                  <u>275.00</u>
               </td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td/>
               <td>$2781.00½</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
               <td>The residue to be equally divided among the deceased's five sons &amp; two daughters, namely Samuel Neale, Richard Neale, Zachariah Neale, William Neale and John Neale, Susanna Neale &amp; Eleanor Neale and is to each the sum of</td>
               <td>$240.14<sup>2</sup>⁄<sub>7</sub>
               </td>
            </tr>
         </table>

         <p>Test. Trueman Tyler Regt.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="caption">

         <p>Maryland, Prince George's County Sct</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>In testimony that the above Distribution is a true copy from the Original Recorded in my office I have hereto set my hand and affixed the Public Seal of my office this tenth day of November in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and fifteen.</p>

         <p>Test. Trueman Tyler Regt</p>
