<p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of James Hutton humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by William Belt who holds &amp; claims your Petitioner, &amp; (as he is informed &amp; believes) means to sell him as a Slave.</p>

<p>Wherefore he prays your Honors that a subpa may be issued to the said William Belt directed, that he may appear &amp; answer hereto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged from his service &amp; obtain such relief in the premisses as to your Honors shall seem right.</p>

<p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr</p>
