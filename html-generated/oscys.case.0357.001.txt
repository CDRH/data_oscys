         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia <del>of</del> for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of  Negro Daniel <span class="unclear torn page">humbly sheweth[?]</span>
         </p>

         <p>That your Petitioner is entitled by law to his freedom &amp; is unjustly held in bondage by a certain         Ballard who hath lately purchased him from a certain John C. Kinchelo of Virginia. Wherefore your Petitioner prays to be released from the custody &amp; possession of said Ballard, &amp; sbpa may be issued, directed to him commanding him to appear &amp; answer to this petition &amp; that your Petitioner may have such relief in the premisses as to your Honors may seem just.</p>
 
         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petr:</p>

         <p>Jany 12, 1822.</p>
               
      </div>
