         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">
     
         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Georgiana Monroe <del>late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, and him safely keep, so that you have his bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 3rd Monday of Octo. next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for freedom of Sarah H. Medulla</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness, James Dunlop Esquire, Chief Justice of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 10 day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and 58</p>

         <p>Issued the 11th day of June 1858.</p>

         <p>Jno A Smith Clk</p>

         <p>C &amp; M</p>
