         <p>To the honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for
            the County of Washington.
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>Valentine Jones a freeman (of Colour) represents that he is unlawfully kept in slavery
            by Nathaniel Seavers and George Hagly of the said County, and that he is entitled
            to his freedom; and he prays your honours that the said Nathanial Seavers and George
            Hagly may be summoned according to Law to appear before your honours, that he may
            be decreed free, and the said Nathaniel Seavers and George Hagly may be decreed to
            pay all costs of this suit and as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioner</p>
         
         
      </div>
