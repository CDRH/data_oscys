         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Priscilla Gant &amp; Basil E. Gant if they shall be
            found in the County of Washington, that they be and appear in the Circuit Court of
            the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County of Washington, at the
            City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> 4th Monday of March next, to answer 
            <del class="to bill of complaint of">to bill of complaint of</del> the Petition for freedom of Hester Johnson Martha Bronson &amp; Randolph Bronson filed
            in Court here against them 
            <del class="and others.">and others.</del>
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>witness the Hon. w. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 21st day of Nov 1846.</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Clk</p>
         
         
         <p>Ratcliffe</p>
