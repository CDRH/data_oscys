<p>We command you that you <del>take</del> sum. James Bowling &amp; Clarissa Gardner, admins of Richard B Gardener <del>in a plea of her</del> <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington in your said district, and safely keep, so that you have</del> to be &amp;<del>bod</del> appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for freedom of Margaret &amp; Alfred Atkins &amp;c.</p>

<p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>
