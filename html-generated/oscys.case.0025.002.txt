<p>The United States of America to the Marshal of the District of Columbia — greeting</p>
                
</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded <del>that we</del> to Summon Francis
                    Neale late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the
                    County of Washington, that all excuses &amp; delays set aside he be and appear before this Court immediately, to answer the Petition for freedom of a certain Priscilla Queen filed in the Court against him.
                    Hereof fail not at your peril— and have you then and there this Writ. Witness
                    the hon Wm. Cranch Chief Judge
                    of our said Court the 8th day of Jany 1810.</p>
                
<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
                
<p>Issued the 8th day of Jany 1810</p>
               
<p>F.S. Key</p>
