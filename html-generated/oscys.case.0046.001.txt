         <p>To the Hon the Judges of the Circuit Court of the 
            United States for the District of Columbia 
            &amp; County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Robert Henley respectfully sheweth that your 
            petitioner is held in bondage &amp; claimed by Samuel Miller of 
            said county as a slave, although your petitioner is justly entitled to his freedom.
            
            Your petitioner therefore prays that your Honors will take his case into 
            consideration &amp; that a subpoena may issue commanding the said Samuel Miller 
            to appear &amp;c &amp;c &amp; that such further and other process &amp; relief as to your 
            Honors may deem meet &amp; his case requires, may be granted to your
            petitioner &amp; he as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>R Wallach for petitioner</p>
