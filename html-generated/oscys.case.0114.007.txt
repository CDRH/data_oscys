         <p>We command you that you 
            <del class="take">take</del> summon Joseph W. Neale and Oliver Simpson late of Washington County, if they shall
            be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, 
            <del class="and safely keep,">and safely keep,</del> so that 
            <del class="you">you</del> they be and appear
