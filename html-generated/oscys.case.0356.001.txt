      <div class="caption">
                
         <p>The Petition of Negro Arnold <del>humble</del> to The Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>humbly Sheweth</p>

         <p>That your Petitioner is a free man &amp; is unjustly held in bondage as a slave by a certain George N. Thomas of Washington County aforesaid from which said bondage your Petitioner prays to be released &amp; that his case may be proceeded with according to Law, &amp; that your Honors will grant him the United States writ of Subpoena to the said Thomas directed to appear &amp; recognize according to Law &amp; all such other &amp; further relief in the premisses as to your Honors may seem just. &amp;c &amp;c</p>
 
         <p>F S Key for Petr</p>
