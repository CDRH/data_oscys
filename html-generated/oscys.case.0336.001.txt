   <div class="case">

      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington now sitting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Negro Fanny for herself &amp; also for &amp; in behalf of her children Mary Becky &amp; James, Infants respectfully sheweth:</p>

         <p>That your Petitioner &amp; her said children are entitled to their freedom &amp; are unjustly held in bondage by a certain Thomas Quandell who claims &amp; holds the said petitioners as his slaves.</p>

         <p>Your Petitioners further state that the said Quandell does not reside in this district &amp; is about now to have the same, &amp; to remove your Petitioners beyond the Jurisdiction of this Court.</p>

         <p>Wherefore your Honors are prayed to grant your Petitioners a subpoena to the said Quandell directed that he may be compelled to appear before your Honors &amp; that just relief may be granted to your said Petitioners in the premisses as to your Honors may seem proper.</p>

         <p>FS Key for Petrs:</p>

      </div>
