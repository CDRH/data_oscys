         <p>To the Honorable the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, sitting or the County
            of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Barney Montgomery humbly sheweth that he is entitled to his freedom.
            And that he is illegally detained and held in bondage as a slave by John Robert Bronaugh
            who claims him as such.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Wherefore he prays that subpa may be issued to said Bronaugh and that he may be compelled
            to appear and answer hereto, and that he may be discharged from his service and adjudged
            free according to law.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>J. M. Carlisle for petnr</p>
