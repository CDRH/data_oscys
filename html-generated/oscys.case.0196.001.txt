<p>To the hon. the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington.</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Robert Trunnel, a free person of colour, shews to your honors that he is now imprisoned in the jail of this county, as the slave of Hope H Slatter Your petitioner avers that he is free and not the slave of the said Hope H Slatter nor of any one else.</p>

<p>Your petitioner therefor <span class="unclear illegible">[prefers?]</span> to your honors this his petition of freedom against the claim of the said         and prays that he may be ordered to be <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span>, and your Petitioner as in duty bound will ever pray &amp;c.</p>

<p>Jas. Hoban for Petitioner</p>

<p>Jany 3. 1842</p>

</div>
