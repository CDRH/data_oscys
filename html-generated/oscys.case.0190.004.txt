      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Nelson Conrad late of Washington County, if he shall be found within the County of Washington in your said District, <del>and safely keep,</del> so that <del>you</del> he be &amp; appear <del>have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition of Olivia Lee &amp; Betty Lee &amp; others against him for their freedoms</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness William Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 4th day of June Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty two</p>

         <p>Issued the 6th day of August 1842.</p>
