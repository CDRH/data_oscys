         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To <del>The Marshal of the District of Columbia</del>—Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> summon Alexr. Hunter Marshal of the District of Columbia <del>late of Washington County, if shall be found within the County of Washington, in your said District, and safely keep, so</del> that he &amp; appear <del>you have bod</del> before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to answer <del>unto</del> Nancy Jones's petition for freedom filed in the said Court against him.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 6th day of June Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and 35</p>

         <p>Issued the 22d day of August 1835</p>

         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

         <p>Dermott</p>
