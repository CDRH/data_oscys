         <p>To the Honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for
            the County of Washington The Petition of James Burk a man of colour, humbly sheweth
            that your Petitioner is justly &amp; lawfully entitled to freedom, and is nevertheless
            unjustly held in bondage as a slave by one Coleman Lewis: and your Petitioner Therefore
            prays that your Honors will order the United States Writ of Subpoena to be issued,
            to the said Coleman Lewis directed, commanding him to appear at the next session of
            the said Court and answer to your Petitioner in the premises. &amp; that your honors will
            grant him such other relief in the premise as the nature of his case may require &amp;
            your honors may deem just &amp; right And yr. Petr. &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>F. S. Key for Petr.</p>
