         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the United States for the 
            District of Columbia &amp; county of Washington
         </p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Robert Loyal a colored man sheweth unto your honors that he is 
            a freeman and that he is illegally held in bondage by one Benjamin Lewis who 
            claims your petitioner as a slave for life. And your petitioner is credably informed
            &amp; veryly 
            believes that the said Benjamin Lewis is now about to sell him as a slave 
            for life to some distant state. Your petitioner therefore prays your honors to grant
            him the benefit 
            of writ of subpoena &amp; such other relief as shall apper to your honors to be 
            meet &amp; proper &amp; he will ever pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Robert Beale for petitioner</p>
