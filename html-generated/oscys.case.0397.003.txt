         <p>Geo. Town March 5th 1824</p>

         <p>Mr. Edmond J. Lee</p>

         <p>Sir,</p>

         <p>The enclosed petitions I wish docketed in your Court &amp; subpoena's issued. I have never qualified in your County though I have been practicing for several years in this. The mother of those petitioners is anxious &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> about them for fear of them being sold out of the District. She has herself petitioned in this County for herself &amp; three of her children, who were here, &amp; <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> in the Court below, &amp; the Deft. has appealed to the Supreme Court, where it is now pending. The claim of the petitioners to the within petitions will abide the decision in my case but <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> to petition to prevent them being sold out of the District so as to prevent them making claim &amp;c.</p>

         <p>If the cases cannot be docketted in consequence of my not having been admitted in your court I
