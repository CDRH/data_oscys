      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honble the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Negro David humbly sheweth that your Petitioner is entitled to his freedom &amp; is unlawfully held in bondage and claimed as a slave by a certain James Hutchison. Wherefore he prays your Honors that the United States writ of subpoena may be issued, commanding the said Defendant Hutchison to appear and answer hereunto, &amp; that your Petitioner may be discharged &amp;ca according to law.</p>

         <p>Key &amp; Dunlop<br/>
for Petr:</p>
