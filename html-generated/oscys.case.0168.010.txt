         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Frances Bowling &amp; Clarissa Gardnier adms of Richard
            B. Gardiner if shall be found in the County of Washington, that they be and appear
            in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, at rules to be held for the County
            of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the 
            <del class="first">first</del> third Monday of October next, to answer to 
            <del class="bill of complaint">bill of complaint</del> the Petition of Margaret &amp; Noah Atkin by David Atkin their next friend for their
