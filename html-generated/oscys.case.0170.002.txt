         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Benjamin Buck if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City of Washington, on Fourth <span class="gap torn page">[torn page]</span> to <del>testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition for Freedom of Maria Kennedy filed against him andsoforth.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness W. Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 12th day of December 1834</p>

         <p>W Brent Clk</p>

         <p>(Bradley)</p>
