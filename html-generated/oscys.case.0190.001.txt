         <p>To the Hon. Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, for the County of Washington</p>

      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petition of Olivia Lee and her infant John Lee and of Betsy Lee for herself and
            for her nine children, Milly Lee, Charles Lee, William Lee, George Lee and Caroline
            Lee, persons of color of the County of Washington in the District of Columbia respectfully
            represents that they are all detained as slaves by a certain Nelson Conrad and 
            <del class="his part">his part</del> Benjamin Homans and that they are free and entitled to be discharged from their illegal
            detention, they therefore pray your Honors, 
            <del class="to grant">to grant</del> that the said Nelson Conrad and Benjamin Homans may be cited to appear as the Law
            directs and that your Petitioners may be declared as free and that their case may
            be examined into by your Honorable Court and that said Defendants may pay all costs
            &amp;c and as in duty bound they will ever pray &amp;c.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Brent &amp; Brent for Petitioners</p>
