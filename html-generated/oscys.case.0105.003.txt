         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon George Miller Sr &amp; George Miller Jr if they shall
            be found in the County of Washington, that (all excuses set aside) they be and appear
            before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of
            Washington, in the City of Washington, on the first Monday 
            <del class="day">day</del> of May next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of Ann Williams &amp; Children exhibited aft them
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 17 day of Feby 1832</p>
         
         
         <p>W Brent Ck</p>
