         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> sum. Isaac Buckingham <del>late of Washington County, if him shall be found within the county of Washington in your said District, and safely keep, so that</del> you <del>have</del> to be &amp; <del>bod</del> appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County aforesaid, at the City of Washington, on the 4th Monday of March next, to answer <del>unto</del> the Petition for freedom of Thomas Taylor filed in the said Court &amp;c.</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness W Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of our said Court, at the City of Washington, the 8th day of January Anno Domini one thousand eight hundred and forty 9.</p>

         <p>Issued the 17th day of January 1849.</p>

         <p>Jno. A. Smith clk</p>

         <p>S. &amp; S.</p>
