<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the
District of Columbia for the County of
Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The Petition of negro Abraham Smith humbly sheweth to your Honors, that he was
born and raised in the State of Virginia, that he was purchased there,
<del class="after the">after the</del> by John Lyons, after
the removal of the said Lyons to the
District of Columbia, and was imported into this
District contrary to Law.</p>

<p>Your Petitioner further sheweth, that he is entitled to his freedom, but is
nevertheless held in bondage, and a slave for life by the said John Lyons.;</p>

<p>Your Petitioner, therefore prays that the United States unit  of subpoena may
issue, directed to the said John Lyons, commanding him to appear and
answer to your Petitioner in the premises, that the claim of your Petitioner
may be tried before your honors, and that he may be discharged from
servitude to the said John Lyons, &amp; that he may have such other &amp; further relief as to your honors may seem right.</p> 

<p>Key &amp; Dunlop for Petitioner</p>

</div>
