      <div class="body">

         <p>In the Tryal of this Cause the Council for the petitioner offered in Evidence the declarations of a Certain Mrs Smith to prove the petitioners were descended from a free White Woman, and the Council for the defendant offered to impeach the credibility of said Mrs Smith, by <del>declarations</del> proving <span class="gap strikethrough">[strikethrough]</span> 
            <del>a Witness to prove</del>to the Jury that the said Mrs Smith was a woman who by General Reputation of the Neighbourhood Aforesaid and kept company with Negroes. To which evidence <del>the court were of opinion was improper to go to Jury</del> the council for the Petitioner objected <del>as being improper to go to the Jury</del> and the court <del>was</del> being of opinion that the same was improper, <del>and</del> refused to allow the same to be offered to the Jury to which opinion of the Court the Council for the defendant prayd leave to <del>Axcept</del> except and that they would sign &amp; seal this his bill of exception according to the statute which is done accordingly</p>

         <p>Samuel Chase (seal)<br/>
Jeremiah Townley Chase (seal)</p>
