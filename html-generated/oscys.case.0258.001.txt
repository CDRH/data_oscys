      <div class="caption ">

         <p>District of Columbia, Set. The United States of America    To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, greeting</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">
     
         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Alexander Scott, if he shall be found in the County of Washington, that all excuses and delays set aside, he be and appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia to be held for the County of Washington at the City of Washington on the fourth Monday of December next to answer the Petition for freedom of Walter Thomas and Dennis Thomas in this Court filed against him. Hereof fail not at your peril and have you then and there this writ. Witness the hon. Wm Cranch Esq chief Judge of our said Court the 8th day of Nov Anno Domini 1809.</p>
    
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
    
         <p>Issued the 7th Dec 1809.</p>
    
         <p>Balch</p>
