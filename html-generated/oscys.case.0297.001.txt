<div class="caption ">

<p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>The petition of Theresa Blackwell a coloured woman, humbly sheweth unto your Honors, that she is entitled to her freedom and is unjustly detained and held in bondage by one Robert Hall of the state of Virginia who claims your petitioner his Slave, wherefore your petitioner prays your Honors to grant a subpoena to be directed to the said Robert Hall and such other relief in the premises as may be according to Law. and your petitioner will as is duty bound ever pray &amp;c</p>

<p>Theresa Blackwell<br></br>
by D Ratcliffe &amp; Key. her attys</p>

</div>
