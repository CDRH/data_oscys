         <p>In the several cases of petitions for freedom by Wallace Blue, other Blues, &amp; Laura, against Williams &amp; Colquhoun pending before this Court, I was retained to succeed Mr Dermott, the original council; &amp; having been satisfied by the trial of the first case in the series that I could not succeed for them upon the law as laid down in that case, I desired to withdraw my name; but the court declined to allow me to do so, I have only to say that the Court may dispose of all the said cases as they <span class="gap illegible">[illegible]</span> fit. April 16, 1845</p>

         <p>Daniel Ratcliffe</p>
