         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>

The United States of America,<br/>

To the Marshal of the District of Columbia--Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>We command you that you <del>take</del> Summon Thomas Grimes if he shall be found within the county of Washington, in your said district, <del>and safely keep so that you have</del> all excuses &amp; delays set aside he <del>had</del> be &amp; appear before the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the county aforesaid, at the city of Washington, on the first Monday of December next, to answer <del>unto</del> the petition of freedom of <del>Thomas Grimes</del> Adam Wigle in said Court exhibited against him</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not, at your peril, and have you then and there this writ.</p>

         <p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, chief judge of our said Court at the city of Washington, the 12th day of May Anno Domini, one thousand eight hundred and Thirty</p>

         <p>Issued the 13 day of May 1831</p>

         <p>W. Brent Ck</p>
