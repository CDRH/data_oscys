         <p>To the honourable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the county of Washington.</p>

      <div class="body ">
    
         <p>The Petition of Michael Shinor shews to your honors that he is unjustly and illegally held held in bondage by Anne Howard and William E. Howard, executors of Thomas Howard deceased, and prays your honors to release him therefrom and as in duty bound he will ever pray &amp;c.</p>

         <p>James Hoban for Petitioner</p>

         <p>March 26, 1836</p>
