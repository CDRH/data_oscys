         <p>The Petition of Letha Dukett, for herself, and of Mary Duckett, Thomas Duckett, Barbara
            Duckett, Julia Duckett and Lewis Duckett, infants by the said Letha, their mother
            and next friend, respectfully sets forth that they are unlawfully held in bondage
            by one John McCutchen, and one John H. G. McCutchen, and they pray 
            <del class="to be">to be</del> that the facts may be inquired into, and they be released from servitude. And they
            pray such security against intolerance or a forcible abuction, prior to a trial of
            their claim to freedom as may consist with equity, and the just rights of your petitioners.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>D.A. Hall for petitioners</p>
