         <h4>To the Honorable Judges of the Circuit Court for Washington County, 
            <del class="Di">Di</del> the District of Columbia
         </h4>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The petition of Gill Litchworth, a man of color, humbly sheweth, that he is entitled
            to his freedom, but is now held in slavery by a certain Zadock Wilson laying an insufficient
            claim to your petitioner. Wherefore you petitioner prays the judgment of your Honors
            that he be released from further bondage. And your petitioner, as in duty bound, will
            ever pray and so forth.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Clement Cox for Petitioner</p>
