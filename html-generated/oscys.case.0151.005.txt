         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon William Acken if he shall be found in the County
            of Washington, that all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit Court
            of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City
            of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to 
            <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del>  answer the Petition John Lee and Anna Maria Lee by Hickey Lee their mother for their
            freedom.
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Hereof, fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>
         
         
         <p>Witness, Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>
         
         
         <p>Issued the 3d day of Augut 1841</p>
         
         
         <p>Wm Brent Clk</p>
