         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the
            County of Washington
         </p>
         
         
      </div>
      
      
      <div class="body ">
         
         
         <p>The Petn of Michael Oakley and Anthony Oakley Humbly sheweth</p> 
         
         
         <p>That your Petitioner is entitled to freedom by birth right, but is detained in Slavery
            by a certain Notley Young who resides within the Jurisdiction of this Honorable Court;
            he therefore prays that process may issue so that he may assert his right. And your
            Petr will ever Pray &amp;c
         </p>
         
         
         <p>Michael Oakley
            <br></br>
            12 Augst 1809
         </p>
         
         
         <p>I undertake to appear as Attorney to prosecute this Claim.
            <br></br>
            Henry Hiort
         </p>
