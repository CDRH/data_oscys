<p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br></br>
The United States of America,<br></br>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

</div>

<div class="body ">

<p>You are hereby commanded to summon Elizabeth Whiting if she shall be found in the County of Washington that (all excuses set aside) he be and appear before the Circuit court of the District of Columbia, to be held for the County of Washington, in the City of Washington, on the fourth Monday of November next, to <del class="testify on the part of">testify on the part of</del> answer the Petition of John Peterson filed against her for her freedom.</p>

<p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

<p>Witness Wm Cranch Esquire, Chief Judge of the said Court. </p>

<p>Issued the 22d day of May 1835.</p>

<p>Wm Brent Clk</p>

<p>Key.</p>
