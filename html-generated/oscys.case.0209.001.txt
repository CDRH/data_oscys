      <div class="caption">

         <p>To the Honorable the Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of Washington</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Susan Wilson by John Moore, her next friend, humbly sheweth, that your petitioner is now held in bondage and claimed as a slave by William Addison. That your petitioner is entitled to her freedom and she therefore prays your Honors to grant unto her the United States writ of subpoena &amp;c and such other relief in the premises as to your Honors may seen just and proper %c</p>

         <p>Richard Wallach for petitioner</p>

         <p>Jos H Bradley</p>
