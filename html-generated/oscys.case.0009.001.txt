         <p>To the Honorable The Judges of the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia for the County of County of Washington.</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body">

         <p>The Petition of Sarah Oakley in behalf of herself and her four Children John, Mary Ann, Eliza and Thomas</p>
                    
         <p>Humbly sheweth</p> 
                
         <p>That your Petitioners are detained in Slavery, by Mary Ann Fenwick, and pray process may issue, so that they may assert their right to Freedom.</p>
                
         <p>And Your Petitioners as in Duty bound will every pray</p>
                
         <p>Hiort for Complts.</p>
