         <p>District of Columbia, to wit:<br/>
The United States of America,<br/>
To the Marshal of the District of Columbia, Greeting:</p>

      </div>

      <div class="body ">

         <p>You are hereby commanded to summon Nathaniel Seavers &amp; George Hagley if they shall be found in the County of Washington, that they be and appear in the Circuit Court of the District of Columbia, <del>at rules to be</del> held for the County of Washington, at the City of Washington, on the <del>first</del> fourth Monday of March next, to answer the <del>bill of complaint of</del> petition of freedom of Valentine Jones filed in Court here against <del>and others.</del> them</p>

         <p>Hereof fail not at your peril; and have you then and there this precept.</p>

         <p>Witness the Hon. W. Cranch, Chief Judge of the said Court.</p>

         <p>Issued the 19th day of January 1843.</p>

         <p>W. Brent Ck</p>

         <p>
            <del>Memorandum: The defendant to enter appearance in the suit, on or before the ady on which the above subpoena is returnable, otherwise the bill may be taken pro confesso.</del>
         </p>
